## S-Tier Sigils: The Meta-Defining Core

S-Tier sigils are non-negotiable additions to any endgame build. They represent the absolute peak of power progression in Endless Ragnarok.

### Warpath+ Sigils
Introduced in the Endless Ragnarok expansion, Warpath+ sigils are character-specific drops found primarily within the deepest depths of the Conflux solo roguelite mode. These sigils modify a character's core mechanic. For example, Beatrix's Warpath+ sigil extends the active window of her Delta Clock, allowing her to maintain her high-damage state for longer periods. For Sandalphon, his Warpath+ sigil amplifies his lightstep sword wave spam while in Supreme Primarch Mode, pushing his DPS to SS-tier levels. You can read more about his optimal setups in our [Sandalphon Character Guide](/en/characters/sandalphon-guide/).

### War Elemental+
War Elemental+ remains the single most important sigil in the game. It allows all attacks to deal superior elemental damage, regardless of the target's actual element. In Chaos difficulty, where bosses like Ragnalia frequently shift elements, War Elemental+ ensures your damage output remains constant.

### Character-Specific Sigils (Double Traits)
With the introduction of Weapon Transcendence, players can now roll Character-Specific traits onto their weapons, but keeping a dedicated Character-Specific+ sigil in your loadout is still highly recommended to free up valuable weapon slots. These sigils combine both unique character traits into a single slot, drastically increasing build flexibility.

## A-Tier Sigils: High-Performance DPS and Utility

A-Tier sigils form the backbone of your build. While they do not alter character mechanics as drastically as S-tier sigils, they provide the necessary stat thresholds required to clear Fatebreaker quests within the strict time limits.

### The Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Series (V+)
These sigils, originally dropped from the Lucilius boss fight, have been expanded in Endless Ragnarok. They allow players to customize their damage cap bonuses based on their playstyle:
*   **Alpha V+**: Increases normal attack damage cap and generates SBA gauge upon landing normal attacks.
*   **Beta V+**: Focuses on skill damage cap increases and reduces skill cooldowns when skills are used.
*   **Gamma V+**: Increases SBA damage cap and provides a temporary protective shield when using SBA.

For characters like Eustace, who relies on heavy ranged skill spam, a combination of Beta V+ and Gamma V+ is optimal. For melee-focused characters like Gallanza, Alpha V+ is the preferred choice.

### Critical Hit Rate V+
Reaching a 100% Critical Hit Rate is mandatory for consistent damage output. Critical Hit Rate V+ sigils are highly sought after, especially when rolled with desirable secondary traits like Damage Cap or Cooldown Reduction.

## B-Tier and C-Tier Sigils: Situational and Niche Choices

While DPS is king, survival is paramount in Chaos difficulty, where even standard mobs can defeat a character in a single strike. B-Tier sigils focus on utility and defensive recovery, while C-Tier sigils are generally avoided in endgame builds.

### B-Tier: The Survival Core
*   **Potion Hoarder V+**: This is the **Endless Ragnarok best support Sigil** for solo progression. In Chaos difficulty, healing options are limited. Potion Hoarder increases the number of Green, Blue, and Mega potions you can carry, ensuring you can revive allies and heal yourself throughout long fights.
*   **Aegis V+**: Increases maximum HP. This is crucial for avoiding one-shot mechanics from bosses like The World.
*   **Improved Dodge V+**: Increases the invincibility window and the number of consecutive dodges you can perform. This is highly effective against Ragnalia's arena-wide hazards.

### C-Tier: Obsolete and Flat Stat Sigils
*   **Attack V / Defense V**: Flat stat boosts quickly hit the game's hard caps, rendering these sigils useless in the endgame.
*   **Specific Elemental Resistances**: These are entirely bypassed by proper positioning and defensive play. They waste valuable sigil slots that could otherwise be used for damage cap or utility traits.

## Optimizing Sigil Slots in the Endgame Loop

To maximize your character's potential, you must integrate your sigil choices with the Weapon Transcendence system. According to the [Cygames Official Site](https://relink.granbluefantasy.jp/en/), the expansion allows players to transfer select sigil traits directly into weapon slots, freeing up space on your main grid.

### Step-by-Step Build Optimization

1.  **Cap Your Critical Hit Rate**: Ensure your character reaches 100% Critical Hit Rate using a combination of weapon masteries, Wrightstones, and a single Critical Hit Rate V+ sigil.
2.  **Equip War Elemental+ and Character-Specific Sigils**: These should occupy your primary S-tier slots. If you have unlocked Weapon Transcendence Slot II (max level 25) or Slot IV (max level 10), consider rolling your character-specific traits onto the weapon to free up a sigil slot. Learn more about this process in our [Weapon Transcendence Guide](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/).
3.  **Distribute Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Sigils**: Depending on your character's kit, slot in the appropriate Lucilius sigils to raise your damage cap. For example, Io benefits greatly from Beta and Gamma, while Lancelot requires Alpha.
4.  **Fill Remaining Slots with B-Tier Utility**: Add Potion Hoarder V+ and Aegis V+ to ensure you can survive the high-damage output of Chaos bosses.
5.  **Run the Conflux for Sigil Upgrades**: Use the gateway in Seedhollow or Tredame Sanctum to farm the necessary enhancement materials. For strategies on clearing these gateways, refer to our [Fatebreaker Quest Guide](/en/endgame/fatebreaker-quests-guide/).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is the best place to farm S-tier Sigils in Endless Ragnarok?
S-tier Sigils, particularly the new Warpath+ and Character-Specific+ sigils, are best farmed in the Conflux roguelite solo mode. Pushing to deeper depths increases the drop rate of high-rarity sigils. Additionally, completing Fatebreaker quests on Chaos difficulty rewards players with materials that can be traded at the Knickknack Shack for specific S-tier sigil transmutations.

### Do Character-Specific Sigils stack with Warpath+ Sigils?
Yes. Character-Specific Sigils (which boost core skills and stats) stack with the new Warpath+ Sigils introduced in the Endless Ragnarok expansion. They modify different aspects of a character's kit. For example, Beatrix can run both her character-specific sigil for increased damage during low health and her Warpath+ sigil to extend her Delta Clock duration simultaneously.

### How does Weapon Transcendence affect my sigil loadout?
Weapon Transcendence allows you to add extra sigil traits (up to Slot II max level 25 and Slot IV max level 10) directly onto your fully enhanced weapons. This means you can transfer essential utility traits like Damage Cap or Critical Hit Rate directly to your weapon, freeing up physical sigil slots on your grid for more specialized S-tier or A-tier sigils.

### Is War Elemental+ still mandatory for all characters in Chaos difficulty?
Yes. War Elemental+ remains mandatory because it converts all outgoing damage to the elemental weakness of the target. Since Chaos-difficulty bosses have massive health pools and frequently shift elemental resistances, not running War Elemental+ results in a significant drop in overall DPS, making it nearly impossible to beat the tight quest timers.
