## The Conflux: Solo Roguelite Farming Strategy

The Conflux is a solo roguelite mode accessed via gateways in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. It serves as one of the primary answers to **Endless Ragnarok where to find Sigils** of the highest rarity. A single run takes approximately 20 minutes and consists of randomly generated combat, treasure, and rare monster rooms.

To secure S-Tier drops, you must target Depth 41 through 50. Before entering a new depth, you are presented with three random power-up choices. For optimal farming speed, prioritize damage-oriented power-ups like *Critical Damage Boost*, *Cooldown Reduction*, and *SBA Charge Rate*. If you encounter a *Rare Monster Room* gateway, select it immediately. Rare monsters within The Conflux have a significantly elevated **Endless Ragnarok Sigil drop rate** for character-specific Warpath+ Sigils.

### Step-by-Step Conflux Farming Loop:
1. **Preparation**: Select a high-mobility, self-sustaining character. Sandalphon (using Supreme Primarch Mode) or Vaseraga are highly recommended due to their high survivability.
2. **Gateway Entry**: Choose the Depth 41 starting point (requires clearing previous depths at least once).
3. **Room Selection Priority**: Rare Monster Room > Treasure Room > Combat Room (Horde) > Combat Room (Single Target).
4. **Power-up Synergy**: Stack *Supplementary Damage* and *Drain* buffs to maintain high DPS without relying on healing items.
5. **Exit Management**: If your HP drops low and you run out of revives, use a green gateway to exit early. Exiting safely preserves all Sigils acquired during the run, whereas failing the run forfeits 50% of your gathered loot.

| Conflux Depth Range | Common Sigil Quality | Rare Monster Spawn Rate | Guaranteed Sigil Rewards | Estimated Drop Rate of V+ Sigils |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Depths 1 - 20** | Tier III to IV | 10% | 1x Sigil IV+ | ~5% |
| **Depths 21 - 35** | Tier IV to V | 20% | 2x Sigil V | ~15% |
| **Depths 36 - 40** | Tier V to V+ | 35% | 2x Sigil V+, 1x Curio | ~30% |
| **Depths 41 - 50** | Tier V+ to S-Tier | 50% | 3x Sigil V+, 2x Curio (Level 5) | ~45% (S-Tier: ~5%) |

## Chaos Difficulty Boss Farming Routes

If you want to know **Endless Ragnarok how to get best Sigils** with specific sub-stats, target farming Chaos-difficulty bosses is the most reliable method. Unlike the randomized loot pool of The Conflux, specific bosses drop dedicated Sigil types. 

Chaos difficulty is the highest quest tier in Endless Ragnarok, unlocked only after completing the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" and finishing the Epilogue. In this difficulty, bosses can easily defeat under-geared characters in a single strike. Therefore, bringing defensive utility or characters with strong invincibility windows is mandatory.

### Targeted Boss Routes:
* **Beelzebub (Chaos)**: The major antagonist of the expansion. Defeating him yields materials for Weapon Transcendence and has a chance to drop the *Warpath+* Sigil for whichever character you are currently controlling. Focus on breaking his wings during the overdrive phase to increase your drop chances.
* **The World (Chaos)**: This Arcarum creation drops *Gamma V+* Sigils. The fight requires high elemental coverage. Use characters like Cagliostro or the Captain to apply defense down debuffs and exploit elemental weaknesses.
* **Seofon & Tweyen Dual Showdown**: This quest pits you against both Eternals simultaneously. It is the fastest source of *Alpha V+* and *Beta V+* Sigils. Focus on defeating Tweyen first to eliminate ranged crowd control, then burn down Seofon.

| Boss Name | Recommended Party Setup | Key Breakable Parts | Target Sigil Drop | Base Drop Rate (Chaos) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Beelzebub** | Sandalphon, Beatrix, Maglielle, Siegfried | Wings, Crown | Warpath+ (Active Character) | 3.0% (Part Break) / 5.0% (Clear) |
| **The World** | Io, Cagliostro, Percival, Vane | Core, Left Arm | Gamma V+ | 8.5% (Clear) |
| **Ragnalia** | Eustace, Charlotta, Lancelot, Yodarha | Tail, Horns | Beta V+ | 10.0% (Clear) |
| **Seofon & Tweyen** | Narmaya, Vaseraga, Ferry, Captain | None (Dual Fight) | Alpha V+ / Beta V+ | 12.0% (Clear Chest) |

## Knickknack Shack Transmutation & Transmarvel Strategy

When you are not actively fighting bosses, Siero's Knickknack Shack in Seedhollow is the absolute fastest way to turn excess materials into top-tier Sigils. The expansion updates the Transmutation system, adding a Level 4 Transmutation tier and expanding the Transmarvel pool.

To execute the **Endless Ragnarok Sigil farming fastest** loop, you must farm high-yield material quests to exchange for Knickknack Vouchers. The best quest for voucher materials is the Chaos-difficulty quest *“Heralds of Doom”* featuring Ragnalia, which drops high-value monster parts that trade for 15 to 25 vouchers each.

### The Transmarvel Loop:
1. **Gather Materials**: Run *“Heralds of Doom”* or *“The World’s End”* until your inventory is full of high-rarity materials.
2. **Trade Vouchers**: Exchange these materials at Siero's shop until you reach the maximum cap of 999 Knickknack Vouchers.
3. **Level 3 Transmutation**: Spend your vouchers on Level 3 Transmutations. Each pull fills your Transmarvel gauge.
4. **Transmarvel Roll**: Once the gauge is full, perform a Transmarvel roll. In Endless Ragnarok, Transmarvel rolls have a guaranteed chance of yielding a V+ Sigil, with a small probability of awarding character-specific Warpath Sigils or War Elemental.

* **Weapon**: Terminus Weapon (fully transcended)

  * **Damage Cap V+** (stacked to Level 65)

Beatrix excels at rapid boss farming due to her *Delta Clock* mechanic. By maintaining her health in the "red zone," she gains massive attack speed and damage multipliers, making her ideal for fast Chaos-difficulty clears.

* **Weapon**: Ascension Weapon (Transcended to Level 10)
* **Key Sigils**:
  * **Delta Clock Unique Sigil** (extends the duration of her low-HP buff window)
  * **Enmity V+** (increases attack power as HP decreases)
  * **Beta V+** (reduces the cooldown of her gap-closers and high-damage skills)
  * **Stout Heart** (prevents interruption during her basic attack combos)

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the fastest quest to farm Knickknack Vouchers for Sigil farming?
The fastest quest to farm vouchers is the Chaos-difficulty quest **"Heralds of Doom"** against Ragnalia. The breakable parts from Ragnalia trade for a high number of vouchers at Siero's Knickknack Shack, allowing you to fill your Transmarvel gauge quickly. Alternatively, running the solo mode **The Conflux** at Depths 31-40 yields a large quantity of Tier V Sigils that can be traded directly for vouchers.

### Can I farm Warpath+ Sigils in multiplayer quests?
Yes, Warpath+ Sigils can drop from Chaos-difficulty multiplayer quests, specifically from the **Beelzebub** and **The World** showdowns. However, the drop rate in multiplayer is identical to solo play. If you want to target farm these Sigils quickly, running **The Conflux** solo is often faster due to the high density of Rare Monster rooms which have elevated drop tables.

### How does Weapon Transcendence affect my Sigil choices?
Weapon Transcendence allows you to unlock swappable traits on your weapons (Slot II up to level 25, Slot IV up to level 10). This unlocks additional Damage Cap and utility traits directly on your weapon, freeing up valuable Sigil slots on your characters. This allows you to run utility Sigils like *Improved Dodge*, *Autorevive*, or additional *Supplemental Damage* without sacrificing your damage output.

### What is the exact drop rate for War Elemental+ in Endless Ragnarok?
The base drop rate for **War Elemental+** from Level 5 Curios is approximately **1.2%**. In The Conflux (Depths 45+), the drop rate rises slightly to **2.0%** from gold chests. Because of this low drop rate, it is highly recommended to combine Curio farming with your standard Chaos-difficulty boss farming routes to passive-farm this Sigil over time.
