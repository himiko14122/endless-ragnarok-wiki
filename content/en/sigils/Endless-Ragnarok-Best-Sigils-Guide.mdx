## The Sigil Hierarchy: S-Tier and A-Tier Rankings

The expansion introduces several new Sigils that completely redefine the endgame meta. The hierarchy is divided into tiers based on how universally applicable a Sigil is, and how significantly it increases your overall Damage Cap. 

S-Tier Sigils are mandatory for almost all 28 characters in the game, including the 6 new DLC additions (Beatrix, Eustace, Fraux, Fediel, Gallanza, and Maglielle). These Sigils either bypass elemental resistances entirely, provide massive character-specific mechanics upgrades, or offer the highest stat scaling available in the game. A-Tier Sigils focus on optimizing specific playstyles, such as Skybound Art (SBA) generation, skill cooldown reduction, or critical hit consistency.

| Sigil Name | Tier | Primary Effect | Max Level | Primary Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warpath+** | SS | Character-specific mechanics overhaul + Damage Cap boost | 15 | The Conflux (Gateways), Fatebreaker Quests |
| **War Elemental+** | S | Converts all attacks into the enemy's weak element + extra stats | 15 | Curio Appraisal, Chaos-difficulty Quests |
| **Character Specific+** | S | Combines two character-exclusive traits into a single slot | 15 | Sierokarte's Knickknack Shack (Transmutation) |
| **Alpha / Beta / Gamma V+** | A | Boosts SBA/Skill/Barrier performance and raises Damage Cap | 15 | Beelzebub & The World Raid Drops |
| **Critical Hit Rate V+** | A | Increases Critical Hit Rate (up to +25% at Level 15) | 15 | Proud/Chaos Quests, Conflux Chests |

### S-Tier Breakdown
*   **Warpath+**: These are the ultimate **Endless Ragnarok must have Sigils** for endgame builds. Unlike standard Sigils, they modify your character's core mechanics (such as Beatrix's Delta Clock or Sandalphon's Supreme Primarch Mode) while simultaneously providing a unique Damage Cap increase that stacks with standard Damage Cap Sigils.
*   **War Elemental+**: A staple from the base game that remains mandatory in the expansion. It ensures your character always hits for elemental weakness damage, bypassing the standard damage reduction on off-element targets. The "+" version now allows for a secondary trait, making it far more versatile.
*   **Character Specific+**: Combining both unique character traits into a single Sigil slot frees up valuable space on your grid. This is essential for characters like Cagliostro or Narmaya, who rely heavily on both of their unique traits to function optimally.

## Optimizing Best Sigil Traits for Chaos Difficulty

When building your characters for Chaos difficulty, you cannot rely solely on offensive stats. Bosses in this difficulty tier can easily one-hit weaker characters if you do not slot in defensive utility. Therefore, identifying and equipping the **Endless Ragnarok best Sigil traits** on your secondary "+" slots is the key to clearing Fatebreaker quests.

The standard offensive cap in Endless Ragnarok is highly restrictive. To bypass this, you must run four Damage Cap V+ Sigils, which brings your Damage Cap trait level to 65. Once this baseline is met, you should focus on utility and survivability traits.

### Essential Offensive Traits
1.  **Supplementary Damage**: Provides a chance to deal an extra instance of damage equal to a percentage of your hit. This bypasses the standard Damage Cap entirely.
2.  **Critical Hit Rate**: Necessary to ensure your attacks consistently hit the damage ceiling.
3.  **Combo Booster**: Excellent for fast-attacking characters like Lancelot, Charlotta, and the new DLC character Fraux.

### Essential Defensive and Utility Traits
1.  **Stout Heart**: Prevents flinching from enemy attacks, allowing you to complete your combos uninterrupted.
2.  **Improved Dodge**: Increases the number of consecutive dodges you can perform and extends the invincibility window. This is mandatory for avoiding Beelzebub's arena-wide AoE attacks.
3.  **Guts / Autorevive**: Provides a safety net against one-hit kills in Chaos difficulty.

| Trait Name | Max Level | Stat Bonus at Max Level | Recommended Secondary Pairing |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Damage Cap** | 65 | +250% Damage Cap | Critical Hit Rate / Supplementary Damage |
| **Supplementary Damage** | 45 | 100% Activation Chance, +20% extra damage | Aegis / Improved Dodge |
| **Stout Heart** | 1 | Grants flinch immunity, -10% incoming damage | Damage Cap V+ |
| **Potion Hoarder** | 15 | Increases green/blue/revival potions carried | Autorevive / Guts |

Integrating these traits requires utilizing the [Weapon Transcendence Guide](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/) system. Fully transcending your weapon unlocks swappable trait slots (Slot II up to level 25, Slot IV up to level 10), which frees up your Sigil grid for more specialized setups.

## Farming Routes: The Conflux and Fatebreaker Quests

Acquiring the **Endless Ragnarok best Sigils** requires a structured farming approach. The two primary sources for these endgame items are **The Conflux** and **Fatebreaker Quests**.

### Route 1: The Conflux Solo Roguelite Run
The Conflux is a solo-only roguelite mode accessed via the gateways in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. It is the absolute fastest source of enhancement materials and high-tier Sigils in the game. A typical run takes roughly 20 minutes.

1.  **Preparation**: Choose your character and select your initial passive power-ups at the gateway interface.
2.  **Room Selection**: Always prioritize **Rare Monster Rooms** and **Treasure Rooms** over standard combat rooms. Rare monsters have a significantly higher chance of dropping Curios that appraise into War Elemental+ or Character Specific+ Sigils.
3.  **Boss Chambers**: Defeat the area bosses to secure Conflux Chests. These chests contain exclusive drop pools containing Warpath+ Sigils.
4.  **Exit Strategy**: If you run out of lives, you lose a portion of your collected rewards. If your build is struggling, exit at the designated checkpoints to secure your loot.

For a detailed breakdown of room layouts and enemy weaknesses in this mode, refer to our [Conflux Roguelite Guide](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/).

### Route 2: Fatebreaker Boss Farming
Fatebreaker quests are high-difficulty boss fights featuring empowered versions of Beelzebub, The World, and Ragnalia. These quests drop the specific materials needed for Weapon Transcendence, as well as high-tier Sigil trade-in items.

1.  **Matchmaking**: Utilize the full cross-platform crossplay (PS5, PS4, Switch 2, and PC) to form a coordinated four-player party.
2.  **Summon Setup**: Ensure Lyria is equipped with high-tier summons. During Link Time, coordinate your Skybound Arts to trigger a **Primal Burst**, which deals massive damage and stuns the boss, giving your team a safe window to farm breakable parts.
3.  **Part Breaking**: Focus all attacks on the boss's head or wings. Breaking parts increases the drop rate of rare Sigil materials by up to 40%.

For more details on the new characters you can bring to these raids, check out our [DLC Characters Guide](/en/characters/new-characters/).

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I unlock the Epilogue to access these endgame Sigils?
To unlock the Epilogue and gain access to Chaos-difficulty quests, The Conflux, and Fatebreaker runs, you must first complete the Proud-difficulty quest titled "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage". Once cleared, a cutscene will play in Seedhollow, unlocking the new progression systems.

### Can I transfer traits between Sigils using Weapon Transcendence?
No. Weapon Transcendence does not allow you to transfer traits directly between Sigils. Instead, it allows you to unlock extra trait slots directly on your weapon (Slot II and Slot IV), which function identically to equipped Sigils. This allows you to run fewer utility Sigils on your main grid.

### Are the new DLC characters' Sigils available in Sierokarte's Knickknack Shack?
Yes. Once you recruit the DLC characters (Beatrix, Eustace, Fraux, Fediel, Gallanza, or Maglielle) using Crewmate Cards or by purchasing the DLC pack, their specific character Sigils will immediately become available for trade and transmutation at Sierokarte's shop.

### What is the fastest way to farm Knickknack Vouchers for Sigil Transmutation?
The fastest method is running the Extreme-difficulty quest "Assault on the Imperial Battleship" and trading the dropped machine parts to Sierokarte. Alternatively, clearing deep floors in The Conflux rewards a massive quantity of high-tier materials that can be traded for vouchers.
