## The Core DPS Sigil Foundation (S-Tier & A-Tier Essentials)

Before diving into character-specific builds, every top-tier DPS character must run a core framework of Sigils. Without this foundation, your character will hit the hard damage cap, rendering any additional Attack or Stamina Sigils useless. The standard setup utilizes a mix of Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Sigils (acquired from Lucilius and high-level Fatebreaker quests) to raise these damage caps.

The *Endless Ragnarok War Elemental plus Sigil* is the single most important Sigil in the expansion. It bypasses elemental resistance entirely, treating all your attacks as the target's weak element while granting a flat 20% damage multiplier. In Chaos-difficulty quests, this is non-negotiable. 

To ensure every attack hits this elevated cap, you must achieve a 100% Critical Hit Rate. This is accomplished using an *Endless Ragnarok Critical Hit Rate V plus* Sigil, which should ideally feature a high-value secondary trait like Stamina, Tyranny, or Quick Cooldown.

| Sigil Name | Tier | Max Level | Primary Effect | Recommended Sub-Trait (Slot +) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **War Elemental+** | S | 15 | Converts all attacks to weak element; +20% damage. | Damage Cap, Aegis, or Potion Hoarder |
| **Critical Hit Rate V+** | S | 15 | +15% Critical Hit Rate (at Lvl 15). | Stamina, Tyranny, or Linked Together |
| **Alpha V+** | A | 15 | Boosts Normal Attack Damage Cap; adds SBA gauge on normal hits. | Cooldown Reduction or Cascade |
| **Beta V+** | A | 15 | Boosts Skill Damage Cap; reduces skill cooldowns on SBA use. | Quick Ability or Nimble Onslaught |
| **Gamma V+** | A | 15 | Boosts SBA Damage Cap; provides a temporary shield upon SBA activation. | Aegis or Improved Dodge |

To maximize these slots, players must utilize the [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/) system. Upgrading your Terminus or Ascension weapons to Transcendence Level 250 unlocks extra trait slots, allowing you to move utility traits (like Potion Hoarder or Aegis) directly onto your weapon, freeing up valuable Sigil slots on your grid.

## Character-Specific Loadouts: Sandalphon & Beatrix

The meta is currently dominated by Sandalphon in the SS-tier and the newly introduced DLC character Beatrix. Both characters feature unique mechanics that require highly specialized Sigil configurations to extract maximum damage.

### Sandalphon (SS-Tier)

Sandalphon relies on entering *Supreme Primarch Mode*, which transforms his standard attacks into rapid, multi-hit lightstep sword waves. The *Endless Ragnarok best Sigils for Sandalphon* focus heavily on normal attack cap limits and rapid SBA generation to maintain his Primarch transformation.

1. **Ainsoph Aur+ (Character Sigil):** Reduces the cooldown of his skills while in Supreme Primarch Mode and increases his lightstep sword wave damage cap by 30%.
2. **Alpha V+ (x2):** Since the majority of Sandalphon's damage during his burst window comes from normal attack sword waves, doubling up on Alpha Sigils is mandatory to raise his normal attack ceiling.
3. **War Elemental+:** Essential for converting his light-element attacks into the weakness of bosses like Beelzebub.
4. **Critical Hit Rate V+:** Paired with a Wrightstone that features at least level 10 Critical Hit Rate to hit the 100% threshold.

For a detailed breakdown of his skills and combat rotation, check the [Sandalphon Character Guide](/en/characters/sandalphon-guide/).

### Beatrix (DLC DPS)

Beatrix is a high-risk, high-reward character who utilizes the *Delta Clock* mechanic. She gains massive attack speed and damage multipliers when her health is low or when she triggers precise timing windows. The *Endless Ragnarok best Sigils for Beatrix* leverage her low-HP playstyle.

1. **Delta Overdrive+ (Character Sigil):** Extends the duration of her Delta Clock buff windows and prevents her from dying to fatal damage while the clock is active (120-second cooldown).
2. **Enmity V+:** Unlike other DPS characters who run Stamina, Beatrix benefits immensely from Enmity due to her self-damaging skills.
3. **Beta V+ (x2):** Beatrix relies on her active skills to reset her Delta Clock. Maximizing her Skill Damage Cap ensures her high-multiplier offensive skills hit their absolute limits.
4. **Glass Cannon:** Because Beatrix has built-in invincibility windows during her Delta Clock, she can safely run Glass Cannon for a flat 30% damage boost, despite the dizzy penalty.

## The Conflux Farming and Sigil Synthesis

Acquiring these high-tier Sigils requires running [The Conflux](/en/conflux/), the roguelite solo mode accessible via gateways in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. The deeper you descend into the Conflux, the higher the drop rates for S-tier Sigils and the rare materials needed for Sigil Synthesis at the Knickknack Shack.

To farm effectively:
1. **Choose the Right Gateways:** Always prioritize Gateways that offer "Sigil Hoard" or "Curio Cache" rewards.
2. **Clear Rare Monsters:** Defeating rare monsters within the Conflux guarantees a drop of high-tier enhancement materials.
3. **Synthesize at Knickknack Shack:** Use the materials gathered from Fatebreaker quests to fuse sub-traits onto your primary Sigils. This is the only way to get combinations like *War Elemental+* with *Potion Hoarder*.

According to the official patch notes on the [Cygames Relink Portal](https://relink.granbluefantasy.jp/en/), the drop rates for "+" variant Sigils scale directly with the depth level of the Conflux:

| Conflux Depth | Key Sigil Drops | Base Drop Rate | Recommended Power Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Depths 1-10** | Critical Hit Rate V, Damage Cap V | 15.0% | 15,000 |
| **Depths 11-20** | Alpha V+, Beta V+, Gamma V+ | 8.5% | 18,000 |
| **Depths 21-30** | War Elemental+, Character Warpath+ | 3.2% | 22,000 |
| **Depths 30+ (Chaos)** | Dual-Trait S-Tier Sigils | 1.5% | 25,000+ |

Farming these depths requires a highly optimized build. If you are struggling to clear Depths 20+, ensure you have unlocked the Master Traits (Insight, Essence, and Crux paths) at Level 100 for your active characters. These paths provide passive stat boosts that make surviving the deeper levels of the Conflux much easier.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Should I run Alpha, Beta, or Gamma Sigils on my DPS characters?
This depends on your character's primary source of damage. If your character relies on normal attack combos (like Sandalphon's lightstep sword waves or Lancelot's slash spam), prioritize Alpha Sigils. If your character deals the majority of their damage through active skills (like Beatrix or Narmaya), prioritize Beta Sigils. Gamma Sigils are generally run as a one-off to provide a safety shield during SBA chains.

### How do I reach 100% Critical Hit Rate without wasting Sigil slots?
To reach 100% Critical Hit Rate, combine a single *Endless Ragnarok Critical Hit Rate V plus* Sigil (which provides +15% at level 15) with your character's base critical hit rate, Master Trait nodes, and a high-level Wrightstone. A Level 10 Critical Hit Rate Wrightstone combined with the Sigil and Master Traits will easily push your character to the 100% cap.

### Can I stack multiple War Elemental+ Sigils?
No. The elemental conversion and the 20% damage multiplier from the *Endless Ragnarok War Elemental plus Sigil* do not stack. Equipping more than one will waste a slot. Instead, focus on stacking Damage Cap, Alpha/Beta/Gamma, or character-specific Warpath+ Sigils to increase your damage ceiling.

### What is the minimum Sigil setup for Chaos difficulty?
At minimum, you need a full set of Level 10+ Sigils across all 12 slots. Prioritize Critical Hit Rate V+, Damage Cap V+, and one Alpha or Beta V+ matching your character's damage type. The remaining slots should be filled with survivability Sigils (Stamina V+, Defense V+, Guts) and character Warpath+ if available. Without this baseline, Chaos-tier bosses like Beelzebub will one-shot your character regardless of skill level.
