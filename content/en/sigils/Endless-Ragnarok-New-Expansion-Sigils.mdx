## Overview of New Sigil Tiers and Rarity

The expansion introduces several new tiers of Sigils that surpass the base game's limits. While standard Tier V Sigils remain useful, the new **Endless Ragnarok Tier V Sigils** (often designated as V+ or S-Tier) feature secondary trait slots that can roll with previously restricted endgame traits. 

The most sought-after additions are the **Warpath+** and **War Elemental+** Sigils. Unlike base game variants, these updated Sigils allow players to stack elemental advantage while simultaneously slotting utility traits like *Autorevive* or *Guts*. Additionally, the expansion introduces specialized Sigil pools that drop exclusively from [The Conflux](/en/conflux/), a roguelite solo mode accessed via gateways in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum.

Refer to the table below for a breakdown of the new Sigil rarities, their upgrade caps, and primary acquisition sources.

| Sigil Tier / Type | Max Level (Base) | Max Level (Transcended) | Primary Acquisition Source | Unique Characteristic |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Tier V+ (Expansion)** | 15 | 25 | Chaos Difficulty Quests / Curios | Rolls with dual offensive/defensive traits |
| **Warpath+** | 15 | 15 | The Conflux (Depths 30+) | Character-specific mechanics booster with + sub-stat |
| **War Elemental+** | 15 | 15 | Fatebreaker Quests / Curio Synthesis | Converts all damage to advantageous element + utility slot |
| **Alpha / Beta / Gamma+** | 15 | 25 | Beelzebub Raid / Trans-Synthesis | Increases specific Skybound Art (SBA) and Link Time caps |

To upgrade these Sigils beyond Level 15, players must engage with the [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/) system, which unlocks swappable trait slots and increases the maximum level cap of equipped Sigils to Level 25. This process requires rare materials dropped by the Heralds of Doom boss, Ragnalia.

## Legendary Hybrid Sigils and Meta Trait Combinations

With the introduction of the Summon System in Chaos-difficulty quests, party synergy is more important than ever. Players can equip passive summons like Lucilius and Rolan, which grant party-wide stat increases. To maximize these bonuses, players use hybrid Sigils like **Alpha+**, **Beta+**, and **Gamma+**.

These hybrid Sigils provide specific buffs during Link Time and Skybound Art (SBA) chains:

*   **Alpha+ (SBA Focus):** Increases Normal Attack Damage Cap. When using an SBA, it refunds a percentage of the SBA gauge to the user and adjacent allies.
*   **Beta+ (Skill Focus):** Increases Skill Damage Cap. Reduces skill cooldowns when performing a Link Attack.
*   **Gamma+ (Survival/Shield Focus):** Increases overall Damage Cap. Generates a temporary barrier equal to a percentage of the user's max HP upon executing an SBA.

To optimize characters like [Sandalphon](/en/characters/sandalphon-guide/), who dominates the SS-tier with his *Supreme Primarch Mode*, players should combine Gamma+ with **Endless Ragnarok Tier V Sigils** that carry the *Cascade* and *Quick Cooldown* sub-traits. This setup ensures that Sandalphon can spam his lightstep sword waves with minimal downtime while maintaining a constant shield.

| Hybrid Sigil Type | Primary Cap Boost (Lvl 25) | Secondary Effect (Lvl 25) | Recommended Character Archetype |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Alpha+** | +40% Normal Attack Cap | +15% SBA Gauge Refunded | Auto-attack focus (e.g., Charlotta, Lancelot) |
| **Beta+** | +40% Skill Damage Cap | -10% Skill Cooldown on Link | Skill-spammers (e.g., Cagliostro, Percival) |
| **Gamma+** | +30% All Damage Caps | 20,000 HP Barrier on SBA | High-risk, low-HP builds (e.g., Vaseraga) |

To farm these hybrid Sigils efficiently, players must challenge Beelzebub in the Chaos-difficulty quest "The Chaos Chamber." This quest requires completing the Proud-tier quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue content.

## Farming Routes: The Conflux and Fatebreaker Quests

Acquiring the items on the **Endless Ragnarok new Sigils list** requires running specific endgame content. The most efficient route for farming these Sigils is **The Conflux**, a solo roguelite mode. 

### Step-by-Step Conflux Farming Guide

1.  **Select Your Gateway:** Travel to Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum and select "The Conflux" gateway.
2.  **Choose Pre-Run Power-ups:** Before entering, choose three passive power-ups. Prioritize *Sigil Drop Rate Up* and *Curio Drop Rate Up*.
3.  **Navigate the Rooms:** The Conflux consists of randomly generated rooms. Always choose *Combat* or *Rare Monster* rooms over *Treasure* rooms, as rare monsters have a higher chance of dropping S-tier Sigils.
4.  **Reach Depth 30+:** The new **Endless Ragnarok new expansion Sigils** only drop at Depth 30 and below. Runs take approximately 20 minutes to reach Depth 40.
5.  **Defeat the Gatekeeper:** Clear the boss at the end of the run to secure your rewards.

For players seeking Weapon Transcendence materials alongside Sigils, **Fatebreaker Quests** are the preferred option. These high-difficulty quests feature bosses like Ragnalia and the Arcarum creation, The World.

## Optimization Strategies for Chaos Difficulty

When tackling Chaos difficulty, running pure offensive builds will often lead to quick defeats. Enemies at this level can knock out weaker characters in a single hit. Therefore, your Sigil loadout must strike a balance between damage cap optimization and survivability.

### The 60-40 Rule for Sigil Slots

An effective baseline for Chaos-difficulty builds is the **60-40 rule**: allocate 60% of your Sigil slots to offensive cap-up flags (such as *Damage Cap V+*, *War Elemental+*, and *Character Specific Sigils*) and 40% to defensive/utility traits. 

Key defensive traits to look for as sub-stats on your **Endless Ragnarok Tier V Sigils** include:

*   **Stout Heart:** Prevents interruption from enemy attacks and reduces incoming damage by 25%.
*   **Steel Nerves:** Further reduces damage taken when Stout Heart is active.
*   **Stronghold:** Increases defense as your HP decreases, scaling up to +45% defense.
*   **Autorevive / Guts:** Essential safety nets for surviving Beelzebub's full-screen attacks.

Additionally, make sure to align your Sigils with your equipped Summons. In Chaos-difficulty quests, Lyria can summon primal beasts once the party triggers a *Primal Burst* (achieved by chaining four Skybound Arts during Link Time). Equipping Sigils that boost SBA charge rates, such as *Uplift V+*, allows your team to summon Lucilius or Rolan faster, granting temporary invincibility and massive attack buffs that help bypass difficult boss phases.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Where is the best place to farm Endless Ragnarok Tier V Sigils?
The fastest source for these Sigils is **The Conflux** solo mode (specifically Depths 30 and below) accessed via Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. For targeted hybrid Sigils like Alpha+, Beta+, and Gamma+, farm the **Fatebreaker Quests** featuring Beelzebub and The World on Chaos difficulty.

### Can you stack Warpath and Warpath+ Sigils?
No, you cannot stack standard Warpath and Warpath+ Sigils to double their unique effects. Equipping both will only apply the higher-level modifier of the two. It is recommended to use the Warpath+ variant, as it includes an extra sub-stat slot for utility traits.

### How do the new DLC character specific Sigils affect Beatrix's Delta Clock?
Beatrix's *Delta Clock Acceleration+* Sigil increases the speed of her Delta Clock hands by 25% and extends her "Overdrive" buff state by 8 seconds. This allows her to enter her high-damage state more quickly and maintain her optimal DPS rotation for longer periods during combat.

### Do these new expansion Sigils work in crossplay multiplayer?
Yes. *Endless Ragnarok* supports full cross-platform play across PS5, PS4, Switch 2, and PC (Steam). Any Sigil obtained and equipped on your character will function normally in multiplayer sessions, regardless of the platform the host or other players are using.
