## Setting Up Crossplay Sessions Across PS5, PS4, Switch 2, and PC

To begin playing with friends on different platforms, you must first enable crossplay within the network settings of Granblue Fantasy: Relink. By default, crossplay is enabled, but manual verification is recommended to prevent matchmaking errors. 

1. Launch the game and load your save file.
2. Open the main menu and navigate to **System** > **Options** > **Network Settings**.
3. Locate the **Cross-Network Matchmaking** toggle and set it to **On**.
4. Set your **Matchmaking Region** to **Any** if you are playing with friends in different geographic territories, or select a specific region (e.g., North America, Europe, Asia) to minimize latency.
5. Save your settings and return to the game world.

Once enabled, you can access the Quest Counter in either Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum to host or join multiplayer lobbies. The crossplay system utilizes Cygames' proprietary matchmaking backend, ensuring that players on Steam can connect directly with players on Nintendo and PlayStation ecosystems.

## Generating and Sharing Your Friend Code

Because players across different platforms do not share a unified console-level friends list, Cygames uses a custom session ID and friend code system. If you want to know about **Endless Ragnarok crossplay how to invite** players from other platforms, the process relies entirely on these unique room codes.

### How to Create a Crossplay Lobby
To host a session, go to the Quest Counter and select **Play Online**. Choose **Create Online Session** and configure your lobby parameters. To keep the lobby private for your friends, set the **Passcode** option to **On** and enter a 4-digit PIN. 

Once the lobby is created, press the Options/Menu button to open the Session Liaison screen. Here, you will see a 9-digit alphanumeric code. This is your **Endless Ragnarok crossplay friend code**. Copy this code and share it with your teammates via external communication channels.

### How to Join Using a Friend Code
For players joining the session, follow these steps:
1. Go to the Quest Counter and select **Play Online**.
2. Select **Search for Online Session**.
3. Choose **Search by Session ID / Friend Code**.
4. Enter the 9-digit **Endless Ragnarok crossplay friend code** provided by the host.
5. If prompted, enter the 4-digit Passcode to enter the lobby.

```
[Quest Counter] ➔ [Play Online] ➔ [Search by Session ID] ➔ Enter 9-Digit Code
```

Using this method bypasses platform restrictions, allowing a PC player to invite a Switch 2 player and two PS5 players into the same party. When configuring these sessions, the lobby host can customize search criteria to optimize matchmaking filters, as outlined in the table below.

To initiate **Endless Ragnarok local wireless play Switch 2**, select the Local Wireless option at the Quest Counter. Keep in mind that **Endless Ragnarok local wireless play Switch 2** does not support crossplay with PC or PlayStation consoles, as it relies on physical ad-hoc wireless chips embedded in the Switch 2 hardware.

Meanwhile, players utilizing **Endless Ragnarok PC crossplay** must ensure their Steam client is updated to the latest version. ## Co-op Strategy for Chaos Difficulty and Fatebreaker Quests

Tackling high-difficulty content like Fatebreaker quests or Chaos-difficulty boss fights against Ragnalia requires precise coordination that latency can easily disrupt. When playing in crossplay lobbies, network synchronization becomes a critical factor, especially for characters with tight counter-attack windows or timing-based mechanics.

### Character Synergy and Latency Mitigation
If your host is on PC and you are playing on Switch 2 with higher latency, avoid playing characters with strict frame-perfect inputs, such as [Sandalphon](/en/builds/sandalphon-build/) in Supreme Primarch Mode or Beatrix with her Delta Clock timing windows. Instead, opt for characters with larger area-of-effect utilities or defensive support capabilities:
*   **Maglielle (DLC Support):** Provides team-wide shields and barrier buffs, allowing players to survive lag-induced hits.
*   **Cagliostro (S-Tier Support):** Offers quick revives, healing, and attack buffs that remain effective even with minor network delays.
*   **Eustace (DLC Ranged):** Can deal consistent damage from a safe distance, mitigating the risk of getting caught in boss telegraphs due to packet loss.

### Managing the Summon System in Co-op
In Chaos-difficulty quests, the Summon System allows players to bring up to four passive primal beasts, with the ability to summon powerful entities like Lucilius or Rolan during Link Time. 
*   **Coordinate Summon Triggers:** Ensure your party coordinates their Skybound Arts (SBA) to trigger a Full Chain. When all four players execute their SBA consecutively during Link Time, a Primal Burst is activated. 
*   **Host Priority:** Have the player with the most stable connection (typically the PC or PS5 wired host) initiate the SBA chain. This minimizes the risk of desync interrupting the chain window.

### The Conflux Co-op Runs
While [The Conflux](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) is primarily designed as a solo roguelite gateway mode, players can run it in duo co-op. During cross-platform Conflux runs, power-ups chosen at the gate apply individually, but the difficulty scales by 1.5x for the second player. Communication is key: coordinate who takes the offensive Master Traits buffs and who builds defensive Sigils to balance the team's composition.

## Troubleshooting Connection Issues and Crossplay Latency

If you encounter errors when attempting to join crossplay lobbies, use this checklist to diagnose and resolve the issue:

### 1. Matchmaking Version Mismatch
This error occurs when one player has not updated their game client to the latest patch. Because Cygames frequently pushes minor hotfixes for the Endless Ragnarok expansion, ensure all players check for updates on their respective platforms. On Steam, verify game file integrity; on Switch 2 and PlayStation, use the "Check for Update" option on the home screen.

### 2. NAT Type Restrictions
Granblue Fantasy: Relink relies on peer-to-peer (P2P) connections for lobby sessions, meaning the host's NAT type determines lobby stability.
*   **NAT Type 1 (Open):** Ideal host. Can connect to all players.
*   **NAT Type 2 (Moderate):** Can connect to Open and Moderate players.
*   **NAT Type 3 (Strict):** Cannot host crossplay lobbies. Can only join Open hosts and will frequently experience disconnects.
If the host has NAT Type 3, designate a different player with NAT Type 1 or 2 as the lobby creator.

### 3. Crossplay-Specific Error Codes
*   **Error Code 09-0002 (Connection Lost):** Usually caused by a regional mismatch during high-traffic hours. Change your Matchmaking Region from "Any" to your specific shared region.
*   **Error Code 12-0084 (Session Full/Expired):** The host's **Endless Ragnarok crossplay friend code** has expired. Lobbies automatically close if inactive in town for more than 20 minutes. The host must recreate the session and distribute a new code.

For further support, check the official [Cygames Support Portal](https://www.cygames.co.jp/en/) for real-time server status updates and scheduled maintenance windows.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I transfer my save progress between platforms using cross-save?
No. While Endless Ragnarok supports full crossplay for multiplayer matchmaking, it does not support cross-save or cross-progression. Your save data on PC (Steam), PlayStation, and Switch 2 are completely separate. You cannot transfer your unlocked characters, Weapon Transcendence levels, or Sigils from one platform to another.

### Are there any graphic or performance differences during crossplay?
Yes. PlayStation 5 and PC players can run the game at up to 120 FPS or higher, while Switch 2 is capped at 60 FPS in docked mode and PlayStation 4 runs at 30 FPS. The netcode compensates for these frame rate differences, but players on lower-performing hardware may experience minor visual desynchronization during flashier visual effects, such as a Primal Burst or during multi-stage boss transitions.

### Can I disable crossplay if I only want to play with players on my own platform?
Yes. You can disable cross-network matchmaking at any time by navigating to **System** > **Options** > **Network Settings** and toggling **Cross-Network Matchmaking** to **Off**. This will restrict your matchmaking queue and lobby searches to players on your specific platform ecosystem (e.g., PlayStation players will only match with other PS4/PS5 players).

### Do DLC characters require separate purchases on each platform for co-op?
Yes, if you own the game on multiple platforms. However, in multiplayer, you can play with anyone who owns the DLC characters (Beatrix, Eustace, Fraux, Fediel, Gallanza, Maglielle) regardless of whether you have purchased them yourself. You will still see their models, skills, and combat animations in your shared crossplay sessions.
