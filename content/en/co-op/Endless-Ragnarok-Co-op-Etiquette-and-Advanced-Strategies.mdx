## Link Time and Chain Burst Optimization

Maximizing the Link Gauge and executing coordinated Skybound Arts (SBA) are core mechanics for high-level co-op play. In Chaos-difficulty quests, random SBA activation can disrupt your team's damage cycle. This often causes the boss to enter an Overdrive state or trigger a wipe mechanic that could have been avoided.

### SBA Chain Ordering

The player who initiates the SBA chain (the "opener") dictates the team's damage window. Fast-activation characters, such as Charlotta or Lancelot, should initiate the chain to lock the boss in place instantly. Slow-activation DPS characters, such as Narmaya or the DLC character Beatrix, should hold their SBAs for the third or fourth slot in the chain. This allows them to maintain their offensive uptime before entering the animation lock.

1. **The Opener (Slot 1):** Initiates when the boss is recovery-locked or immediately after a major area-of-effect (AoE) attack ends.
2. **The Bridges (Slots 2 & 3):** Trigger their SBAs when the preceding character's SBA gauge connection circle reaches approximately 20% remaining. This maximizes the duration of the boss's stun lock.
3. **The Anchor (Slot 4):** Triggers their SBA to finalize the Full Chain. In Chaos difficulty, if all four players have equipped summons, this Full Chain initiates a Primal Burst, dealing massive non-elemental damage.

### Link Time Activation

Triggering Link Time requires all four players to perform a Link Attack when the Link Gauge reaches 100%. Do not trigger a Link Attack if a party member is currently performing an SBA or is incapacitated, as this wastes the trigger window. During Link Time, skill cooldowns are significantly reduced, and damage caps are raised.

| Link Level / State | Damage Cap Bonus | SBA Gauge Gain Modifier | Co-op Action Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **0% - 49%** | +0% | 1.0x | Focus on building Link Temp via blue-labeled attacks. |
| **50% - 99%** | +10% | 1.2x | Prepare SBA gauges; do not initiate solo SBAs. |
| **Link Time (100%)** | +30% | 1.5x | Spam high-damage skills; delay SBA until Link Time expires. |
| **Primal Burst Window** | +50% | N/A | Triggered by executing a Full Chain during Link Time. |

## Summon System Coordination in Chaos Difficulty

The Summon System is restricted to Chaos-difficulty quests and higher. It requires coordination to prevent players from overriding each other's active summons. Because only one summon can be active on the field at a time, activating a summon at the wrong moment can cancel a teammate's active summon and waste its cooldown.

### Summon Rotation Priority

When entering a lobby for Fatebreaker quests, players should designate a summon order based on their party roles. Controllable summons, specifically Lucilius and Rolan, should be saved for specific boss phases. For example, Rolan's protective shields are best used during Beelzebub's "Black Flies" phase, while Lucilius's offensive sword waves should be deployed during break windows to maximize damage.

```
[Player 1: Rolan (Defense)] ---> [Player 2: Proto-Bahamut (DPS)] ---> [Player 3: Lucilius (Controllable DPS)] ---> [Player 4: Support Summon]
```

*   **Rule 1:** Never summon immediately after a teammate has activated theirs. Monitor the active summon duration bar on the left side of the screen.
*   **Rule 2:** Avoid summoning during a boss's invincibility transition. Bosses like The World often enter untargetable states where summon attacks will deal zero damage.
*   **Rule 3:** Save your summon for the Primal Burst chain. A Primal Burst triggered during Link Time gains a 50% damage bonus based on the average level of the equipped summons.

## The Ultimate Co-op Carry Build

For players looking to assist struggling teams in public lobbies, running a dedicated support-hybrid build is highly effective. Using characters like Maglielle or Vane allows you to protect teammates from one-hit knockouts while maintaining respectable damage output.

### Sigil Configuration

To run an effective **Endless Ragnarok co op carry build**, you must balance personal survivability with team utility. Prioritize S-tier Sigils like *Warpath+* and *War Elemental+* alongside defensive utility options.

*   **Warpath+ (Character Specific):** Reduces skill cooldowns when performing perfect dodges or parries, allowing you to reuse defensive skills quickly.
*   **Alpha/Beta/Gamma V+:** These Sigils increase your SBA damage cap and provide team-wide barrier shields when you execute an SBA.
*   **Stout Heart & Aegis V+:** Prevents interruption from enemy attacks and increases maximum health to survive Chaos-difficulty mechanics.

```
[Warpath+] + [War Elemental+] + [Alpha/Beta/Gamma V+] + [Stout Heart/Aegis V+] = Co-op Carry Foundation
```

Ensure your weapon has undergone [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/) to at least Level 10. This unlocks Slot II and Slot IV trait customizations. For a co-op carry build, roll for the following traits:

1. **Slot II (Max Level 25):** Critical Hit Rate or Cascade (to reduce cooldowns on hit).
2. **Slot IV (Max Level 10):** Auto-Revive or Guts. In Chaos difficulty, having these passive survival traits prevents you from consuming the team's shared critical gauge.

## Communication Protocols and Developer Tips

Effective communication prevents coordination errors in public lobbies. While voice chat is available, using quick-chat shortcuts and stickers is often faster and helps bridge language barriers in crossplay matchmaking.

### Developer Tips for Quick-Chat

According to **Endless Ragnarok co op developer tips** shared by Cygames during the July 2026 update broadcast, players should customize their quick-chat wheel specifically for Fatebreaker quests. The developers recommend assigning the following callouts to your primary shortcuts:

*   *"Hold SBA!"* – Use this when the boss is about to enter an invincibility phase or when you want to save SBAs for Link Time.
*   *"Summon Ready!"* – Indicates that you are about to summon Lucilius or Rolan, alerting teammates to hold their summons.
*   *"Focus the Core!"* – Used during the Ragnalia fight to coordinate damage on the boss's elemental shields.

These **Endless Ragnarok co op communication tips** help organize random groups without voice chat. Additionally, players should wait at least 5 seconds after joining a lobby before posting a quest. This gives the host time to adjust their gear or select the correct difficulty level.

## Advanced Boss Strategies for Co-op Teams

Defeating bosses like Beelzebub and Ragnalia on Chaos difficulty requires precise execution of phase-specific mechanics.

### Beelzebub's Chaos Phase Coordination

During Beelzebub's "Chaos Legion" phase, he shields himself and summons dark energy rings across the arena. 

1. **Spread Out:** Do not stack on top of each other. Stacking causes the dark energy rings to overlap, dealing fatal damage to anyone caught inside.
2. **Break the Shield:** Use ranged characters like Eustace or multi-hit physical attackers like Charlotta to deplete his shield gauge.
3. **Save SBA for the End:** Once the shield breaks, Beelzebub is stunned for 8 seconds. Initiate your SBA chain immediately to extend this stun window.

### Ragnalia's Wipe-Mechanic Interruption

Ragnalia features a wipe mechanic called "Genesis Nova." If the team fails to deplete her elemental shield within 25 seconds, the party will wipe.

1. **Element Matching:** Ensure at least one party member is running a *War Elemental+* Sigil to bypass her elemental resistance shields.
2. **Deploy Maglielle's Flying Weapons:** Maglielle players should deploy their flying weapons to deal constant stagger damage while staying safe from ground hazards.
3. **Use Controllable Summons:** If your team's damage is low, summon Lucilius. His light waves deal high stagger damage, which helps deplete Ragnalia's shield quickly.

## Co-op Strategy FAQ

### How do we trigger a Primal Burst during Link Time?
To trigger a Primal Burst, all four players must equip a summon and execute a Full Chain (four consecutive SBAs) while Link Time is active. The Primal Burst will trigger automatically at the end of the chain, dealing high non-elemental damage based on your team's average summon level.

### What is the etiquette for using summons in public lobbies?
Only one summon can be active on the field at a time. Never activate your summon if a teammate's summon is currently active, as this will overwrite theirs and waste its cooldown. Coordinate your summon order using quick-chat before the fight begins.

### Which character is best for an Endless Ragnarok co op carry build?
Maglielle and Vane are excellent choices for carry builds. Maglielle provides team-wide shields, barrier buffs, and ranged support, while Vane can deploy a protective dome that blocks all incoming damage during major boss wipe mechanics.

### How does crossplay affect matchmaking latency in high-difficulty raids?
Endless Ragnarok features full crossplay across PS5, PS4, Switch 2, and PC. While latency is generally low, players should avoid initiating SBAs during high-latency spikes to prevent input lag from disrupting the chain timing. Use the "SBA Ready!" quick-chat command to signal your inputs clearly.

## FAQ

**Q: Is voice chat required for Chaos-tier co-op?**
A: No, voice chat is not required. The in-game quick chat system provides all necessary signals for coordinating SBAs, heals, and summons. Most successful Chaos clears happen through quick chat coordination alone.

**Q: What happens if a player disconnects mid-quest?**
A: If a player disconnects, the remaining party members can continue the quest. The missing player's character will be controlled by AI, though AI performance is significantly lower than a human player. The quest can still be completed successfully.

**Q: Can I join Chaos-tier quests as a lower-level character?**
A: Technically yes, but it is strongly discouraged. Undergeared characters will be one-hit by most boss attacks in Chaos difficulty. Always ensure your character has Level 100, a Master Trait, and optimized Sigils before joining Chaos-tier content.

**Q: How does crossplay matchmaking work?**
A: Crossplay is seamless — all platforms share the same matchmaking pool. When searching for a party, you will be matched with players from any platform. There are no platform-specific restrictions or separate queues.
