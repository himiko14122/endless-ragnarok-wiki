## Core Archetypes and the Co-op Meta

## Executing Primal Bursts and Link Time in Co-op

The Summon System is a core mechanic in Chaos-difficulty quests. Lyria allows players to bring up to four summons into battle, though only one can be fielded at a time. In co-op play, coordinating your summons and triggering a **Primal Burst** is the most reliable way to defeat bosses like Beelzebub.

A Primal Burst is triggered when all four party members execute their Skybound Arts (SBA) in rapid succession during **Link Time** (which is achieved by reaching a 100% Link level through coordinated attacks and link attacks).

### Step-by-Step Primal Burst Coordination

1.  **Build the Link Gauge:** Use high-stun attacks (like Zeta's loops or Siegfried's combos) to trigger Link Attacks. Do not activate your SBAs yet.
2.  **Enter Link Time:** Once the Link Gauge reaches 100%, execute a Link Attack to enter Link Time. During this period, skill cooldowns are drastically reduced, and damage caps are raised.
3.  **Summon Field Control:** Have one player activate a controllable summon, such as **Lucilius** or **Rolan**. Lucilius will rain down light swords to stagger the boss, while Rolan provides a massive defense and attack speed aura to the party.
4.  **Chain SBAs:** Before Link Time ends, the first player must initiate their SBA. The remaining three players must chain their SBAs immediately after.
5.  **Trigger Primal Burst:** Once the fourth SBA finishes, the Primal Burst will trigger, dealing millions of neutral element damage and leaving the boss in a prolonged Break state.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Does Endless Ragnarok support crossplay across all platforms?
Yes. Endless Ragnarok features full cross-platform play between PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Switch 2, and PC (Steam). You can invite friends using their in-game CyID regardless of the platform they are playing on.

### How do we unlock Chaos difficulty and Fatebreaker quests?
To unlock Chaos difficulty and access Fatebreaker quests, your lobby host must complete the Proud Quest titled **"The Tale of Bahamut's Rage"**. This will unlock the Epilogue campaign and grant access to the gateways in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum.

### What is the benefit of running a controllable summon like Lucilius in co-op?
Controllable summons can be directed by the summoning player to target specific boss limbs or shield generators. Lucilius, in particular, deals massive area-of-effect damage that can instantly clear adds during the Beelzebub and Ragnalia boss fights, keeping your DPS players safe.

### How do S-tier Sigils like Warpath+ affect co-op builds?
Warpath+ Sigils are character-specific drops found in deep runs of **The Conflux** roguelite mode. They grant unique gameplay modifiers—such as modifying Beatrix's Delta Clock decay rate or increasing the damage cap of Sandalphon's lightstep sword waves—making them mandatory for end-game co-op builds.
