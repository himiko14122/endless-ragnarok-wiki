## Weapon Transcendence and the Road to Slot EX

Weapon Transcendence is divided into seven distinct stages, each requiring rare materials sourced from Fatebreaker quests and The Conflux. Reaching Transcendence Stage 7 transforms your gear into an **Endless Ragnarok max level 7 weapon**, which automatically unlocks the Slot EX passive benefit and activates the corresponding cosmetic aura. 

To begin transcending a weapon, you must first complete the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue content, followed by unlocking Chaos difficulty. Only weapons that have been fully upgraded to Level 150 and have their weapon masteries maxed out are eligible for Transcendence.

| Transcendence Stage | Level Cap | Major Unlock | Key Materials Required |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1** | 160 | Slot II Active | 10x Fatebreaker Core, 50x Refinement Sand |
| **Stage 2** | 170 | Slot II Level 15 Cap | 15x Fatebreaker Core, 5x Conflux Splinter |
| **Stage 3** | 180 | Slot II Level 25 Cap | 20x Fatebreaker Core, 10x Conflux Splinter |
| **Stage 4** | 190 | Slot IV Active | 10x Astral Shard, 15x Conflux Splinter |
| **Stage 5** | 200 | Slot IV Level 5 Cap | 15x Astral Shard, 5x Ragnarok Onyx |
| **Stage 6** | 210 | Slot IV Level 10 Cap | 20x Astral Shard, 10x Ragnarok Onyx |
| **Stage 7** | 220 | **Slot EX Reward** | 5x Beelzebub Jewel, 1x Genesis Essence |

Upgrading a weapon to Stage 7 requires defeating Beelzebub in the Chaos-difficulty raid "Fatebreaker: Fall of the False God." This fight yields Beelzebub Jewels, which are the primary bottleneck for unlocking Slot EX. Additionally, players must run [The Conflux solo mode](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) via the gateways in Seedhollow or Tredame Sanctum to farm the necessary Conflux Splinters and Ragnarok Onyx.

## Deciphering Slot II and Slot IV Swappable Traits

As you progress through the stages of Weapon Transcendence, you will unlock two customizable slots: Slot II and Slot IV. Unlike standard Sigil slots, these traits are embedded directly into the weapon itself, freeing up valuable space in your main Sigil loadout.

### The Slot II Swappable Mechanic

The **Endless Ragnarok Slot II swappable** feature allows players to embed common offensive and defensive traits up to a maximum level of 25. These traits can be swapped freely at the blacksmith in Seedhollow using standard Refinement Sands and character-specific materials. The primary benefit of the Slot II slot is reaching critical stat caps (such as Critical Hit Rate or Attack) without wasting S-tier Sigil slots.

| Slot II Trait | Max Level | Effect at Max Level | Optimal For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Critical Hit Rate** | Level 25 | +25% Critical Hit Rate | All DPS Characters (Sandalphon, Narmaya) |
| **Attack** | Level 25 | +250 Flat Attack | Ranged DPS (Eustace, Rackam) |
| **Stamina** | Level 25 | Up to +50% Attack (based on high HP) | Melee DPS (Siegfried, Gallanza) |
| **Aegis** | Level 25 | +35% Maximum HP | Tank/Support (Vane, Maglielle) |

### The Slot IV Swappable Mechanic

The **Endless Ragnarok Slot IV swappable** feature is unlocked at Transcendence Stage 4 and maxes out at Level 10. This slot is reserved for high-impact utility, cooldown reduction, and rare combat modifiers. Because these traits are capped at Level 10, they are best used to supplement existing Sigil builds or to slot in rare traits like War Elemental or character-specific passives.

| Slot IV Trait | Max Level | Effect at Max Level | Optimal For |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **War Elemental** | Level 10 | Converts all attacks to the enemy's elemental weakness | Universal (Mandatory for Chaos Difficulty) |
| **Cascade** | Level 10 | Reduces skill cooldowns on hit by up to 1.5% | Skill-Spam Builds (Cagliostro, Io) |
| **Quick Cooldown** | Level 10 | Reduces skill cooldowns by 10% | Support/Shield Buffers (Maglielle) |
| **Dodge Payback** | Level 10 | Extends perfect dodge invincibility by 2 seconds | High-mobility DPS (Lancelot, Charlotta) |

To optimize your build, it is recommended to pair a Level 25 Critical Hit Rate trait in your **Endless Ragnarok Slot II swappable** slot with a Level 10 War Elemental trait in your **Endless Ragnarok Slot IV swappable** slot. This combination provides a massive baseline damage boost, allowing you to focus your remaining Sigil slots on [Master Traits pathing](/en/master-traits/master-traits-explained/) and character-specific S-tier Sigils.

## Slot EX Rewards and Weapon Aesthetic Effects

Reaching Stage 7 Transcendence unlocks the ultimate weapon upgrade: the Slot EX reward. The Slot EX slot is unique because it does not require manual slotting or upkeep; instead, it grants a permanent, weapon-type-specific passive buff that stacks with all other active Sigils and Master Traits. 

Alongside this power spike, players unlock unique **Endless Ragnarok weapon aesthetic effects**. These visual upgrades add a distinct glow, particle trail, or elemental aura to the weapon model, indicating to other players in crossplay lobbies that you have fully mastered that specific weapon.

       │

### Slot EX Passive Bonuses by Weapon Type

Each of the six weapon types (Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, Terminus) receives a specialized Slot EX bonus tailored to that weapon's original role.

| Weapon Type | Slot EX Passive Reward | **Endless Ragnarok Weapon Aesthetic Effects** |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Terminus** | +10% Damage Cap & +5% Damage Limit Break | Dark-purple cosmic aura with floating runes |
| **Ascension** | +15% Skill Damage & -5% Skill Cooldown | Radiant golden light with feather particles |
| **Defender** | +20% Max HP & +10% Defense | Shield-like blue crystalline barrier around the hilt |
| **Stinger** | +15% Critical Damage | Sharp red lightning crackling along the blade/barrel |
| **Executioner** | +15% Weak Point Damage | Dark red mist trailing behind weapon swings |
| **Stunner** | +25% Stun Power & +10% Link Temp Gain | Yellow kinetic sparks emitting during attacks |

These aesthetic effects can be toggled on or off in the weapon customization menu at the blacksmith. If you prefer the clean look of your base weapon but still want the Slot EX passive stats, you can disable the visual aura while maintaining the stat bonuses.

## Best Transcendence Paths for Top-Tier Characters

To make the most of your resources, it is essential to prioritize which characters and weapons you transcend first. Because materials like Beelzebub Jewels and Genesis Essences are rare drops from high-level Fatebreaker quests, focus on characters that benefit the most from the **Endless Ragnarok max level 7 weapon** stat boosts.

### Sandalphon (SS-Tier)
Sandalphon remains the strongest character in the meta due to his Supreme Primarch Mode and lightstep sword wave spam. 
*   **Target Weapon:** Terminus Weapon (Ain Soph Aur).
*   **Slot II Setup:** Critical Hit Rate Level 25 (to guarantee crits on sword waves).
*   **Slot IV Setup:** War Elemental Level 10 or Alpha/Beta/Gamma Sigil trait.
*   **Slot EX Benefit:** The +10% Damage Cap from the Terminus Slot EX allows Sandalphon to bypass standard damage ceilings during his Primarch transformation.

### Beatrix (New DLC - DPS)
Beatrix utilizes the Delta Clock mechanic, which rewards precise timing and high attack speed.
*   **Target Weapon:** Stinger Weapon (Sadistic Edge).
*   **Slot II Setup:** Stamina Level 25 (to boost baseline damage when maintaining high HP).
*   **Slot IV Setup:** Cascade Level 10 (to reduce the cooldown of her Delta Clock activation skills).
*   **Slot EX Benefit:** The +15% Critical Damage from the Stinger weapon type synergizes perfectly with her high critical hit rate, turning her into a burst-damage powerhouse.

### Maglielle (New DLC - Support/DPS)
Maglielle controls flying weapons to shield allies and deal damage from safety.
*   **Target Weapon:** Ascension Weapon (Claiomh Solais).
*   **Slot II Setup:** Aegis Level 25 (to increase survivability while channeling spells).
*   **Slot IV Setup:** Quick Cooldown Level 10 (to keep her shields and healing spells active).
*   **Slot EX Benefit:** The Ascension Slot EX passive reduces her skill cooldowns by an additional 5%, allowing near-permanent uptime on her protective barriers.

## Farming Strategies for Fatebreaker Quests and The Conflux

Unlocking the **Endless Ragnarok max level 7 weapon** upgrades requires a systematic approach to farming high-tier materials. You cannot rely solely on one game mode; you must balance co-op Fatebreaker raids with solo Conflux runs.

### Step 1: Farm Conflux Gateways for Splinters and Onyx
The Conflux is a solo-only roguelite mode that can be accessed via Seedhollow. It is the fastest source of Refinement Sands, Conflux Splinters, and Ragnarok Onyx.
*   **Strategy:** Select gateways that feature combat or rare monster rooms. Avoid treasure rooms if your goal is farming Transcendence materials, as combat rooms offer higher drop rates for Splinters.
*   **Run Time:** Aim for 20-minute runs, clearing up to Depth 15 for the best reward multipliers.

### Step 2: Clear Fatebreaker Quests on Chaos Difficulty
Fatebreaker quests are high-difficulty boss fights designed for coordinated groups. 
*   **Target Bosses:** Beelzebub ("Fall of the False God") and Ragnalia ("Heralds of Doom").
*   **Requirements:** Ensure your party has at least one player running a build with equipped Primals (such as Lucilius or Rolan) to trigger Primal Bursts during Link Time.
*   **Drops:** Beelzebub drops the Beelzebub Jewels required for Stage 7 Transcendence, while Ragnalia drops Astral Shards needed for Stages 4-6.

For more details on boss mechanics, refer to the [Beelzebub raid guide](/en/bosses/beelzebub-boss-guide/) and learn how to survive his wipe mechanics. ## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I transfer Slot EX passives between different weapons?
No. Slot EX passives are permanently tied to the specific weapon type they were unlocked on. For example, if you unlock Slot EX on Sandalphon's Terminus weapon, that passive only applies when that specific weapon is equipped. However, you can transfer the visual **Endless Ragnarok weapon aesthetic effects** to other weapons of the same type via the weapon skin customization menu.

### How do I change the traits in my Slot II and Slot IV swappable slots?
You can swap these traits at the blacksmith in Seedhollow or Tredame Sanctum. Swapping a trait requires a small fee of Refinement Sands and specific boss drops (such as Ragnalia cores or Astral Shards). The level of the trait remains at the maximum unlocked stage (up to Level 25 for Slot II and Level 10 for Slot IV) regardless of how many times you swap the trait.

### What is the fastest way to get an Endless Ragnarok max level 7 weapon?
The fastest path is to prioritize farming "Fatebreaker: Fall of the False God" in a coordinated online co-op group to secure Beelzebub Jewels. Combine this with daily runs of The Conflux to gather the necessary Conflux Splinters and Ragnarok Onyx. Avoid upgrading multiple weapons at once; focus all your resources on a single top-tier character's weapon (like Sandalphon's or Beatrix's Terminus weapon) until it reaches Stage 7.

### Do the aesthetic effects provide any gameplay bonuses?
No. The weapon aesthetic effects (such as the glowing runes and lightning trails) are purely cosmetic. All gameplay bonuses are derived from the Slot EX passive stats, the Slot II/IV swappable traits, and the increased weapon level cap (Level 220).
