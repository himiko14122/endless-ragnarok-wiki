## Core Mechanics and the Aureola System

Maglielle's combat loop centers on the **Aureola Gauge**, a unique resource bar located above her skill shortcuts. This gauge is divided into five segments, each representing a floating spectral blade. By executing basic attack combos and specific skills, Maglielle summons these blades to hover around her, automatically firing at enemies within range.

```
[ Aureola Gauge: ▢ ▢ ▢ ▢ ▢ ] -> Active Blades: Up to 5
  - Light Attack Combo: Generates 1 Blade on the 4th hit
  - Heavy Attack (Charge): Launches all active blades for burst damage
  - Support Skills: Consume active blades to enhance healing and shields
```

### Basic Attack Inputs and Gauge Generation

To maximize your damage and utility, you must understand how to quickly generate and expend your Aureola blades. Maglielle's light attack string consists of rapid-fire magic projectiles. The final hit of her four-hit basic combo summons one blade. Conversely, her heavy attack fires all currently summoned blades in a piercing fan pattern, dealing damage scaling with the number of blades consumed.

| Input Command | Action Name | Aureola Gauge Effect | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Light Attack (Combo x4)** | Spectral Needle | Generates +1 Blade | Ranged projectile string; final hit summons a floating dagger. |
| **Heavy Attack (Tap)** | Blade Release | Consumes All Blades | Launches active blades forward. Applies a 10% Defense Down debuff. |
| **Heavy Attack (Hold)** | Aureola Salvo | Consumes All Blades | Charges a piercing beam. Deals 150% increased damage at 5 blades. |
| **Air Light Attack** | Falling Shard | None | Quick downward projectile. Good for repositioning. |
| **Link Attack** | Harmonic Strike | Generates +3 Blades | Instantly fills the gauge and triggers a coordinated party strike. |

### Skill Synergies: Healing and Shields

Maglielle's active skills change properties based on how many Aureola blades are active. When casting **Endless Ragnarok Maglielle healing** spells or defensive barriers, having a full gauge increases the radius, duration, and potency of the effects.

*   **Aegis Wall**: Deploys a stationary dome shield. At base level, it blocks projectiles. If cast with 5 blades, it grants all allies inside the dome **Invincibility** for 5 seconds.
*   **Sacred Sanctuary**: Places a healing circle on the ground. It heals for a flat percentage of maximum HP every 1.5 seconds. Consuming blades increases the healing tick rate and extends the zone's duration.
*   **Verge Drive**: A high-damage offensive skill that consumes all blades to unleash a massive spinning sword vortex. This is her primary method for breaking enemy parts and staggering bosses.

## Master Traits and Skill Tree Paths

Upon reaching Level 100, Maglielle gains access to her Master Traits. These traits are divided into three distinct progression paths: **Insight**, **Essence**, and **Crux**. Unlocking these nodes requires Master Points (MSP) earned from level-up progression, Fatebreaker quests, and clearing runs in [The Conflux](/en/conflux/).

### The Three Master Trait Paths

1.  **Insight Path (Support & Healing Focus)**: This path enhances Maglielle's defensive utility. It reduces the cooldown of her barrier skills, increases the healing cap of her area-of-effect spells, and adds a Cleanse effect to her primary heal.
2.  **Essence Path (DPS & Blade Automation)**: This path focuses on her flying weapons. It allows blades to attack faster, increases their critical hit rate, and grants a chance to refund a blade when launching a heavy attack.
3.  **Crux Path (Utility & Link Time)**: This path boosts party-wide utility. It increases SBA (Skybound Art) gauge generation rates, enhances Link Tempests during Link Time, and grants Maglielle high-tier crowd control capabilities.

| Master Trait Path | Rank Perk 1 (Level 1) | Rank Perk 2 (Level 5) | Rank Style Upgrade (Level 10) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Insight** | +10% Healing Cap | Cleanse on Sacred Sanctuary | Aegis Wall grants Stout Heart to all allies |
| **Essence** | +15% Blade Auto-Attack DMG | 20% Blade Refund Chance | Aureola Salvo ignores 15% of enemy DEF |
| **Crux** | +8% SBA Gauge Gain | +15% Link Attack Damage | Link Time extends active blade duration by 8s |

To optimize your **Endless Ragnarok Maglielle support** capabilities, prioritize unlocking the Insight path up to Rank Style Upgrade first. This ensures your team can survive high-damage phases in Chaos-difficulty encounters, such as [The World](/en/bosses/the-world-boss-guide/) boss fight.

Creating an effective **Endless Ragnarok Maglielle build** requires balancing her offensive flying weapons with her support capabilities. Below are two highly optimized builds designed for endgame progression.

*   **Key Skills**: Aegis Wall, Sacred Sanctuary, Divine Grace, Verge Drive.

## Summon System Integration and Combat Strategy

The expansion introduces a revamped Summon System, allowing players to field powerful primal beasts in Chaos-difficulty quests. Maglielle plays a critical role in summon-focused strategies due to her rapid SBA generation and ability to protect the party during summon casting animations.

### Triggering Primal Bursts

In Endless Ragnarok, Lyria can summon primal beasts once the party builds up enough Link energy. When all four party members execute their Skybound Arts (SBA) during Link Time, it triggers a **Primal Burst**, summoning controllable entities like Lucilius or Rolan.

```
Combat Loop for Summon Activation:
1. Build Aureola Gauge -> 2. Use Aureola Rain to build SBA -> 3. Trigger Link Time -> 4. Chain 4 SBAs -> 5. Summon Lucilius / Rolan
```

To execute this effectively with Maglielle:
1.  **Maintain Blade Uptime**: Keep 5 blades active at all times. Their automatic attacks generate SBA gauge even while you are dodging boss attacks.
2.  **Cast Aegis Wall Before Chain**: Right before initiating the SBA chain, drop Aegis Wall. The invincibility buff carries over into the chain animation, protecting your allies from stray boss projectiles.
3.  **Positioning**: Stay at medium range. Maglielle's projectiles have travel time, but staying too far reduces the effectiveness of her healing circles.

For detailed information on upgrading your summons and maximizing their passive bonuses, refer to our comprehensive [Summon System Guide](/en/summons/summon-system-guide/).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I unlock Maglielle in Endless Ragnarok?
To unlock Maglielle, you must first complete the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue content. Once unlocked, you can redeem a Crewmate Card obtained from Fatebreaker quests or purchase her DLC pack directly from the storefront.

### Can Maglielle act as a primary DPS?
While Maglielle can deal respectable damage with a fully transcended Terminus weapon and the *Aureola's Authority* Sigil, her kit is fundamentally designed around support. She is best played as a hybrid DPS/Support rather than a pure damage dealer.

### Does Maglielle's healing scale with her Attack power?
Yes, **Endless Ragnarok Maglielle healing** skills scale with a percentage of her overall Attack power, up to a specific healing cap. Equipping Sigils like *Healing Cap V+* is necessary to maximize her healing potential in endgame content.

### What is the best summon to pair with Maglielle?
Rolan is the best summon to pair with Maglielle. Rolan's passive bonuses increase the defense and recovery speed of all party members standing inside active barrier zones, directly synergizing with Maglielle's Aegis Wall.
