## The Delta Clock Mechanic Explained

The core of playing Beatrix lies in managing the Delta Clock, a unique UI gauge displayed above her health bar. The Delta Clock continuously rotates between three distinct phases: **Nayle (Attack)**, **Orest (Defense)**, and **Zephyr (Recovery)**. Each phase lasts for a default duration of 8 seconds before automatically transitioning to the next in a clockwise cycle. 

```
   [ Nayle (Red) ]  -->  +30% ATK, +15% Damage Cap
          |
          v
   [ Orest (Blue) ] -->  +40% DEF, Stout Heart
          |
          v
  [ Zephyr (Yellow) ]-->  3% HP Drain, +20% Skill Haste
```

To play Beatrix optimally, you must manually "freeze" these phases using specific combat finishers or skills. Freezing a phase locks its active bonuses for 15 seconds, preventing the clock from rotating and allowing you to exploit specific stat boosts.

### Delta Clock Phases and Effects

| Phase | Color | Base Stat Bonuses | Optimal Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Nayle** | Red | +30% Attack, +15% Damage Cap, +10% Critical Hit Rate | Primary DPS windows, Link Time, and chain bursts. |
| **Orest** | Blue | +40% Defense, Stout Heart (Interrupt Resistance), -15% Damage Taken | Navigating boss overdrive phases, executing charge attacks. |
| **Zephyr** | Yellow | 3% HP Drain (up to 500 per hit), +20% Skill Cooldown Recovery | Quick recovery after taking damage, cycling skill cooldowns. |

### How to Freeze and Manipulate the Clock
1. **Combo Finishers:** Pressing the Heavy Attack button (Y on Xbox / Triangle on PlayStation) at the precise moment a normal attack combo ends will execute a Phase Lock Strike. The active phase of the Delta Clock at the moment of impact will freeze.
2. **Perfect Dodges and Parries:** Successfully executing a Perfect Dodge or a counter via her skill *Sleek Guard* instantly freezes the current phase and extends its duration to 20 seconds.
3. **Manual Cycling:** Beatrix's unique character action allows her to spend 10% of her SBA (Skybound Art) gauge to instantly advance the clock to the next phase, bypassing the natural 8-second wait.

## Beatrix's Skill Kit & Ability Breakdown

Selecting the right combination of Endless Ragnarok Beatrix skills is vital for maintaining uptime on her buffs and maximizing damage output. Her skills interact directly with the active state of the Delta Clock, altering their damage properties, cooldowns, or utility based on whether the clock is running or frozen.

### Key Active Skills

*   **Rising Blast (Cooldown: 18s):** A rapid, multi-hit forward slash. If executed during the **Nayle** phase, the final hit deals 50% extra damage and bypasses 20% of the target's defense. This is your primary damage-dealing skill.
*   **Sunlight Blade (Cooldown: 25s):** Beatrix charges forward, unleashing a fiery shockwave. Using this skill during the **Orest** phase grants Beatrix a barrier equal to 20% of her maximum HP for 10 seconds.
*   **Immortal Assault (Cooldown: 45s):** A high-damage physical slash that consumes the current Delta Clock phase. In exchange, Beatrix gains Invincibility for 6 seconds. The cooldown of this skill is reduced by 2 seconds for every second the Delta Clock remains frozen.
*   **Sleek Guard (Cooldown: 22s):** A counter-stance skill. If Beatrix is struck while in this stance, she parries the attack, deals massive Fire damage, and instantly freezes the current Delta Clock phase.

```
   [Enemy Attack] -> Activate Sleek Guard -> Counter Triggered -> Instant Delta Clock Freeze
```

## Optimal Endless Ragnarok Beatrix Build: The Crimson Tempest Setup

This Endless Ragnarok Beatrix build is designed for high-tier endgame content, specifically targeting Chaos-difficulty quests and deep runs in [The Conflux](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/). It maximizes her damage output during the **Nayle** phase while using S-tier Sigils to bypass standard damage caps.

### Weapon Choice: Embrasque Transcendence
For this build, your weapon of choice must be the **Embrasque** (Terminus Type), upgraded via Weapon Transcendence to at least Level 10. This unlocks Slot II (Max Level 25 traits) and Slot IV (Max Level 10 traits), allowing you to slot in critical offensive perks.

*   **Slot II Trait:** Critical Hit Rate (Max Level 25)
*   **Slot IV Trait:** Warpath (Max Level 10)

### Recommended Sigil Setup

To push Beatrix to her absolute limit, you must run a combination of character-specific Sigils and the expansion's new S-tier Sigils.

| Sigil Slot | Sigil Name | Tier | Key Effects |

### Step-by-Step Combat Rotation
1. **Initiation:** Start the fight by using manual cycle (spending SBA gauge) or basic combos to rotate the Delta Clock to the **Nayle (Red)** phase.
2. **The Freeze:** Execute a basic combo ending with a Heavy Attack Phase Lock Strike to freeze the Nayle phase, activating the +30% ATK and +15% Damage Cap bonuses.
3. **Burst Phase:** Immediately fire off **Rising Blast** followed by **Sunlight Blade**. The frozen Nayle phase ensures both skills hit their maximum possible damage cap.
4. **Defensive Pivot:** If the boss prepares a major AoE attack, use **Sleek Guard** to counter. This will refresh your freeze duration. If Sleek Guard is on cooldown, use **Immortal Assault** to consume the phase and gain 6 seconds of Invincibility to ride out the attack.
5. **Cooldown Recovery:** If your skills are on cooldown and your health is low, rotate the clock to **Zephyr (Yellow)** and freeze it to rapidly regain HP and slash your skill cooldowns.

## Master Traits and Weapon Transcendence Progression

Unlocking Beatrix's full potential requires progressing through her Master Traits paths after she reaches Level 100. These traits are divided into three distinct paths: **Insight**, **Essence**, and **Crux**.

```
               [ Level 100 Beatrix ]
             /          |          \
            /           |           \
     [ Insight ]    [ Essence ]    [ Crux ]
     (DPS/Crit)    (Utility/CD)   (Def/SBA)
```

### Master Trait Paths for Beatrix
*   **Insight Path:** Focuses on boosting Critical Hit Rate, Critical Damage, and physical attack power. Unlocking Rank 3 of the Insight path grants Beatrix the *Embrasque's Edge* perk, which increases her physical damage cap by an additional 10% when her health is below 50%.
*   **Essence Path:** Focuses on utility, reducing skill cooldowns and increasing the duration of frozen Delta Clock phases. The Rank 3 perk, *Clockwork Master*, extends all frozen phases by 3 seconds.
*   **Crux Path:** Focuses on defensive stats, SBA gauge generation, and Link Temp efficiency. The Rank 3 perk, *Resilient Soldier*, grants Beatrix Stout Heart permanently during the Orest phase, freeing up a Sigil slot.

To gather the high-grade materials required for Weapon Transcendence and Master Traits, players must farm [Fatebreaker quests](/en/endgame/fatebreaker-quests-guide/). These high-difficulty encounters drop items like *Ragnarok Decrystallization Cores* and *Fatebreaker Emblems*, which are mandatory for upgrading Embrasque past level 100.

## Combat Strategies and Team Synergy

Beatrix excels in teams that can control the battlefield, allowing her to stay stationary and execute her full high-damage combos without interruption. When tackling endgame bosses like *Ragnalia* or *Seofon*, coordination with your team's support and crowd control is vital.

### Boss-Specific Strategies

*   **Beelzebub:** During Beelzebub's black hole phase, use the **Orest (Blue)** phase for the defense boost and Stout Heart effect. This prevents Beatrix from being pulled in and interrupted while charging her heavy attacks.
*   **The World:** The World frequently shifts elements and teleports. Use **Sleek Guard** to counter its teleportation strikes. A successful counter will freeze your active phase, allowing you to instantly retaliate with **Rising Blast** when the boss reappears.

### Team Synergies

Beatrix pairs exceptionally well with characters who can lock down enemies or provide continuous healing, allowing her to run low-health DPS setups safely.

1.  **Sandalphon:** As the undisputed SS-tier character in the current meta (see our [Sandalphon Guide](/en/characters/sandalphon-guide/)), Sandalphon provides unmatched utility. His ability to inflict Slow and lock down bosses gives Beatrix the perfect window to freeze her **Nayle** phase and execute her full DPS rotation without fear of retaliation.
2.  **Cagliostro:** Cagliostro provides critical attack and defense buffs, but more importantly, her healing allows Beatrix to play aggressively. The synergy between Cagliostro's Phantasmagoria and Beatrix's Delta Clock buffs allows the party to hit the maximum damage cap consistently.
3.  **Maglielle:** As a new DLC support character, Maglielle provides flying shields and barriers. This allows Beatrix to maintain her low-HP damage bonuses from the *Embrasque's Edge* Master Trait without risking getting one-shot on Chaos difficulty.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I unlock Beatrix in Endless Ragnarok?
To unlock Beatrix, you must first complete the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue. Once completed, you can exchange a *Crewmate Card* obtained from the new DLC quests at the Knickknack Shack in Seedhollow.

### Can I run Beatrix as a tank using the Orest phase?
While the Orest phase provides +40% DEF and Stout Heart, Beatrix is fundamentally a DPS character. The defensive phase should be used to survive major boss mechanics or to execute charged attacks safely, not to act as a primary tank. For dedicated tanking, characters like Vane or Siegfried are much better suited.

### What is the fastest way to farm materials for Beatrix's Weapon Transcendence?
The fastest method is running gateways in **The Conflux** solo mode. Look for gateways in Seedhollow or Tredame Sanctum. Focus on clearing combat and rare monster rooms, as they provide the highest yield of enhancement materials per 20-minute run.

### Does Beatrix's Delta Clock buff stack with regular Sigil buffs?
Yes. The attack and defense bonuses from the Delta Clock are multiplicative and stack with standard Sigils like *Attack Power V* or *Defense V*. However, they are still subject to the global damage cap, which is why running *War Elemental+* and *Gamma* Sigils is necessary to see the full benefit of the Nayle phase.
