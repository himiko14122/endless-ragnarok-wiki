## Core Abilities and Support Mechanics

Cagliostro's kit revolves around alchemy, allowing her to transmute spikes, healing circles, and defensive wards. Her unique mechanic is **Collapse**, a charged attack that can be chained at the end of any basic attack combo. Releasing a fully charged Collapse not only deals respectable Earth damage but also reduces the cooldowns of her active skills if her character-specific Sigils are equipped.

### Key Support Skills

*   **Phantasmagoria**: The cornerstone of any **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro support** build. It applies a party-wide 30% Attack, 30% Defense, and 15% Critical Hit Rate buff. This buff lasts for 30 seconds and has a base cooldown of 90 seconds, which can be significantly reduced through combos.
*   **Revive**: Casts a green rune on the ground. Any ally defeated within its radius is instantly revived with 50% HP and a temporary defense boost. If cast on living allies, it grants them the Auto-Revive status effect, protecting them from the next fatal blow.
*   **Reinforce**: A large-area healing spell that restores HP based on Cagliostro's maximum HP and Healing Cap. It also removes debuffs if upgraded via her Master Traits.
*   **Pain Train**: Cagliostro summons a spiked block and charges forward. This is her primary movement and stun-building tool. It helps close the distance to bosses while building the stun gauge rapidly to trigger Link Attacks.
*   **Mimic**: A defensive maneuver where Cagliostro leaves a straw doll decoy to draw enemy aggro while teleporting backward. This skill cleanses all active debuffs from her and grants brief invincibility frames.

To maximize her support output, players must master the combo flow. Executing a standard Light-Light-Light-Heavy combo allows Cagliostro to transition instantly into a Level 3 Collapse charge. Releasing this charge at the correct moment ensures her Phantasmagoria buff has nearly 100% uptime.

## Top Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro Build for End-Game Support

Building Cagliostro for the endgame requires balancing her support duties with enough damage capability to contribute to the team's overall DPS cap. In Endless Ragnarok, relying solely on healing will drag out fights, leading to boss enrage timers. The optimal setup is a Support-Hybrid build.

### Weapon Selection: Zosimos (Terminus Weapon)
The ultimate weapon for Cagliostro is **Zosimos**, her Terminus Weapon. It must be upgraded via [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/) to reach its maximum potential. Prior to obtaining Zosimos, the **Sacred Codex** (Ascension Weapon) is the best alternative, providing a massive boost to HP and healing caps.

### Optimal Sigil Setup
To run this build effectively, you need a combination of character-specific Sigils and general utility Sigils. The expansion introduces Sigil+ variants that allow for secondary traits.

*   **Founder's Strategy+**: Cagliostro's unique Sigil. It reduces her skill cooldowns by 3% whenever she hits an enemy with a fully charged Collapse attack.
*   **Founder's Truth+**: Increases the charge speed of Collapse by 20% and increases its damage cap by 50%.
*   **War Elemental+**: Converts all of Cagliostro's attacks into the element the target is weak against, ensuring constant maximum damage.
*   **Gamma Sigil (V+)**: Obtained from high-level Fatebreaker quests. It provides a shield to the entire party whenever a Skybound Art (SBA) is activated.
*   **Quick Cooldown V+ & Cascade V+**: Essential for keeping Phantasmagoria and Revive off cooldown.
*   **Potion Hoarder V+**: Provides extra blue potions for group heals and green potions for self-sustain.

## Master Traits and Weapon Transcendence Progression

With the release of the Endless Ragnarok expansion, characters can progress beyond Level 100 using the **Master Traits** system. These traits are divided into three distinct paths: **Insight**, **Essence**, and **Crux**. Unlocking these paths requires materials gathered from [The Conflux](/en/conflux/) and Fatebreaker quests.

### Master Trait Paths for Cagliostro

1.  **Insight Path (Support & Healing)**: Unlocks upgrades to Cagliostro's healing caps and adds a debuff cleanse to her Reinforce skill. The Rank 3 Perk, *Alchemical Grace*, reduces the casting time of her Revive skill by 50%.
2.  **Essence Path (Combat & Charge Speed)**: Focuses on improving Collapse. The Rank 3 Perk, *Transmutation Speed*, grants Cagliostro temporary Stout Heart (stagger immunity) while charging Collapse.
3.  **Crux Path (SBA & Link Utility)**: Enhances Link Temp generation and Skybound Art damage. The Rank 3 Perk, *Ouroboros Link*, increases the party's Link Gauge gain by 10% whenever Cagliostro participates in a Link Attack.

### Weapon Transcendence Priorities
Once you unlock Weapon Transcendence for the Terminus Weapon Zosimos, you can upgrade it up to Level 200. This unlocks swappable traits in Slot II and Slot IV.

*   **Slot II Trait**: Roll for **Critical Hit Rate (Max 25)**. This allows you to free up a Sigil slot on your grid for more utility or damage cap Sigils.
*   **Slot IV Trait**: Roll for **Skill Cooldown Reduction (Max 10)**. This stacks with Quick Cooldown and Cascade, allowing Phantasmagoria to be recast almost immediately after its duration expires.

## Advanced Combat Strategies and Boss Tactics

Playing Cagliostro in Chaos-difficulty quests requires a shift in mindset. You are not just a healer; you are the tempo-keeper of the fight. Your actions dictate when the team can push for damage and when they need to play defensively.

### Managing the Beelzebub Boss Fight
During the [Beelzebub boss fight](/en/bosses/beelzebub-boss-guide/), the boss will frequently cast *Chaos Calamity*, a wide-area attack that applies a defense-down debuff and deals lethal damage to non-tank characters. 

1.  **Pre-Buffing**: Always cast Phantasmagoria right before Beelzebub enters his overdrive phase. The 30% Defense buff is crucial for helping your allies survive his initial shockwaves.
2.  **Revive Placement**: Do not wait for allies to die to use Revive. Place the Revive circle in the center of the arena during Beelzebub's transition phase. This grants the party Auto-Revive, allowing them to ignore one-hit mechanics while maintaining their DPS uptime.
3.  **Collapse Animation Canceling**: Cagliostro's Collapse charge can be held while dodging. If Beelzebub targets you with a red indicator, begin charging Collapse, dodge through the attack, and immediately release the charge. This maintains your damage output without resetting your combo chain.

### Integrating the Summon System
In Chaos difficulty, Lyria can summon primal beasts once the party triggers Link Time. When your team coordinates their Skybound Arts to achieve a 100% Link Gauge, Cagliostro should immediately drop her Phantasmagoria buff before participating in the chain. 

Once Link Time is active, summon **Lucilius** or **Rolan**. The passive bonuses from equipped summons, combined with Cagliostro's Phantasmagoria, will push the party's damage output far past the standard damage cap, allowing you to melt boss health bars during break phases.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is Cagliostro preferred over Maglielle for support?
While Maglielle is excellent at mitigating incoming projectile damage with her barriers, she lacks a direct revive mechanic. In Chaos difficulty, where bosses use unblockable, high-damage area attacks, Cagliostro's ability to grant Auto-Revive and instantly resurrect players without consuming the quest's critical gauge makes her the superior safety net.

### Does Phantasmagoria's attack buff stack with the Captain's Rage IV?
No, major attack buffs of the same type do not stack; the game will apply the highest value buff (Phantasmagoria's 30% takes priority over Rage IV's 25%). However, the Critical Hit Rate and Defense buffs from Phantasmagoria will still apply, making it highly efficient even in parties with other buffing characters.

### What is the fastest way to farm materials for Zosimos Transcendence?
The fastest source of Weapon Transcendence materials is completing Fatebreaker quests on Chaos difficulty and running [The Conflux](/en/conflux/) gateways in Seedhollow. Deeper depths in the Conflux provide higher drop rates for the specific elemental essences and weapon shards required for Level 150+ upgrades.
