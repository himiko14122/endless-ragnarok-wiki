## Endless Ragnarok Beelzebub Attack Patterns and Phases

Beelzebub's combat loop is defined by high-velocity teleportation, lingering dark energy pools, and massive screen-clearing ultimate moves. Memorizing the **Endless Ragnarok Beelzebub attack patterns** is mandatory, as mistiming a single dodge on Chaos difficulty will result in an immediate knock-out.

### Phase 1: The Primarch's Fall (100% - 80% HP)

During this opening phase, Beelzebub tests your basic positioning and reaction times. He relies heavily on physical sword strikes and tracking projectiles.

*   **Black Flies:** Beelzebub summons homing dark orbs that track the target player. *Dodge Timing:* Wait until the orbs are roughly two character-lengths away, then dodge forward-right to slip past their tracking arc. Do not run in straight lines, or the trailing orbs will hit your teammates.
*   **Langue de Chat:** A rapid three-strike sword combo that concludes with a forward shockwave. *Counter:* Do not try to block the final hit; its stun value will break your guard instantly. Instead, use a dodge with invincibility frames (such as Lancelot's Shimmer or Sandalphon's lightstep) to slide through the shockwave.
*   **Shadow Wave:** Beelzebub slams his wings into the ground, sending out a circular shockwave across the entire arena. *Counter:* Jump over the wave rather than dodging, as the active frames of the wave last longer than a standard dodge roll's invincibility window.

### Phase 2: Overdrive & The Great Void (80% - 40% HP)

When Beelzebub enters Overdrive, he gains a defensive barrier that reduces all incoming damage by 50% unless shattered by Link Attacks or high-stun skills.

*   **Karma:** Beelzebub teleports to the center and pulls all players toward him while summoning concentric rings of dark energy. *Strategy:* Run to the outer edge of the arena and spam dodge to resist the pull. Characters with high mobility, like [Sandalphon](/en/characters/sandalphon-guide/), can use their movement skills to escape the pull zone.
*   **Anthem:** A series of falling meteors targeted at each player. *Strategy:* Keep moving in a circle around the perimeter. Do not run into your teammates' paths. If a teammate is downed, do not attempt to revive them until this move concludes.
*   **Chaos Edge:** Beelzebub fires multiple dark lasers in a fan pattern. These lasers leave lingering lines on the ground that explode after a 2-second delay. Avoid standing on the glowing purple lines.

### Phase 3: Chaos Legion (40% - 0% HP)

This is the ultimate DPS check of the fight. Beelzebub flies to the sky and channels **Chaos Legion**, a wipe mechanic that will instantly fail the quest if the casting bar fills up.

*   **Chaos Legion:** You must deplete his shield bar within 30 seconds. This is the optimal time to trigger your Summon System, calling upon Lucilius or Rolan to deal massive stagger damage.
*   **Megiddo:** Giant fireballs rain down, creating lingering lava pools that inflict the Burn status effect. Players must navigate these pools while continuing to damage the boss.

## Build Optimization: Sigils, Summons, and Weapon Transcendence

Understanding the **Endless Ragnarok Beelzebub weakness** is less about elemental matching and more about optimizing your build to bypass his high defense. Because Beelzebub is non-elemental, equipping the **War Elemental+** Sigil is mandatory. This Sigil forces all your attacks to deal elemental superiority damage, granting a flat 20% damage boost that ignores standard damage caps.

To survive Beelzebub's hard-hitting attacks on Chaos difficulty, you must also utilize the new Summon System. Players can equip up to four summons, but only one can be active at a time. For this fight, equipping **Lucilius** as your primary summon is highly recommended; his passive bonuses boost your maximum HP and skill cooldown speed, while his active summon deals massive stun damage to break Beelzebub's Overdrive.

Furthermore, players should push their weapons through Weapon Transcendence to at least Level 10. This unlocks Slot II and Slot IV traits, allowing you to slot in extra Damage Cap and Stout Heart traits. You can farm the necessary materials for Weapon Transcendence by running [The Conflux](/en/conflux/) roguelite mode, which serves as the fastest source of high-tier enhancement materials.

## Step-by-Step Strategy: Endless Ragnarok How to Beat Beelzebub

To secure victory against Beelzebub, follow this structured tactical plan:

### Step 1: The Opening Salvo (100% - 85% HP)
Do not use your Skybound Arts (SBA) immediately. Save them. Focus on building your Link Level by performing link attacks whenever Beelzebub is staggered. Use standard combos and keep your distance when he begins his **Langue de Chat** sword sweeps.

### Step 2: Surviving the First Overdrive (85% HP)
As soon as Beelzebub enters Overdrive, he will cast **Karma**. Run to the edges of the map immediately. If you have Vane in your party, have him deploy **Rampart** to shield the team from the pull effect and falling meteors. If you do not have Vane, spam your dodge roll outwards to resist the vacuum pull.

### Step 3: Breaking the Shield
Once the pull ends, unleash your high-stun skills. If your Link Level is at 100%, trigger **Link Time** now. This slows down Beelzebub and allows your team to bypass his defensive barrier rapidly. Aim for his back to deal extra backstab damage.

### Step 4: The Chaos Legion Phase (40% HP)
When Beelzebub teleports to the center for Chaos Legion, immediately summon **Lucilius**. Have all four party members chain their SBAs. This triggers a **Primal Burst**, which instantly depletes Beelzebub's shield bar and knocks him into a vulnerable, downed state for 10 seconds.

### Step 5: The Final Burn (20% - 0% HP)
Beelzebub will become highly aggressive, spamming **Black Flies** and **Langue de Chat** simultaneously. Do not get greedy. Stick to safe poke damage, prioritize dodging, and let your ranged characters (like Eustace or Sandalphon) finish him off from a safe distance.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### What is the best character to solo Beelzebub?
Sandalphon is currently the best character for soloing Beelzebub due to his Supreme Primarch Mode, which allows him to maintain high DPS while staying mobile enough to dodge Beelzebub's rapid teleportation attacks. His lightstep dodge provides generous invincibility frames, making it easier to survive Chaos difficulty.

### How do I unlock the Beelzebub quest in Endless Ragnarok?
You must first complete the Proud difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue. After completing the Epilogue, clear five Fatebreaker quests to unlock "Chaos's Descent" on both Proud and Chaos difficulties.

### Which summon is best for the Beelzebub boss fight?
The **Lucilius** summon is the best choice. His active ability deals massive stagger damage, which is crucial for breaking Beelzebub's shield during the Chaos Legion phase, and his passive boosts your survivability by increasing your maximum HP.

### Does Beelzebub have an elemental weakness?
No, Beelzebub does not have an inherent elemental weakness. To maximize your damage, you must equip the **War Elemental+** Sigil, which converts your attacks into elemental superiority damage against him, effectively functioning as his weakness.
