## Streamlining The Conflux: The Roguelite Farm Loop

The Conflux is a solo roguelite mode accessed via gateways in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. It serves as the fastest source of enhancement materials, Sigil upgrade components, and Weapon Transcendence tokens. A standard run takes approximately 20 minutes, but you can reduce this to 12–15 minutes by selecting the correct paths and room types.

When entering The Conflux, your primary goal is to reach the deepest depths as quickly as possible. Deeper depths yield higher-tier rewards, including Luster Crystals and Transcendence Splinters. Before entering a new area, you must choose a temporary power-up. Prioritize Cooldown Reduction, Skill Damage Boosts, and SBA (Skybound Art) Gauge Gain to clear combat rooms faster.

| Room Type | Primary Rewards | Priority Level | Strategic Value |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Rare Monster Room** | Transcendence Splinters, Luster Crystals | **High** | Essential for Weapon Transcendence; skip other rooms if this is active. |
| **Combat Room** | Master Points (MSP), Sigil EXP | **Medium** | Good for leveling up Master Traits; try to clear under 90 seconds. |
| **Treasure Room** | Curios, Gold Dalia Badges, Knickknack Vouchers | **Medium** | Safe room with no combat; ideal for fast-forwarding to deeper depths. |
| **Rest Area** | HP Recovery, Temporary Attack Buffs | **Low** | Only choose if your active character is below 30% HP. |

To achieve maximum **Endless Ragnarok farming optimization** in The Conflux, use high-mobility characters like Sandalphon or Lancelot. Sandalphon's Supreme Primarch Mode allows you to spam lightstep sword waves, clearing entire waves of enemies instantly. If you are using a new DLC character like Beatrix, utilize her Delta Clock mechanic to freeze enemy cooldowns, allowing you to bypass mechanics in combat rooms. For a detailed breakdown of room layouts, check out our [Conflux Progression Guide](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/).

## Weapon Transcendence & Fatebreaker Quest Priority

Weapon Transcendence allows you to push fully enhanced weapons beyond their standard limits. This system applies to all six weapon types: Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, and Terminus. Upgrading these weapons unlocks swappable traits—up to level 25 for Slot II and level 10 for Slot IV—alongside Slot EX rewards and unique visual effects.

To upgrade these weapons, you must farm Fatebreaker quests. These high-difficulty encounters drop specific materials required for each Transcendence level. Because these quests feature bosses like Beelzebub and Ragnalia, running them with an unoptimized party will result in failed runs and wasted time.

### Step-by-Step Fatebreaker Farming Loop
1. **Clear the Prerequisite**: You must complete the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue and access Fatebreaker missions.
2. **Target Specific Bosses**: Focus on "Fatebreaker: The Wings of Rebellion" for Beelzebub drops, which are required for Terminus Weapon Transcendence.
3. **Equip the Right Summons**: Ensure your party has at least one player carrying a controllable Lucilius or Rolan summon to interrupt wipe mechanics.
4. **Break Specific Parts**: Focus attacks on Beelzebub's wings or Ragnalia's horn to secure bonus drop rewards upon quest completion.

| Weapon Type | Key Transcendence Material | Primary Source | Recommended Character |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Terminus** | Chaos Core, Void Splinter | Fatebreaker: Beelzebub | Sandalphon, Vaseraga |
| **Ascension** | Hallowed Bloom, Astral Tears | Fatebreaker: Ragnalia | Cagliostro, Maglielle |
| **Stinger** | Sharp Sentinel Claw | Fatebreaker: The World | Charlotta, Zeta |
| **Defender** | Fortitude Crystal (Giga) | Conflux Depths 50+ | Vane, Siegfried |

Using these **Endless Ragnarok time saving tips**, you should focus on upgrading your Terminus weapon first. The raw damage scaling provided by Terminus Transcendence far outclasses the defensive bonuses of Defender or Ascension weapons. For more details on weapon paths, refer to the [Weapon Transcendence Guide](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/).

## Master Traits Optimization: Insight, Essence, and Crux Paths

Unlocking Master Traits at Level 100 is a core milestone for all 28 characters. Each character features three distinct paths: Insight (focused on cooldowns and utility), Essence (focused on raw damage and critical stats), and Crux (focused on defense and SBA generation). Each path contains three unique Rank Perks and four Rank Style upgrades.

Farming Master Points (MSP) to unlock these traits can be slow if you do not target specific quests. The most efficient way to farm MSP is by running the "Slimepede" quest on Maniac difficulty or by clearing Combat Rooms in The Conflux. 

```
[Level 100 Character] 
       │
       ├──► Insight Path (Utility & Cooldowns) ──► Rank Perks 1-3 ──► Style Upgrades
       ├──► Essence Path (Offense & Crit)     ──► Rank Perks 1-3 ──► Style Upgrades
       └──► Crux Path (Defense & SBA)         ──► Rank Perks 1-3 ──► Style Upgrades
```

To implement effective **Endless Ragnarok farming optimization**, do not distribute your MSP evenly. Maximize the Essence path first for your primary DPS characters (such as Narmaya or Zeta) to hit damage caps faster. For support characters like Maglielle, focus on the Insight path to maintain high uptime on her shields and barrier skills. You can read more about trait combinations in our [Master Traits Guide](/en/master-traits/master-traits-explained/).

## Summon System Integration for Chaos Difficulty

The Summon System is restricted to Chaos-difficulty quests and higher. Lyria can summon primal beasts in combat to turn the tide of battle. Each player can bring up to four summons, but only one can be active on the field at any given time. Controllable summons like Lucilius and Rolan provide massive active damage, while passive summons offer stat boosts.

To maximize your damage output, coordinate your party's Skybound Arts (SBA). When all four party members activate their SBA during Link Time, it triggers a Primal Burst. This mechanic inflicts massive elemental damage and stuns the boss, allowing you to bypass phase transitions on bosses like The World.

| Summon | Active Ability | Passive Bonus | Ideal Scenario |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lucilius** | Paradise Lost (AoE Light Damage + Slow) | +15% Damage Cap | DPS phase during Link Time |
| **Rolan** | Sacred Shield (Party Invincibility 10s) | +20% Defense | Survival during Beelzebub's Chaos Legion |
| **Proto-Bahamut** | Supernova (Destroys enemy shields) | +10% Critical Hit Rate | Breaking Ragnalia's elemental shields |
| **Tiamat Omega** | Cyclone (Group heal + Regen) | +15% Skill Cooldown Speed | High-damage uptime support |

To get the most out of your summons, ensure that your party members do not activate their summons simultaneously. Staggering your summon activations ensures you maintain active passive buffs throughout the entirety of a Chaos-difficulty quest. For summon locations and unlock requirements, see the [Summon System Mechanics](/en/summons/summon-system-guide/) page.

## Sigil Synthesis and Loadout Builds

Sigils are the core of your character's build. In the Endless Ragnarok expansion, S-tier sigils like Warpath+, War Elemental+, and Character-Specific Sigils dominate the meta. A-tier sigils such as Alpha, Beta, and Gamma V+ provide essential cooldown and damage cap increases.

To farm these efficiently, utilize the Knickknack Shack's Sigil Transmutation system. Do not waste time manually farming low-tier quests for specific V+ sigils. Instead, run high-yield quests like "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to collect high-tier materials, trade them for Knickknack Vouchers, and perform Level 3 Transmutations.

```
[High-Yield Quests] ──► [Excess Materials] ──► [Knickknack Vouchers] ──► [Level 3 Transmutations] ──► [S-Tier Sigils]
```

### Essential Sigil Setup for Endgame Grinding
* **War Elemental+**: Converts all attacks to the enemy's elemental weakness. Essential for all DPS characters.
* **Character-Specific+**: Combines both unique character traits into a single slot.
* **Alpha V+ / Beta V+**: Increases SBA damage and reduces cooldowns when triggering SBA.
* **Damage Cap V+**: Ensure you have at least four of these equipped to avoid hitting the default damage ceiling.

By focusing on these specific setups, you will save hours of grinding. For a comprehensive list of drop rates, check the official database on the [Cygames Official Website](https://www.cygames.co.jp/en/).

## End-Game Boss Speedrun Strategies (Beelzebub, The World, Ragnalia)

Speedrunning endgame bosses is the ultimate test of your build. Beelzebub, The World, and Ragnalia feature mechanics that can wipe an unprepared party instantly. To farm them efficiently, you must skip these phases by dealing burst damage during specific windows.

When fighting Beelzebub, he will cast "Chaos Legion" at 70% and 30% HP. This attack covers the arena in void zones and deals lethal damage. To bypass this, save your party's SBA chain until he reaches 75% HP. Triggering a full four-chain SBA during Link Time will stun Beelzebub, skipping the first Chaos Legion phase entirely.

For Ragnalia, utilize characters with high stun values like Vaseraga or Gallanza. Breaking Ragnalia's elemental shields quickly prevents him from summoning Heralds of Doom, which drag out the fight. If you are playing with cross-platform friends across PS5, PS4, Switch 2, or PC, ensure your communication is synchronized to land your SBA chains at the exact moment the boss enters their vulnerability window. Learn more about these encounters in our [Chaos Boss Strategies](/en/bosses/chaos-difficulty-boss-tips/).

## FAQ: Endless Ragnarok Efficiency

### How do I unlock Chaos difficulty quests?
To unlock Chaos difficulty, you must complete the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue. Once you complete the subsequent Epilogue story quests, Chaos difficulty will become available at the Quest Counter.

### What is the fastest way to farm Weapon Transcendence materials?
The fastest way to farm these materials is by clearing Depth 50+ in The Conflux for general tokens, and target-farming specific Fatebreaker quests (such as Beelzebub or Ragnalia) in a coordinated multiplayer lobby.

### Should I prioritize Master Traits or Weapon Transcendence first?
Prioritize Master Traits first. Unlocking the Rank Perks on the Essence and Insight paths provides a more immediate power spike for your character than the initial levels of Weapon Transcendence.

### Can I play crossplay with players on other platforms to farm faster?
Yes, Endless Ragnarok supports full cross-platform play across PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Switch 2, and PC (Steam). You can create lobbies with players on any of these platforms to coordinate your farming sessions.
