## The Core Endgame Loop: Conflux vs. Fatebreaker vs. Chaos Quests

Endgame progression in Endless Ragnarok is split across three primary activities, each serving a distinct purpose in your character development. Understanding where to spend your time prevents the common pitfall of farming the wrong content for materials that could be obtained faster elsewhere. 

The Conflux is a solo roguelite mode accessible via gateways in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. Runs take approximately 20 minutes and serve as the fastest source of general enhancement materials, such as Fortitude Crystals (L), Refinement Sand, and basic Master Trait scrolls. Because rooms are randomly generated, you must adapt your build on the fly, but the payout in volume of materials is unmatched.

Fatebreaker quests are high-difficulty boss fights designed specifically to gate Weapon Transcendence. These quests pit you against challenging encounters, such as the dual boss fight against Seofon and Tweyen, or the destructive Ragnalia. These fights do not reward general materials; instead, they drop highly specific materials like Fatebreaker Shards, Astral Tears, and weapon-type essences.

Chaos-difficulty quests represent the pinnacle of team-based combat. This is where you farm S-tier Sigils (such as Warpath+ and War Elemental+) and materials required to upgrade your Summon System. In Chaos difficulty, boss attacks can easily one-hit weaker characters, making proper defensive sigils and team synergy mandatory.

| Activity Type | Primary Location | Key Drops | Average Run Time | Difficulty Tier |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **The Conflux** | Seedhollow / Tredame Gateways | Master Trait Scrolls, Fortitude Crystals, Refinement Sand | 18–22 minutes | Medium to High (Scales with Depth) |
| **Fatebreaker Quests** | Quest Counter (Fatebreaker Tab) | Fatebreaker Shards, Astral Tears, Transcendence Essences | 5–8 minutes | High |
| **Chaos Quests** | Quest Counter (Chaos Difficulty) | S-tier Sigils, Primal Cores, Genesis Feathers | 8–12 minutes | Very High (One-hit mechanics) |

To optimize your playtime, follow a structured loop: run The Conflux to gather the raw materials needed to level your weapons to 150, push Fatebreaker quests to transcend those weapons, and finally farm Chaos quests to optimize your Sigil builds and summon loadouts.

## Weapon Transcendence Material Farming Routes

Weapon Transcendence allows you to push your fully enhanced weapons beyond their standard limits, unlocking customizable trait slots (Slot II up to level 25, Slot IV up to level 10) and powerful Slot EX rewards. This system applies to all six weapon types: Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, and Terminus. However, the materials required for this process are gated behind high-difficulty encounters.

To begin **Endless Ragnarok weapon farming**, you must target specific Fatebreaker quests. The most critical materials are *Fatebreaker Cores* and *Transcendence Essences*. These materials are drop-restricted to specific bosses. For instance, the quest "Fatebreaker: Twin Blades of Fate" (featuring Seofon and Tweyen) is the optimal source for Ascension and Terminus weapon materials, while "Fatebreaker: Heralds of Doom" (featuring Ragnalia) drops the components needed for Defender and Executioner weapons.

When targeting Seofon and Tweyen, your party composition must account for Tweyen's ranged crowd control. Bring characters with high gap-closers or ranged capabilities. Eustace and Beatrix excel here; Eustace can lock down Seofon from afar while Beatrix utilizes her Delta Clock mechanic to accelerate her DPS and shred Tweyen's shield. If you are playing solo, Sandalphon in Supreme Primarch Mode is highly recommended, as his lightstep sword wave spam can hit both bosses simultaneously, breaking their parts for extra drop chests.

For Ragnalia, the fight is a DPS race against his doom timer. You will need high-stagger characters like Vaseraga or Siegfried to consistently interrupt his casting phases. Breaking Ragnalia's tail and horns increases the drop rate of Fatebreaker Cores by 15%.

## High-Efficiency Sigil Farming Strategies

Securing S-tier Sigils is the most time-consuming aspect of the endgame. The expansion introduces new Sigils with expanded traits, making older farming routes obsolete. Your main targets are Warpath+, War Elemental+, and Character Specific Sigils (which now feature dual-trait rolls, such as Beatrix's *Delta Clock + Critical Damage*).

The absolute **Endless Ragnarok best quests to farm** for these Sigils are "The World's End" (featuring The World) and "The False Prophet" (featuring Beelzebub). Beelzebub, in particular, is the exclusive source for War Elemental+ Sigils, which bypass elemental resistance entirely and grant a flat 20% damage increase against all foes.

To maximize your efficiency during **Endless Ragnarok sigil farming endgame**, run these quests on Chaos difficulty. While the enemies are brutal, the drop rate for V+ Sigils increases from 2% on Proud to 8% on Chaos. To consistently clear these quests under the 10-minute mark, your team should utilize the following setup:

1. **The DPS Carry**: Sandalphon or Narmaya. Sandalphon's lightstep spam provides safe, continuous damage during Beelzebub's chaotic teleportation phases.
2. **The Support/Buffer**: Cagliostro or Maglielle. Maglielle is highly valued in Chaos difficulty for her ability to generate barriers and summon flying weapons that attack automatically while she focuses on dodging.
3. **The Debuffer**: Fediel. Her dark pools reduce enemy defense by 25% and inflict slow, giving your DPS windows to execute full combos.
4. **The Tank/Utility**: Vane or Vaseraga. Vane's Rampart shield protects the team from Beelzebub's arena-wide ultimate attacks.

If you are farming solo with AI companions, equip your AI with full defensive Sigils (Autorevive, Guts, and Aegis) and set their skill usage to "Immediate." AI companions dodge perfectly unless they are locked in skill animations, so reducing their offensive load increases their survivability.

## Optimizing The Conflux for Speed Runs

The Conflux is the premier solo activity in Endless Ragnarok. Because it bypasses the matchmaking queue, it is the fastest way to acquire the sheer volume of materials required for Master Traits (Insight, Essence, and Crux paths). A standard run takes 20 minutes, but with the right routing and character choice, you can cut this down to under 12 minutes.

When you enter The Conflux through the Seedhollow gateway, you are presented with a choice of starting buffs. Always prioritize **Cooldown Reduction** and **Link Time Generation**. 

As you progress through the rooms, you will encounter three room types: Combat, Treasure, and Rare Monster. While Treasure rooms seem appealing, they do not reward the high-tier Master Trait scrolls. Instead, prioritize Combat rooms with "Elite" modifiers and always enter a Rare Monster room if it appears. Rare Monsters, such as the Golden Slime King, drop massive amounts of Refinement Sand and Master Trait scrolls.

### Speedrun Strategy: The Sandalphon/Cagliostro Duo

For solo Conflux farming, Sandalphon is the undisputed king due to his Supreme Primarch Mode. His ability to clear entire waves of enemies with wide-reaching light waves minimizes room clear times. If you prefer a safer, automated approach, Cagliostro's Phantasmagoria buff combined with her quick-charge collapse attacks allows you to shred boss armor values instantly.

Before entering, ensure your character is level 100 with at least the first rank of their Master Traits unlocked. This grants a passive stat boost that scales inside The Conflux, regardless of the temporary power-ups you choose between rooms.

## Primal Summon Materials and Chaos-Difficulty Farming

The Summon System is a game-changer for Chaos-difficulty quests. Lyria can summon primal beasts to assist in combat, but these summons are restricted to Chaos-difficulty quests and higher. Each player can bring up to four summons, though only one can be active on the field at a time. The most coveted summons are the controllable ones: Lucilius and Rolan.

Farming the materials to upgrade these summons requires targeting specific high-tier bosses. To trigger a Primal Burst—which deals massive damage and extends your Link Time—all four party members must execute their Skybound Arts (SBA) in rapid succession while Link Time is active. This requires precise coordination or highly optimized AI companions.

To farm the *Genesis Feathers* and *Primal Cores* needed to upgrade Lucilius and Rolan, you must run the Chaos quest "The Creator's Shadow." 

### Chaos Difficulty Survival Tips
At this level of play, defense is just as important as offense. One mistake can wipe your party. Follow these guidelines to ensure successful runs:
* **Equip Flight over Fight**: This Sigil increases your dodge invincibility frames at the cost of some attack power. In Chaos difficulty, surviving to deal consistent damage is faster than constantly waiting for revives.
* **Coordinate SBA Chains**: Do not use your Skybound Art as soon as it is charged. Wait until the boss enters their "Overdrive" state to chain all four SBAs, forcing them into a "Break" state and canceling their wipe mechanics.
* **Utilize Shields**: Maglielle's barrier skills can absorb a single lethal hit, making her an invaluable support character for online matchmaking.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the fastest way to farm Weapon Transcendence materials?
The fastest route is to target the Fatebreaker quests "Twin Blades of Fate" and "Heralds of Doom" in a coordinated online lobby. Breaking specific boss parts, such as Seofon's sword or Ragnalia's horns, grants a guaranteed bonus chest containing the required essences. Solo farming is viable but takes roughly 30% longer per run.

### Can I farm S-tier Sigils in The Conflux?
Yes, but only at Depth 50 and below. The drop rate for S-tier Sigils like Warpath+ and War Elemental+ is significantly lower in The Conflux compared to Chaos-difficulty boss quests like Beelzebub. Use The Conflux primarily for Master Trait scrolls and weapon enhancement materials, and stick to Chaos quests for Sigil farming.

### How does crossplay affect farming efficiency?
Endless Ragnarok features full cross-platform play across PS5, PS4, Switch 2, and PC (Steam). This significantly reduces matchmaking times for high-difficulty Chaos and Fatebreaker quests. To maximize efficiency, enable crossplay in your network settings to quickly find lobbies dedicated to specific boss farms.

### Do AI companions use Summons in Chaos-difficulty quests?
AI companions will not manually trigger controllable summons like Lucilius or Rolan. However, they will contribute to the passive bonuses of your equipped summons, and they will participate in triggering a Primal Burst if you initiate the Skybound Art chain during Link Time.
