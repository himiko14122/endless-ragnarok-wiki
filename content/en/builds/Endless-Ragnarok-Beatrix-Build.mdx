## The Delta Clock Mechanic and Rotation Strategy

The core of Beatrix's gameplay revolves around managing her Delta Clock gauge. This resource is represented by a three-pronged clock interface on the HUD. Certain skills and combo finishers move the clock hands. Aligning these hands triggers "Delta Drive," a state that grants massive buffs to attack speed, critical hit damage, and damage cap limits.

To trigger Delta Drive efficiently, you must land the final hit of her heavy combo (*Embrasque Strike*) exactly when the clock hand overlaps with the active marker. Pressing the input too early or too late resets the alignment progress, severely dropping your DPS.

```
[Normal Attacks (X3)] ➔ [Embrasque Strike (Timed Heavy)] ➔ [Delta Hand Aligns] ➔ [Delta Drive Active]
```

### Optimal Delta Clock DPS Loop

To maintain near-permanent uptime on Delta Drive, follow this specific skill rotation:

1. **Initiation:** Cast *Rising Star* to gap-close and instantly advance the Delta Clock by one stage.
2. **Combo Setup:** Perform three light attacks followed by a timed heavy attack (*Embrasque Strike*). Release the heavy attack button the moment the visual flash appears on her sword, Embrasque.
3. **Buff Activation:** Cast *Sleight of Hand* to animation-cancel the recovery of your heavy attack and immediately align the second hand.
4. **Burst Phase:** Once Delta Drive is active, execute *Embrasque Wreck*. This skill consumes the Delta Drive state to unleash a high-damage multi-hit flurry that bypasses standard damage cap limits.
5. **Cooldown Recovery:** Use *Thunder Spit* to deal ranged damage and reduce active skill cooldowns while resetting your positioning.

| Delta Clock Stage | Buff Name | Attack Power Modifier | Critical Rate Bonus | Damage Cap Boost |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1 (One Hand)** | Tic-Tac | +10% | +5% | None |
| **Stage 2 (Two Hands)** | Chrono Shift | +20% | +10% | +5% |
| **Stage 3 (Aligned)** | Delta Drive | +45% | +20% | +15% |

During Delta Drive, Beatrix also gains the Jamming effect. This effect increases her attack power as her current health drops. Maintaining your HP between 30% and 50% is crucial for maximizing this bonus.

## Beatrix Master Traits Priority

Upon reaching Level 100, you unlock Master Traits. These traits are divided into three distinct progression paths: Insight, Essence, and Crux. Each path provides unique passive bonuses that reshape how Beatrix interacts with the Delta Clock and her overall survivability.

For this DPS build, prioritizing the **Essence** path is mandatory, followed closely by **Crux**. The **Insight** path provides defensive utility, which is less useful for endgame time-attack scenarios but can assist players struggling with survival in Chaos difficulty.

### Recommended Master Trait Allocation

* **Essence Path (Primary):** Increases the duration of the Delta Drive state and boosts the attack speed multiplier. Unlocking the Rank 3 Style upgrade *Temporal Mastery* prevents the Delta Clock from resetting if you miss a timed combo window once every 45 seconds.
* **Crux Path (Secondary):** Reduces skill cooldowns whenever a timed combo finisher is successfully executed. The Rank 3 Style upgrade *Fated Hour* increases the damage cap of all skills by 10% while Delta Drive is active.
* **Insight Path (Tertiary):** Provides a shield when entering Delta Drive. Allocate points here only after maximizing the Essence and Crux trees.

| Master Trait Path | Key Upgrade Name | Rank | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Essence** | Temporal Mastery | Rank Style 4 | Prevents clock reset on failed timing; increases Delta Drive duration by 5 seconds. |
| **Essence** | Overclocked Edge | Rank Perk 3 | Increases critical damage by 15% when HP is below 45%. |
| **Crux** | Fated Hour | Rank Style 4 | Boosts skill damage cap by 10% during Delta Drive. |
| **Crux** | Swift Alignment | Rank Perk 2 | Reduces all active cooldowns by 8% upon successful timed finishers. |

To gather the necessary points and upgrade materials to max out these traits, run [Conflux runs](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) to farm High-Grade Merit scrolls and Master Points efficiently.

## Best Weapon Choices and Transcendence Path

Beatrix's performance depends heavily on her weapon choice. While her Ascension weapon offers decent baseline stats, her Terminus Weapon, **Embrasque**, is the best-in-slot option. It features the *Regenesis* trait, which automatically boosts damage caps when your HP is managed within the optimal low-health range.

Once you fully enhance Embrasque, you must unlock Weapon Transcendence. This system allows you to upgrade the weapon up to level 150, unlocking additional Sigil slots and swappable trait options.

### Weapon Transcendence Progression

To transcend Embrasque, you must gather specialized materials from Fatebreaker quests and high-depth gateways in The Conflux. 

* **Slot II Trait Recommendation:** Level 25 Critical Hit Rate (allows you to swap out a Critical Hit Sigil for more utility).
* **Slot IV Trait Recommendation:** Level 10 Damage Cap (essential for squeezing out maximum DPS during the Delta Drive burst phase).
* **Slot EX Reward:** Unlocks the *Fiery Retribution* aesthetic effect and grants a permanent +500 raw Attack Power bonus.

| Transcendence Stage | Required Materials | Source | Key Benefit |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Stage 1 (Lvl 110)** | 5x Fatebreaker Core, 20x Conflux Shard | Fatebreaker Quests / Conflux | Unlocks Slot II Trait (Max Lvl 25) |

| **Stage 3 (Lvl 150)** | 2x Abyssal Core, 1x Omega Splinter | Chaos Difficulty Quests | Unlocks Slot EX Reward & Aesthetic |

## Endgame Summon Setup and Combat Strategy

In the Chaos-difficulty quests introduced in the Endless Ragnarok expansion, Lyria can summon primal beasts to assist in combat. Each party member can equip up to four summons, but only one can be active at a time. The passive stat bonuses from equipped summons apply to your character throughout the fight.

For Beatrix, you want to equip summons that boost critical damage, skill cooldown speed, or provide temporary invincibility to protect her during her low-HP windows.

### Recommended Summons

* **Lucilius (Active/Passive):** Provides a passive 10% boost to all damage caps. When summoned actively, Lucilius casts *Paradise Lost*, dealing massive AOE Light damage and slowing down enemy movement speed for 8 seconds. This slow effect makes it incredibly easy to land perfect Delta Clock combos on highly mobile bosses.
* **Rolan (Active/Passive):** Provides a passive shield equivalent to 20% of maximum HP when your character drops below 30% health. This acts as a safety net for Beatrix's low-HP playstyle.
* **Bahamut (Passive):** Boosts Skybound Art (SBA) damage and charge rate, allowing Beatrix to initiate Link Time faster.

### Utilizing Summons in Chaos Raids

During high-difficulty fights like Beelzebub or The World, save your active summon for the boss's overdrive phase. When the boss enters overdrive, initiate a Link Time chain. 

If all four party members activate their Skybound Arts during Link Time while a summon is active, it triggers a **Primal Burst**. This dealing massive neutral damage that bypasses all boss defensive shields and automatically aligns Beatrix's Delta Clock to Stage 3.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I stop getting interrupted while timing the Delta Clock combos?
To prevent enemy attacks from interrupting your combos, slot a *Stout Heart* Sigil or rely on the shield provided by the **Insight** Master Trait path. Additionally, using the skill *Rising Star* gives Beatrix temporary debuff immunity, allowing you to execute your timed heavy attacks without interruption.

### Is the Terminus Weapon always better than the Ascension Weapon for Beatrix?
Yes. The Terminus Weapon, *Embrasque*, features unique scaling that directly boosts your damage cap based on how low your HP is. Since Beatrix naturally wants to stay at low HP to utilize her Jamming buffs, the synergy makes the Terminus Weapon significantly stronger than the Ascension weapon at all stages of endgame content.

### What is the fastest way to farm materials for Beatrix's Weapon Transcendence?
The fastest method is running high-depth gateways in **The Conflux** solo mode. These runs take approximately 20 minutes and yield a high volume of Conflux Shards and High-Grade Merit scrolls. For boss-specific drops like Dread-Wing Feathers, you must farm the Beelzebub raid on Proud difficulty or higher.

### Can I run Beatrix as a full-health DPS instead of low-health?
While you can run Beatrix at full health, you will lose out on the massive attack modifiers from her Jamming mechanic and the *Sadistic Edge+* character Sigil. Running her at full health reduces her overall DPS output by roughly 30% to 40%, making it difficult to clear the tight DPS checks in Chaos-difficulty raids.
