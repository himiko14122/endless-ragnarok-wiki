## Active Skill Setup for Endgame Support

Cagliostro's active skills form the core of her utility. Unlike pure DPS setups, a support-oriented build prioritizes uptime on party buffs and defensive recovery. This makes her the ultimate choice for players looking to play an **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro support** role in matchmaking or solo play.

The four skills selected for this build focus on maximizing party attack power, cleansing debuffs, restoring health, and providing instant resurrection.

*   **Phantasmagoria**: This is Cagliostro's signature buff. It applies a 30% Attack, 30% Defense, and 30% Critical Hit Rate increase to all party members within range. Uptime on this buff should be maintained at 100% using cooldown reduction mechanics.
*   **Reinforce**: An area-of-effect heal that scales with Cagliostro's maximum HP and healing cap. It provides immediate recovery after boss transition phases or raid-wide damage events.
*   **Revive**: In Chaos difficulty, where bosses like Ragnalia or The World can defeat under-geared allies in a single strike, the **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro revive** skill acts as a crucial safety net. Using the **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro revive** spell allows fallen allies to return to the fight instantly without depleting the Critical Gauge.
*   **Sacred Wonder**: A ranged attack that inflicts Slow on the target. This skill is vital for delaying boss actions, giving your party time to position or execute Skybound Arts (SBA) chains.

## Master Traits Progression (Insight, Essence, Crux)

Unlocking the **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro Master Traits** requires reaching character Level 100 and utilizing materials gathered from high-tier gateways in Seedhollow or Tredame Sanctum. The Master Traits system features three distinct paths: Insight, Essence, and Crux. Each path offers unique Rank Perks and Rank Style upgrades.

The optimal pathing for **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro Master Traits** prioritizes the **Insight** path to maximize healing cap limits and skill uptime, followed by the **Essence** path for buff duration extensions.

1.  **Insight Path (Priority 1)**: Unlocks Rank Perks that reduce active skill cooldowns by a flat percentage when landing a fully charged Collapse attack. It also raises the healing cap of *Reinforce* by up to 45%.
2.  **Essence Path (Priority 2)**: Provides Rank Style upgrades that extend the duration of *Phantasmagoria* by 10 seconds, reducing the window of downtime between casts. It also increases Cagliostro's defense while charging.
3.  **Crux Path (Priority 3)**: Boosts Link Time efficiency and increases the stun value of Collapse attacks. While useful, it takes a backseat to the utility offered by Insight and Essence.

By fully investing in the **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro Master Traits**, you gain access to Rank Style upgrades that allow you to swap passive bonuses depending on the encounter. For instance, when fighting Seofon & Tweyen, switching to defense-oriented Rank Styles prevents Cagliostro from being interrupted during her charge sequences. Understanding how to allocate points in the **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro Master Traits** system is the difference between a mediocre healer and an unkillable support engine.

## Chaos Difficulty Rotation & Summon Strategy

Running this **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro build** in Chaos difficulty requires strict execution of your combat rotation. You cannot play passively; Cagliostro must actively attack to reduce her skill cooldowns.

### Combat Rotation Loop
To maximize your utility, execute the following loop during combat:

1.  **Buff**: Start the encounter by casting *Phantasmagoria* to buff the party's attack, defense, and critical hit rate.
2.  **Charge**: Execute a basic attack combo leading into a fully charged *Collapse* (hold heavy attack).
3.  **Quick Collapse**: Release the charge at the perfect timing window to trigger *Quick Collapse*. This triggers the cooldown reduction from *Founder's Truth+*.
4.  **Debuff**: Cast *Sacred Wonder* to apply Slow to the boss, extending the damage window for your DPS players.
5.  **Repeat**: Loop basic attacks and charged Collapse strikes until *Phantasmagoria* is off cooldown.

```
[Start] ──> Phantasmagoria ──> Basic Combo ──> Charged Collapse ──> Sacred Wonder (Slow) ──> [Loop]
```

### Managing Resurrections
In Chaos difficulty, saving the **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro revive** cooldown for critical moments is essential. Do not waste the spell if an ally has *Autorevive* active or if they have green potions remaining. Instead, monitor the Critical Gauge. If an ally is downed during a major boss mechanic (such as Beelzebub's black hole attack) and their gauge is rapidly ticking down, use the revive instantly. Mastering the timing of the **Endless Ragnarok Cagliostro revive** ability is what keeps teams from failing high-difficulty [Fatebreaker quests](/en/endgame/fatebreaker-quests-guide/).

### Summon System Integration
During Chaos difficulty quests, Lyria can utilize the Summon System to field primal beasts. As a support player, you should coordinate with your team to bring defensive summons. While DPS players may prioritize Lucilius for offensive power, Cagliostro players should equip **Rolan** or support-oriented summons. 

Triggering a **Primal Burst** requires all four party members to chain their Skybound Arts during Link Time. Doing so freezes the boss and deals massive elemental damage, giving Cagliostro a safe window to cast *Reinforce* and top off the party's health bars.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Why is Cagliostro ranked S-tier instead of SS-tier like Sandalphon?
Sandalphon occupies the SS-tier due to his Supreme Primarch Mode, which allows him to bypass normal damage caps and spam lightstep sword waves with minimal setup. Cagliostro is ranked S-tier because she is a dedicated support. While her damage output is lower than Sandalphon's, her utility, healing, and revive capabilities make her indispensable for clearing Chaos-difficulty content safely.

### How does the cooldown reduction from Founder's Truth+ interact with Quick Cooldown Sigils?
They stack multiplicatively. The *Quick Cooldown* Sigils reduce your base cooldowns by up to 45% at Level 45. The *Founder's Truth+* character Sigil then subtracts a flat 3% from the remaining cooldown time every time a fully charged Collapse attack hits an enemy. This allows Cagliostro to cast her skills twice as fast as other characters.

### Should I prioritize Defense or HP on Cagliostro's Sigils?
In Chaos difficulty, bosses can easily defeat characters with low defense. You should prioritize HP up to the point where you can survive a single hit (usually around 45,000 HP), and then focus on Defense and Stout Heart. Cagliostro's *Phantasmagoria* provides a 30% Defense buff, which becomes much more effective if you have high base defense stats.

### Can I use the Revive skill in solo mode?
Yes. In solo mode, if one of your AI-controlled party members is defeated, Cagliostro will automatically target them with *Revive* if the skill is off cooldown and you are not currently performing another action. This makes solo runs of Fatebreaker quests much safer.
