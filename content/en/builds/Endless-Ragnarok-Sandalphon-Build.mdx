## Supreme Primarch Mode Mechanics & Lightstep Rotation

The core of Sandalphon's offensive power lies in his transformation state: Supreme Primarch Mode. When active, Sandalphon sprouts six wings of light, altering his basic attacks into ranged sword waves that pierce enemies and deal multi-hit light elemental damage. Maintaining this mode and canceling animations using the lightstep technique is key to achieving SS-tier DPS.

### Activating and Maintaining the Gauge

To enter Supreme Primarch Mode, you must fill the Primarch Gauge to 100%. This is accomplished through two primary methods:
1. **Elation (Skill):** Instantly grants 50% gauge and applies a temporary Attack Up buff.
2. **Aetherial Flurry (Combo):** A rapid-slashing combo that generates gauge per hit. 

Once the gauge is full, press the unique action button to activate the mode. The gauge drains continuously over time, but landing hits with the lightstep sword wave spam slows down this decay, extending the transformation duration.

### The Lightstep Sword Wave Spam Rotation

The "lightstep" is an advanced animation-canceling technique. By dodging immediately after the second light attack in Sandalphon's Supreme Primarch combo, you skip the recovery frames of the physical swing while instantly triggering the projectile generation of the subsequent heavy attack. 

Execute this rotation using the following steps:
1. **Initiate:** Perform Light Attack 1 -> Light Attack 2.
2. **Cancel:** Input a directional Dodge (Lightstep) just as the second swing connects.
3. **Execute:** Immediately press Heavy Attack. Sandalphon will fire three massive light waves in a wide arc.
4. **Loop:** Repeat the sequence. The dodge reset allows you to bypass the standard combo finisher, keeping you in a constant state of high-damage projectile output.

## Master Traits Allocation: Crux, Insight, and Essence

Introduced in the Endless Ragnarok DLC, the Master Traits system unlocks once Sandalphon reaches Level 100. Each character features three distinct paths: Insight, Essence, and Crux. Each path offers 3 Rank Perks and 4 Rank Style upgrades. For this SS-tier DPS build, prioritizing the Crux and Insight paths is mandatory to bypass standard damage caps and maximize skill uptime.

### Crux Path: Raw Offense and Cap Breaking

The Crux path directly enhances Sandalphon's sword wave damage caps and critical strike efficiency. You should allocate your Master Trait points here first. The Rank 3 Perk, *Aetherial Resonance*, increases the damage cap of all lightstep sword waves by an additional 15% when Supreme Primarch Mode is active.

### Insight Path: Cooldown Reduction and Utility

The Insight path is secondary but crucial. It reduces the cooldown of *Elation* and *Baraqiel* (Sandalphon's primary defensive self-buff). Reaching Rank 2 Perk *Chronos Shift* ensures that you can loop Supreme Primarch Mode with minimal downtime between transformations.

### Essence Path: Defensive Utility

Only allocate leftover points into the Essence path. While the health and defense boosts are helpful for surviving Chaos difficulty, Sandalphon relies on the invulnerability frames of his lightstep dodge to avoid damage rather than face-tanking hits.

## Best Sigils and Loadout Optimization

To maximize the output of your lightstep sword waves, you must equip a precise combination of S-tier and A-tier Sigils. The expansion introduced new Sigil traits that expand on the base game's limits, making slot optimization critical for tackling endgame content like the [Chaos Difficulty Boss guide](/en/guides/difficulty-settings-explained/).

### S-Tier Sigils: The Core Foundation

1. **Warpath+ (Sandalphon Specific):** This exclusive DLC Sigil increases the damage cap of Supreme Primarch Mode attacks by 20% and adds a light defense shred to all sword waves.
2. **War Elemental+:** Converts all of Sandalphon's attacks into the enemy's elemental weakness, ensuring a flat 20% damage increase across all matchups.
3. **Primarch's Authority+:** Combines Sandalphon's two character-specific traits, extending the duration of Supreme Primarch Mode by 5 seconds and increasing lightstep travel distance.

### A-Tier Sigils: Cap Raising and Cooldowns

1. **Alpha V+ / Gamma V+:** These Sigils are obtained from high-level raids. Alpha boosts normal attack damage caps (which applies to lightstep sword waves), while Gamma provides a protective shield when performing a Skybound Art (SBA).
2. **Critical Hit Rate V+:** Essential for reaching a 100% critical hit rate. Ensure the sub-trait is *Stamina* or *Combo Booster* to maintain maximum damage scaling.

Weapon Transcendence allows you to push Sandalphon's Terminus weapon, *Ain Soph Aur*, past its standard limits. This process unlocks swappable traits in Slot II and Slot IV, along with unique aesthetic effects and Slot EX rewards. 

### Transcending Ain Soph Aur

To begin Weapon Transcendence, you must first complete the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue, then farm materials from Fatebreaker quests and The Conflux. 

* **Slot II Trait (Max 25):** Select *Normal Damage Cap Up*. This directly stacks with your Alpha Sigils, pushing the damage of your lightstep sword waves to their absolute limit.
* **Slot IV Trait (Max 10):** Select *Quick Cooldown*. This works in tandem with your Insight Master Traits to ensure *Elation* is available almost immediately after Supreme Primarch Mode ends.

### Summon System Integration

In Chaos-difficulty quests, Lyria's Summon System becomes active. Sandalphon players should coordinate with their party to equip summons that complement his burst windows. **Lucilius** is the premier controllable summon for this build. When summoned during Link Time, Lucilius triggers a Primal Burst that freezes the boss's action bar, allowing Sandalphon to execute uninterrupted lightstep rotations.

## Boss-Specific Strategies: Beelzebub and The World

When taking this Sandalphon build into endgame encounters, your playstyle must adapt to the specific attack patterns of bosses like Beelzebub (the expansion's major antagonist) and The World. For a broader look at how Sandalphon ranks against other characters in these fights, check out the [DLC Characters tier list](/en/tier-list/).

### Defeating Beelzebub with Lightstep Spacing

Beelzebub frequently deploys large, red-and-black area-of-effect (AoE) attacks that can easily one-hit weaker characters on Chaos difficulty. 
* **Strategy:** Use the mobility of the lightstep to circle Beelzebub. The dodge component of the rotation keeps you invulnerable during his *Chaos Calamity* sword sweeps.
* **Execution:** Do not commit to the full heavy attack sword wave if Beelzebub is preparing his teleport slam. Instead, perform Light Attack -> Lightstep to reposition behind him, then unleash the heavy waves into his back for backstab damage bonuses.

### Breaking The World's Shield Phases

The World utilizes multi-layered elemental shields that mitigate incoming damage unless hit by high-frequency attacks.
* **Strategy:** Sandalphon's lightstep sword waves hit multiple times per projectile, making him the fastest shield breaker in the game.
* **Execution:** Save *Elation* for the start of the shield phase. Immediately enter Supreme Primarch Mode and spam the lightstep rotation. The sheer volume of hits will deplete the shield before the boss can execute its wipe mechanic.

## Endless Ragnarok Sandalphon Build FAQ

### Can I run this Sandalphon build without his Terminus Weapon?
While the Terminus Weapon (*Ain Soph Aur*) is optimal due to its built-in Catastrophe trait, you can use the Ascension Weapon (*Chevalier Sword Omega*) as a temporary substitute. Ensure you fully upgrade it and unlock Weapon Transcendence to compensate for the lower base damage caps.

### How does Sandalphon's lightstep compare to other DLC characters?
Sandalphon's lightstep offers superior safety and range compared to other high-tier characters like Beatrix or Eustace. The invulnerability frames of his dodge cancel are active for 12 frames, which is slightly longer than the standard dodge window, making it highly forgiving in Chaos difficulty.

### What is the fastest way to farm Master Traits materials for Sandalphon?
The fastest source of Master Traits enhancement materials is [The Conflux solo guide](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) mode. Aim for Depths 40 and above, focusing on clearing combat rooms quickly using Sandalphon's high AoE sword waves. The chests at these depths have a high drop rate for Conflux Crystals and Fatebreaker Essence.
