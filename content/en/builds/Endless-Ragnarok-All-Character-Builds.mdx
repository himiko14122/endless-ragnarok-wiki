## Core Build Fundamentals in Endless Ragnarok

Building a character in the Endless Ragnarok expansion differs significantly from the base game due to the introduction of [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/) and the Level 100 Master Trait system. Players can no longer rely solely on generic Damage Cap V+ Sigils. The endgame meta requires balancing the three Master Trait paths—Insight, Essence, and Crux—while optimizing custom traits in Transcendence Slots II and IV.

### Sigil Tiering and Cap Management
With the level cap for weapon upgrades extended, hitting the raw damage cap is easier than ever. The focus has shifted to utility, supplemental damage, and character-specific Warpath+ Sigils. 

* **S-Tier Sigils**: *Warpath+* (character-specific buffs), *War Elemental+* (converts all damage to the enemy's elemental weakness), and *Character Specific+* double-trait Sigils.
* **A-Tier Sigils**: *Alpha V+*, *Beta V+*, and *Gamma V+* (which boost normal, skill, and SBA damage caps respectively, while providing barrier and cooldown reduction effects).
* **Utility Sigils**: *Potion Hoarder V*, *Autorevive V*, and *Guts V* remain mandatory for surviving the one-hit mechanics found in Chaos-difficulty quests.

| **Warpath+** | Character | 15 | Grants unique offensive mechanics (e.g., Sandalphon's Primarch gauge retention). |
| **Alpha V+** | Offense / Utility | 30 (Set) | Boosts normal attack damage cap by up to 40% and restores SBA gauge on hit. |
| **Beta V+** | Offense / Utility | 30 (Set) | Boosts skill damage cap by up to 40% and reduces skill cooldowns upon SBA usage. |

### Weapon Transcendence Slots
When you transcend a Terminus or Ascension weapon, you unlock custom trait slots. For Slot II, players should target Level 25 traits such as *Supplemental Damage* or *Crit Damage*. For Slot IV, utility traits like *Cascade* or *Uplift* (up to Level 10) are optimal for maintaining high uptime on skills and the [Summon System](/en/summons/summon-system-guide/).

## Top-Tier Meta Builds (SS & S Tier)

The top tiers of Endless Ragnarok are dominated by characters who can exploit rapid attack speed, high skill multipliers, or invincibility frames to bypass heavy boss mechanics. Below are the optimal builds for the strongest characters in the current meta.

### Sandalphon (SS-Tier)
Sandalphon remains the undisputed king of DPS due to his Supreme Primarch Mode. His build focuses on maximizing the uptime of his lightstep sword waves.
* **Weapon**: *Aeterna* (Terminus Weapon, Transcended to Level 10)
* **Master Trait Path**: **Crux** (unlocks Rank 3 Perk: *Primarch's Authority*, reducing skill cooldowns by 15% when entering Supreme Primarch Mode)
* **Core Sigils**: Sandalphon's *Primarch's Wings+*, *Warpath+*, 2x *Alpha V+*, 2x *Gamma V+*, *War Elemental+*, *Critical Hit Rate V+* (with *Stamina* sub-trait).
* **Skill Rotation**: Start with *Naturalis* to build gauge, activate *Supreme Primarch Mode*, spam lightstep sword waves, and use *Sanctus* on cooldown to maintain the defense debuff on the boss.

### Narmaya (S-Tier)
Narmaya relies on rapid stance swapping between Freeflutter and Dawnfly to build butterflies and unleash high-damage *Setsuna* strikes.
* **Weapon**: *Ame-no-Habakiri* (Terminus Weapon)
* **Master Trait Path**: **Essence** (unlocks *Butterfly's Grace*, increasing butterfly consumption damage by 25%)
* **Core Sigils**: *Butterfly's Valor+*, *Beta V+* (to maximize *Setsuna* and *Transient* skill damage), *Warpath+*, *Supplemental Damage V*.
* **Skill Rotation**: Generate maximum butterflies using Freeflutter combo loops, swap to Dawnfly, charge *Setsuna* to full, and release. Cancel recovery animations with *Transient*.

### Vaseraga (S-Tier)
Vaseraga is a high-risk, high-reward powerhouse that utilizes his *Immortal* buff to ignore incoming damage while delivering massive charged attacks.
* **Weapon**: *Grosza* (Terminus Weapon)
* **Master Trait Path**: **Insight** (increases charge speed and charge attack damage cap by 20%)
* **Core Sigils**: *Ebony's Poise+*, *Warpath+* (increases charged attack speed based on missing HP), *Steel Nerves V*, *Stronghold V*.
* **Skill Rotation**: Activate *Immortal* to prevent interruption, use *Great Scythe Gryn* to pull enemies, and execute fully charged heavy attack chains.

## The Ultimate Conflux & Chaos Difficulty Setup

When taking your builds into high-level content like [The Conflux](/en/conflux/) or Chaos-difficulty quests, standard DPS builds must be adjusted. Survival becomes a priority because enemies in these modes can easily one-shot characters lacking defense multipliers.

### Conflux Survival Adjustments
The Conflux is a roguelite solo mode where power-ups are selected before entering each area. Because you cannot change your gear mid-run, your base build must be versatile.
1. **Ditch Pure Glass Cannon Sigils**: Replace *Glass Cannon V* with *Stronghold V* or *Flight over Fight V*. The penalty of being stunned or losing your dodge frames in Chaos difficulty is too high.
2. **Equip Auto-Revive and Guts**: Ensure every character has at least one source of *Autorevive* and *Guts*. In The Conflux, death can end a run instantly, losing all accumulated enhancement materials.
3. **Optimize for Link Time**: Equip *Uplift V+* to speed up SBA generation. Link Time is crucial for freezing the bosses' action timers and dealing uninterrupted damage.

### Summon Integration
In Chaos quests, the [Summon System](/en/summons/summon-system-guide/) allows players to call upon primal beasts. Your build should complement your chosen summon.
* If running **Lucilius**, stack *Beta V+* Sigils. Lucilius reduces skill cooldowns, allowing you to cycle high-damage skills rapidly.
* If running **Rolan**, focus on normal attack damage caps with *Alpha V+*, as Rolan increases the party's basic attack speed and critical hit rate.

More details on surviving these encounters can be found in our [Chaos difficulty guide](/en/guides/difficulty-settings-explained/). ## Weapon Transcendence and Master Trait Pathing

To unlock the true potential of your characters, you must farm [Fatebreaker quests](/en/endgame/fatebreaker-quests-guide/) for Transcendence materials. Once a weapon reaches Transcendence Level 10, you can allocate points into the three Master Trait paths at Level 100.

```
[Level 100 Character] 
       │
       ├─► Insight Path (Utility & Charge Speed) ──► Rank Style: Dodge/Parry Frames
       ├─► Essence Path (Defense & Buff Duration) ──► Rank Style: Shield/Absorption
       └─► Crux Path (Pure Skill & SBA Damage)    ──► Rank Style: Cooldown Reduction
```

### Master Trait Path Choice by Archetype
Each character has access to all three paths, but you should prioritize unlocking the path that aligns with their primary role.

* **DPS Archetypes (e.g., Sandalphon, Narmaya, Beatrix)**: Prioritize the **Crux** path. The Rank Style upgrades in this path directly boost skill damage caps and reduce cooldowns when executing perfect dodges or parries.
* **Tank/Bruiser Archetypes (e.g., Vaseraga, Gallanza, Siegfried)**: Prioritize the **Essence** path. This path grants additional defense, debuff resistance, and HP absorption, allowing these characters to trade blows with bosses without losing their attack chains.
* **Support/Ranged Archetypes (e.g., Cagliostro, Maglielle, Io)**: Prioritize the **Insight** path. This path increases casting speed, charge speed, and item/heal potency, ensuring support skills are deployed quickly during chaotic fights.

### Transcendence Progression Priority
Always transcend your main DPS character's Terminus weapon first. The raw attack power gained from Transcendence levels 1 to 10 scales exponentially, making it much easier to clear the DPS checks in Fatebreaker quests. Once your primary DPS is maxed, focus on your support characters' Ascension weapons to boost their HP and utility slots.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I unlock Weapon Transcendence in Endless Ragnarok?
To unlock Weapon Transcendence, you must first complete the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue. Once completed, you will gain access to Fatebreaker quests. Clearing these quests awards the specialized materials required to transcend fully enhanced Level 150 weapons at the blacksmith.

### Which Master Trait path is best for DPS characters?
For pure DPS characters like Sandalphon, Narmaya, and Beatrix, the **Crux** path is almost always the best choice. It provides direct increases to skill and SBA damage caps, alongside Rank Style upgrades that reward precise play (such as perfect dodges) with skill cooldown reductions.

### Can I run the same build in solo Conflux runs as in Chaos raid quests?
While the core damage setups remain similar, solo Conflux runs require more defensive utility. In solo runs, you should swap out highly offensive Sigils like *Glass Cannon* for survivability options like *Flight over Fight*, *Potion Hoarder*, and *Autorevive*, as you do not have teammates to revive you if you fall.

### How does the new Summon System affect my build's Sigil slots?
The Summon System does not occupy standard Sigil slots. Instead, summons are equipped in a dedicated Summon Slot unlocked after reaching Chaos difficulty. However, you should tailor your Sigil loadouts to match your summon's passive bonuses—for example, pairing *Alpha V+* Sigils with Rolan to maximize normal attack efficiency.
