## Summon System Mechanics in Chaos Difficulty

Summons in Endless Ragnarok are not merely passive stat sticks; they are dynamic combat assets that can only be deployed in Chaos-difficulty quests and higher, including deep runs in [The Conflux](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) and high-tier Fatebreaker quests. Before you can equip summons, you must first complete the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue and access the Chaos difficulty tier.

Every player can equip a grid of up to four summons. However, only one summon can be actively fielded on the battlefield at any given time. While a summon is not actively fielded, it grants powerful passive bonuses to the equipping character. These passives stack with your equipped S-tier Sigils (such as Warpath+ or War Elemental+) and [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/) upgrades.

| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Equip Grid** | Max 4 summons per player character. | Unlocked after completing "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage". |
| **Active Summoning** | Only 1 active summon can be on the field per team at once. | Requires a full Summon Gauge, filled by landing hits and performing Link Attacks. |
| **Passive Bonuses** | All 4 equipped summons grant passive stat boosts. | Always active, even when the summon is not actively fielded. |

| **Primal Burst** | A devastating team-wide ultimate attack. | Triggered when all 4 party members initiate Skybound Arts (SBA) during Link Time. |

## Deep Dive: Controllable Summons (Lucilius & Rolan)

Unlike standard primal beasts that execute a single attack and vanish, Lucilius and Rolan are controllable summons. When summoned, the player's character is temporarily replaced on the field by the primal beast for 20 seconds. During this window, you gain access to a unique set of skills and basic attack strings.

```
[Summon Gauge Full] ➔ [Press L3 + R3 to Summon] ➔ [20-Second Control Window] ➔ [Execute Signature Skill] ➔ [Exit with Passive Buff]
```

### The Lucilius Summon Playstyle
When controlling Lucilius, your primary objective is to execute his basic lightstep sword wave spam to build his Singularity stacks. At 5 stacks, his active skills deal double damage.
*   **Iblis:** A rapid-fire projectile attack that inflicts Attack Down and Defense Down (15% potency).
*   **Phosphorus:** A teleporting slash that closes distances instantly, bypassing boss AoE fields.
*   **Paradise Lost (Signature):** Can only be cast when the summon timer is below 5 seconds. It freezes the quest timer and unleashes a screen-wide holy blast, dealing capped damage to all targets.

### The Rolan Summon Playstyle
Controlling Rolan requires a defensive, counter-centric mindset. He excels at protecting teammates who are executing long channel times, such as Io or charging attacks from Vaseraga.
*   **Sacred Shield:** Casts a physical and magic barrier over all active party members.
*   **Lex Talionis:** A stance skill. If Rolan is hit during this stance, he unleashes a counter-slash that fills the party's Link Gauge by 25%.
*   **Starfall (Signature):** Rolan summons a shower of light spears that heal the party for 50% of their maximum HP while dealing heavy stun damage to the boss.

## Activating Primal Burst and Link Time Synergy

The peak of summon coordination in Endless Ragnarok is the **Primal Burst**. When your party enters Link Time (achieved by performing Link Attacks until the gauge reaches 100%), the summon system gains a massive boost. If all four party members activate their Skybound Arts sequentially during this window, the standard Full Chain is replaced by a Primal Burst.

During a Primal Burst, the active summons of all four players descend onto the battlefield simultaneously to execute a combined elemental assault. The damage of the Primal Burst scales directly with the average level of the summons equipped in the primary slots of all party members. 

To execute this consistently:
1.  Save your SBA gauges until the boss enters an Overdrive state.
2.  Trigger Link Attacks to push the party into Link Time.
3.  Have the player with the **Endless Ragnarok Lucilius summon** initiate the SBA chain to apply the Slow debuff.
4.  Chain the remaining three SBAs immediately. The resulting Primal Burst will deal up to 40% more damage than a standard Full Chain and will bypass the boss's elemental resistances.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I unlock the summon system in Endless Ragnarok?
To unlock the summon system, you must complete the base game's main story and clear the Proud-difficulty quest titled "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage." Once cleared, the Epilogue chapter of the Endless Ragnarok DLC will begin, unlocking Chaos-difficulty quests and giving you access to the summon inventory screen via Lyria's Journal.

### Can I use the Endless Ragnarok Lucilius summon in lower-difficulty quests?
No. Summons are high-tier tactical assets reserved exclusively for Chaos-difficulty quests, Fatebreaker challenges, and [The Conflux](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) solo mode. If you queue for Maniac, Proud, or lower-difficulty quests, your summon grid will be disabled, and you will not receive their passive bonuses or active skills.

### What is the fastest way to farm Weapon Transcendence materials using summons?
Equip a speed-run summon setup featuring the **Endless Ragnarok Lucilius summon** as your primary and Tiamat Omega in a sub-slot. Target the Fatebreaker quests that drop Transcendence essences. Use Lucilius's active control window to force boss breaks quickly, allowing you to bypass phase transitions and clear the quests under the 3-minute mark.

### Do summon passive bonuses stack with S-tier Sigils?
Yes. Passive bonuses such as Lucilius's Damage Cap Break and Rolan's SBA Gauge Gain stack additively with S-tier Sigils like Warpath+ and Alpha/Beta/Gamma Sigils. This makes optimizing your summon grid essential for reaching the absolute maximum damage potential in the endgame.
