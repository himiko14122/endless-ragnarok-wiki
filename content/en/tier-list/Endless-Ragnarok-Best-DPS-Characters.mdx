## Top-Tier DPS Character Breakdowns

To maximize your damage, you must understand the specific mechanics that elevate these characters to the top of the meta. Below is a detailed breakdown of the premier damage dealers in the expansion.

### Sandalphon (SS-Tier)
Sandalphon solidifies his position as the **Endless Ragnarok best DPS character** for general content. His kit revolves around **Supreme Primarch Mode**, which alters his normal attack string into lightstep sword waves. These waves pierce enemies and hit multiple times per animation frame, effectively doubling his DPS. 

*   **Key Rotation:** Activate *Epitaph* to build gauge, enter Supreme Primarch Mode, and use *Banishment* to close distances. During Link Time, spam *Sanctus* followed by rapid normal attacks.
*   **Why He Rules the Meta:** His dodge animations during Supreme Primarch Mode do not reset his attack combo, allowing him to maintain maximum DPS while dodging boss mechanics.

### Narmaya (S-Tier)
If you are searching for the **Endless Ragnarok best DPS character** for pure, stationary burst damage, Narmaya is the premier choice. Her gameplay requires shifting between Freeflutter Stance (speed) and Dawnfly Stance (heavy charge attacks). 

*   **Key Rotation:** Build butterflies in Freeflutter stance using *Kyokasugetsu*, transition to Dawnfly stance with a charge attack, and unleash *Setsuna* at max charge.
*   **Why She Rules the Meta:** Her unique Sigil, *Butterfly Effect*, adds extra damage instances to every slash, allowing her to bypass standard damage caps more effectively than almost any other character.

### Zeta (S-Tier)
Zeta is the queen of aerial combat, making her a strong contender for the **Endless Ragnarok best DPS character** in fights with high boss mobility, such as the encounter against Ragnalia. 

*   **Key Rotation:** Launch into the air with *Rain of Furies*, perform three perfectly timed loop attacks, and finish with a high-damage *Arvess Hammer*.
*   **Why She Rules the Meta:** While airborne, Zeta is immune to ground-based hazards and shockwaves. This allows her to continue dealing damage while ground-based melee characters are forced to retreat and dodge.

## Weapon Transcendence and Master Traits Impact

The Weapon Transcendence system, unlocked after completing the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" and progressing through the epilogue, allows players to upgrade their weapons beyond Level 150. This system is vital for unlocking the **Endless Ragnarok highest damage** limits.

### Weapon Transcendence Slots
When you transcend a weapon (such as a Terminus or Ascension weapon), you unlock additional trait slots:
*   **Slot II (Max Level 25):** Typically reserved for Damage Cap or Critical Hit Rate upgrades.
*   **Slot IV (Max Level 10):** Used for utility traits like Cascade or Quick Cooldown, which directly translate to higher DPS by shortening skill downtime.
*   **Slot EX:** Provides a flat multiplier to overall attack power and raises the baseline damage cap of all skills.

### Master Traits
Unlocked at Level 100, Master Traits provide three paths: **Insight**, **Essence**, and **Crux**. For DPS characters, the Crux path is the priority as it directly boosts skill damage and critical hit damage.

| Character | Weapon Type | Slot II Priority | Slot IV Priority | Master Trait Focus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sandalphon** | Terminus (World Cutter) | Damage Cap +25 | Quick Cooldown +10 | Crux (Path of Light) |
| **Narmaya** | Terminus (Ame-no-Habakiri)| Critical Damage +25 | Cascade +10 | Crux (Path of Butterflies) |
| **Zeta** | Terminus (Brionac) | Damage Cap +25 | Quick Cooldown +10 | Crux (Path of the Spear) |
| **Charlotta** | Executioner (Claidheamh) | Combo Booster +25 | Cascade +10 | Essence (Path of Haste) |

To farm the materials required for Weapon Transcendence, players must challenge Fatebreaker quests and exchange rewards gained from deep runs in [Conflux Mode](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/). ## Sigil Builds and Summon Synergies

Achieving top-tier DPS requires a meticulous Sigil setup. In the Endless Ragnarok expansion, S-tier Sigils like **Warpath+** and **War Elemental+** are mandatory for any build aiming to hit the absolute damage cap.

### Core Sigil Setup
The table below outlines the optimal Sigil configuration for maximizing DPS in Chaos-difficulty quests.

| Sigil Slot | Sigil Name | Tier | Primary Effect | Strategy / Usage |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Slot 1** | War Elemental+ | S | Converts all attacks to the enemy's elemental weakness. | Increases overall damage by a flat 20% and bypasses elemental resistance. |
| **Slot 2** | Warpath+ | S | Unique character-specific damage multiplier. | Boosts specific mechanic damage (e.g., Sandalphon's lightstep waves by 15%). |
| **Slot 3** | Damage Cap V+ | A | Increases the maximum damage limit of all attacks. | Stack up to 4 of these to ensure your attacks do not hit the base cap. |
| **Slot 4** | Critical Hit Rate V+ | A | Increases critical hit chance. | Essential for ensuring 100% critical rate when paired with Wrightstones. |
| **Slot 5** | Alpha / Beta / Gamma | A | Boosts SBA damage, Skill damage, or provides barrier shields. | Mix and match based on character (e.g., Gamma for barrier protection). |

### Summon System Integration
In Chaos difficulty quests, players can utilize the Summon System. Each player can equip up to 4 summons, with one active on the field. Controllable summons like **Lucilius** and **Rolan** provide massive passive stat boosts to the party.

When all four party members activate their Skybound Arts (SBA) during Link Time, it triggers a **Primal Burst**. This burst deals millions of damage and applies a temporary defense shred to the boss. Top-tier DPS players coordinate their SBA usage to ensure the Primal Burst occurs when the boss is in a vulnerable state, allowing the party to melt the boss's remaining health.

## DLC Characters: Early DPS Evaluation

The six new DLC characters released on July 9, 2026—Beatrix, Eustace, Fraux, Fediel, Gallanza, and Maglielle—are still undergoing rigorous testing, but early data indicates they are highly competitive.

### Beatrix (Potential S-Tier)
Beatrix utilizes the **Delta Clock** mechanic. By timing her skills with the ticking of her clock gauge, she gains massive attack speed and attack power buffs. If players can master the strict timing requirements, Beatrix easily rivals Narmaya in sustained DPS.

### Eustace (A-Tier)
Eustace provides high-damage ranged combat. His ability to charge his attacks while moving allows him to maintain a safe distance from bosses like The World while dealing consistent damage. He is a highly reliable choice for players struggling with the close-range mechanics of Chaos difficulty.

### Fraux & Fediel (A-Tier)
*   **Fraux:** As a pactbearer to The Devil, Fraux excels at dealing fire-element damage over time. Her skills apply unique debuffs that increase the damage the boss takes from all sources.
*   **Fediel:** Fediel creates dark barrier zones that protect allies while dealing continuous damage to enemies inside the zone. Her DPS is highly dependent on keeping the boss positioned within her fields.

### Gallanza & Maglielle (A-Tier / B-Tier)
*   **Gallanza:** A spear-wielding melee fighter who gains increased attack power as his health decreases. While his burst damage is high, the risk of running low HP in Chaos difficulty makes him difficult to play.
*   **Maglielle:** Primarily a support character who summons flying weapons to attack automatically. While her personal DPS is lower, her utility shields and healing make her an invaluable asset to any party.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Who is the absolute best DPS character in Endless Ragnarok?
Sandalphon is widely considered the **Endless Ragnarok best DPS character** due to his Supreme Primarch Mode. His ability to deal multi-hit lightstep sword wave damage while maintaining high mobility allows him to bypass damage caps more efficiently than any other character in the game.

### How do I unlock Weapon Transcendence?
To unlock Weapon Transcendence, you must first complete the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue. Once completed, you can transcend fully enhanced Level 150 weapons at the blacksmith using materials obtained from Fatebreaker quests and Conflux runs.

### Can the new DLC characters compete with the top-tier base characters?
Yes. Beatrix, in particular, has shown potential to reach S-tier damage levels thanks to her Delta Clock mechanic. Eustace and Fraux are also highly viable A-tier options that offer excellent utility alongside their damage output.

### What Sigils should I prioritize for a pure DPS build?
You should prioritize stacking **Damage Cap V+** Sigils until you reach the maximum cap. Once capped, slot in **War Elemental+** and your character's specific **Warpath+** Sigil to further multiply your damage output. Pair these with Critical Hit Rate Sigils to ensure consistent critical strikes.
