## Deep Dive: SS & S-Tier Character Analysis

### Sandalphon (SS-Tier)
Sandalphon stands undisputed as the **Endless Ragnarok best character for Conflux** runs. His Supreme Primarch Mode transforms his basic attacks into ranged lightstep sword waves. This allows him to clear entire rooms of mobs from a safe distance. In the solo roguelite mode, keeping distance while dealing maximum damage is invaluable. 

His Master Trait *Insight* further boosts his dodge window and adds supplementary damage to his lightstep attacks. When entering Supreme Primarch Mode, his skills receive reduced cooldowns, allowing him to cycle through his offensive abilities rapidly. His self-heal skill, *Aetherial Shield*, also grants him a barrier that absorbs incoming damage, making him incredibly forgiving to play in deeper, high-damage depths.

### Charlotta (S-Tier)
Charlotta excels due to her *Konig Schild* and her ability to maintain invincibility through continuous attacking. In the deep levels of the Conflux where enemies can one-hit weaker characters, Charlotta's ability to parry and counter-attack makes her nearly unkillable. 

Her *Noble Stance* Master Trait path increases her attack speed and reduces the startup frames of her parry. This synergy allows her to stay glued to bosses like Beelzebub or Ragnalia, ignoring their high-damage area-of-effect (AoE) attacks while maintaining maximum DPS uptime.

### Vaseraga (S-Tier)
Utilizing Vaseraga requires a specific **Endless Ragnarok Conflux build guide** focus. His *Undying* status buff prevents him from falling below 1 HP for a set duration. When combined with Conflux power-ups that increase attack power at low HP, Vaseraga becomes a high-risk, high-reward powerhouse that can melt bosses in seconds.

His heavy attacks charge quickly when his Master Trait *Crux* is fully unlocked, allowing him to stagger large monsters and bosses with ease. The key to playing Vaseraga in the Conflux is managing the cooldown of his *Undying* skill and choosing power-ups that boost his defense and attack power when his HP is low.

## Optimal Pre-Run Build Setup and Sigils

Before stepping through the gateway, your gear setup must be optimized. Although you collect power-ups during the run, your starting Sigils and Weapon Transcendence levels dictate your baseline stats. For a successful run, your build should balance damage cap increases with survivability traits.

| Sigil Name | Tier | Recommended Level | Primary Effect in Conflux |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Warpath+** | S | 15 | Character-specific unique performance boost (e.g., Sandalphon's faster gauge gain) |
| **War Elemental+** | S | 15 | Converts all attacks to the enemy's elemental weakness |
| **Alpha / Beta / Gamma** | A | 15 | Enhances Skybound Art damage and provides shields/cooldown reduction |
| **Autorevive V+** | A | 15 | Automatically revives the player once per encounter (crucial for solo runs) |
| **Potion Hoarder V+** | A | 15 | Increases the number of usable potions (essential since green potions are scarce) |

To unlock the full potential of these builds, players must invest in [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/) to break past standard damage caps. Transcending a Terminus weapon to Stage V unlocks additional swappable traits, allowing you to slot in extra Critical Hit Rate or Cooldown Reduction without sacrificing primary Sigil slots.

### Step-by-Step Pre-Run Preparation
1. **Select your Weapon**: Equip a fully transcended Terminus weapon. The raw stats and damage cap increases are mandatory for clearing the deeper depths within the 20-minute time limit.
2. **Equip Survivability Sigils**: Ensure *Potion Hoarder V+* and *Autorevive V+* are slotted. In solo play, these Sigils act as your safety net.
3. **Configure Master Traits**: Allocate your Master Points into the *Insight* or *Essence* paths depending on your character. For Sandalphon, focus on the *Insight* path to maximize his Supreme Primarch Mode duration.
4. **Choose your Summons**: Since summons are only available in Chaos-difficulty quests and higher, equip high-stat primal beasts. Having Lucilius or Rolan equipped provides passive bonuses to your attack and defense stats during the run.

## Conflux Power-Up Selection Strategy

As you progress through the randomly generated rooms, you will be prompted to choose power-ups before entering each new area. These power-ups remain active until you exit the gateway. Making the correct **Endless Ragnarok Conflux character picks** is only half the battle; you must also choose upgrades that synergize with your character's kit.

| Power-Up Name | Common Rarity Effect | Legendary Rarity Effect | Best Synergy Characters |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Drain Boost** | Recovers 1% HP per hit | Recovers 3% HP per hit + 10% Def | Charlotta, Lancelot, Beatrix |
| **SBA Charge Rate** | +10% SBA Gain | +25% SBA Gain & +15% Skill DMG | Sandalphon, Cagliostro |
| **Glass Cannon** | +30% ATK, but get dizzy on hit | +50% ATK, but get dizzy on hit | Vaseraga (during Undying), Zeta |
| **Cooldown Accelerator** | -5% Skill Cooldowns | -15% Skill Cooldowns & +10% Crit | Io, Captain, Maglielle |

When navigating rooms, prioritize "Treasure" and "Rare Monster" rooms over standard combat rooms. Rare monsters yield higher-tier enhancement materials and drop legendary power-ups. When facing the final boss of a depth (such as The World or Beelzebub), ensure you have at least one defensive power-up active to prevent getting one-shot by their arena-wide AoE attacks.

### Room Priority Guide
* **Priority 1: Rare Monster Rooms**: These rooms feature tough mini-bosses but guarantee high-tier power-ups and Weapon Transcendence materials.
* **Priority 2: Treasure Rooms**: Safe rooms that contain chests with Sigils and upgrade materials. Always choose these if your HP is low and you lack healing options.
* **Priority 3: Combat Rooms**: Standard wave-clearing rooms. Use these to charge your Skybound Art (SBA) gauge before facing a boss.

## FAQ

### What is the fastest way to clear the Conflux?
To achieve the fastest clear times, choose Sandalphon or Charlotta and prioritize "Rare Monster" rooms. These rooms yield the highest-tier power-ups, which exponentially increase your damage output as you descend deeper into the gateway. Ensure your Terminus weapon is fully upgraded before starting.

### Can I use Summons in the Conflux?
Yes, but only in Chaos-difficulty quests and higher. You can equip up to four summons, but only one can be active in combat at a time. Controllable summons like Lucilius and Rolan are highly effective for dealing massive burst damage to bosses like The World.

### Which Master Trait path should I prioritize for solo runs?
For most characters, the *Insight* path is preferred because it focuses on utility, cooldown reduction, and evasion. However, characters like Vaseraga benefit more from the *Crux* path, which enhances charged attack damage and stagger values.

### How do I unlock the Conflux gateways?
You must complete the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue. Once completed, the gateways will appear in Seedhollow and the Tredame Sanctum, allowing you to access the solo roguelite mode.
