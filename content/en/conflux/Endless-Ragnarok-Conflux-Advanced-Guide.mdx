## Depth Scaling Mechanics and Enemy Stat Progression

The Conflux scales dynamically as you descend through its floors. Each depth level increases enemy health, attack power, and break resistance while introducing deadlier status ailments. Upon reaching **Endless Ragnarok Conflux deeper depths** (Depth 40 and beyond), standard enemies acquire the *Ragnarok Aura*, which reduces incoming damage by 30% unless they are afflicted with a status ailment or placed in a Break state.

Stat scaling is not linear; it spikes significantly at milestones (every 10 depths). The table below outlines the exact HP and ATK multipliers you will encounter, alongside the unlockable reward tiers.

### Depth Scaling & Milestone Rewards

| Depth Range | HP Multiplier | ATK Multiplier | Key Boss Encounters | Unique Drops / Rewards |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Depths 1–10** | 1.0x | 1.0x | Griffon, Golem | Refinement Shards, Low-tier Sigils |
| **Depths 11–20** | 2.5x | 1.8x | Furycane, Nazarbonju | Silver Centrums, Dread Crystals |
| **Depths 21–30** | 5.0x | 3.2x | Ragnalia (Herald of Doom) | Fatebreaker Keys, Astral Shards |
| **Depths 31–40** | 10.0x | 5.5x | Seofon & Tweyen (Dual Boss) | Transcendence Essence, S-tier Sigils |
| **Depths 41–50+** | 22.0x | 12.0x | Beelzebub (Projection), The World | Ragnarok Crystals, Warpath+ Sigils |

To counter the massive health pools at Depth 40+, your character must utilize [Weapon Transcendence](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/) upgrades to push their damage cap beyond standard limits. Standard Level 100 characters without Transcendence traits will struggle to clear combat rooms within the strict time limits imposed by higher depths.

## Optimizing Resonance Points and Gateway Blessings

Resonance is the core power-up mechanic within the Conflux. Before entering each room, you are presented with three Gateway Blessings. Each blessing belongs to one of three Resonance families: **Insight**, **Essence**, or **Crux**. Selecting blessings of the same family builds Resonance Points (RP), which unlock powerful passive synergy buffs at specific thresholds.

For an effective run, do not simply pick the highest rarity blessing. Instead, focus on building a single Resonance family to its maximum threshold to unlock game-changing perks.

### Resonance Synergy Families

| Resonance Family | Focus | Tier 1 Buff (5 RP) | Tier 2 Buff (15 RP) | Tier 3 Buff (30 RP) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Insight (Blue)** | Skill CD & Utility | +10% Skill Cooldown Cut | +20% Link Temp Gain | Skills bypass 20% Enemy DEF |
| **Essence (Red)** | Raw DPS & Cap Up | +15% Attack Power | +10% Damage Cap | Adds 15% Echo to all attacks |
| **Crux (Yellow)** | Survival & Summons | +20% Max HP & Guts | +15% SBA Gauge Fill Rate | Summons deal 50% more damage |

### Step-by-Step Resonance Optimization Strategy

1. **Initialize the Run:** Choose a starting blessing that matches your character's primary scaling method. For high-frequency skill users like Cagliostro or Lancelot, start with an **Insight** blessing. For burst DPS like Vaseraga or Beatrix, start with **Essence**.
2. **Prioritize Gateway Types:** Choose Gateways that offer "Resonance Catalysts." These special rooms do not grant standard blessings but double your existing RP for your highest-represented family.
3. **Reroll Management:** Save your Gateway Rerolls (obtained by clearing Rare Monster rooms) for Depth 30+. You will need them to force Tier 4 blessings (e.g., *Essence of the Founder* or *Crux of the Protector*) which only appear when your corresponding Resonance is above 20 points.
4. **Summon Synergy:** If running a **Crux** build, ensure you equip high-impact passive summons like Lucilius or Rolan. At 30 Crux RP, your summoned primal beasts can deplete a boss's overdrive bar in a single active phase.

## Multiple Run Chaining and Time-Saving Strategies

To farm the vast amount of materials required for upgrading all 28 characters, players must implement a highly efficient **Endless Ragnarok Conflux multiple runs strategy**. Chaining runs utilizes the *Resonance Preservation* upgrade from the [Master Traits System](/en/master-traits/master-traits-explained/), allowing you to carry over 20% of your accumulated Resonance Points into your next run, provided you exit via a Gateway rather than dying.

To optimize your farming speed, follow these **Endless Ragnarok Conflux efficiency tips**:

* **Skip Optional Combat Rooms:** If a floor offers a choice between a Combat Room and a Treasure Room, always choose the Treasure Room unless you require Resonance Points. Treasure Rooms take 10 seconds to clear, whereas combat rooms can take up to 2 minutes at high depths.
* **Target Rare Monsters:** Keep an eye out for the *Gold Slimepede* or *Prisma Slime* rooms. These rooms grant massive amounts of XP, master points, and rare Fatebreaker keys needed for Weapon Transcendence.
* **Leverage SS-Tier Speedrunners:** Characters like Sandalphon can clear rooms in seconds. By entering Supreme Primarch Mode and spamming lightstep sword waves, Sandalphon bypasses many of the defensive shields that slow down other characters.

### Room Priority for Fast Clears

| Room Icon / Type | Time to Clear | Primary Reward | Priority Level |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Treasure Vault** | < 10 seconds | Sigil Upgrade Materials, Curios | **Critical** (Always pick) |
| **Rare Monster (Gold Icon)** | 1–2 minutes | Fatebreaker Keys, Large XP Pots | **High** (Pick if available) |
| **Gateway Catalyst** | < 5 seconds | +5 Resonance Points, Buff Upgrade | **High** (If building Resonance) |
| **Standard Combat** | 2–3 minutes | Low-tier Refinement Materials | **Low** (Avoid if possible) |
| **Boss Arena** | 3–5 minutes | Transcendence Materials, S+ Sigils | **Mandatory** (Every 10 depths) |

## Target Farming for Weapon Transcendence and Master Traits

Weapon Transcendence requires materials that drop exclusively from high-depth Conflux runs and Fatebreaker quests. Each weapon type (Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, Terminus) requires a different combination of materials.

To farm efficiently, you must target specific depth brackets. For example, *Transcendence Essence*—required for unlocking Slot IV traits—only drops from bosses defeated at Depth 40 or higher on Chaos difficulty.

### Transcendence Material Drop Locations

| Material Name | Required For | Primary Source (Conflux Depth) | Drop Rate (Chaos Difficulty) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Fatebreaker Shard** | Transcendence Levels 1-5 | Depths 21–30 (Any Room) | 45% per chest |
| **Astral Shard** | Transcendence Levels 6-10 | Depths 31–40 (Boss Chest) | 30% per run |

When target farming, ensure you run characters who can easily break boss parts. Melee powerhouses like Gallanza or Siegfried excel at breaking the headpieces of Ragnalia and Beelzebub, which doubles the drop rate of *Astral Shards*.

## Chaos-Difficulty Survival and Boss Countermeasures

At Depth 40+, the game transitions into [Chaos Difficulty](/en/bosses/chaos-difficulty-boss-tips/). In this bracket, bosses like Beelzebub and The World can defeat even fully upgraded characters in a single hit. Survival requires a combination of defensive Sigils, precise use of the Summon System, and exploiting character-specific mechanics.

### Essential Sigil Setup for Deeper Depths

* **Warpath+**: Provides character-specific unique multipliers that do not count toward the standard damage cap.
* **Alpha / Beta / Gamma**: These Sigils should be mixed to maximize SBA damage and provide shield barriers upon using Skybound Arts.
* **Flight over Fight**: While it reduces your attack power by 50%, the infinite dodge window it provides is essential for characters like Maglielle or Fraux when navigating bullet-hell phases in the deeper depths.

### Utilizing the Summon System

Lyria's ability to summon primal beasts is a critical tool for surviving boss overdrive phases. When a boss enters Bloodthirst mode (reducing all incoming damage and executing map-wide attacks), summon **Lucilius** to cast *Paradise Lost*. This action inflicts a temporary Slow status on the boss, allowing your party to safely navigate the arena or build up their SBA gauges.

If you are playing solo, ensure your AI companions are equipped with passive summons that boost defense, such as *Tiamat Omega* or *Grand Order*, to passive mitigate damage during chaotic phases. For more detailed strategies on managing high-level encounters, refer to the official [Cygames Official Relink Portal](https://relink.granbluefantasy.jp/en/).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What is the fastest way to clear Depth 50 in the Conflux?
The fastest method is using a high-DPS character like Sandalphon or Beatrix with a fully maximized **Essence (Red)** Resonance build. Prioritize Treasure and Catalyst rooms to reach 30 Essence RP before Depth 40, which grants a 15% Echo buff to all attacks, allowing you to bypass boss damage shields quickly.

### Can I run the Conflux with a full party of friends?
No, the Conflux is a solo-only roguelite mode. However, you can bring up to three AI companions. Ensure your AI companions are equipped with supportive Master Traits (Insight path) to provide constant healing, shields, and defense buffs while you focus on dealing damage.

### How do I unlock the gateway to the Conflux?
To unlock the Conflux gateways in Seedhollow and Tredame Sanctum, you must first complete the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Epilogue of the Endless Ragnarok expansion. Once completed, the gateways will activate automatically.

### What happens if I die during a Conflux run?
If you die and run out of revives, the run ends immediately. You will keep any items collected up to the last cleared floor, but you will lose all accumulated Resonance Points and fail to receive the completion bonus for that depth run. To preserve your Resonance for a multiple-run chain, you must manually exit the run using a gateway after defeating a boss.
