## Unlocking the Expansion and Campaign Preparation

Before you can dive into the new storyline, you must meet specific progression milestones in the base game. The expansion content does not automatically trigger upon purchasing the DLC; you must have cleared the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the transition questline. Once cleared, a new gateway will appear in both Seedhollow and the Tredame Sanctum, prompting you to begin the expansion campaign.

For players struggling with the jump in difficulty, these **Endless Ragnarok story progression tips** will outline the optimal setup. First, ensure your primary party members are at Level 100 with their weapon trees fully upgraded. The expansion introduces a significant spike in enemy damage and health pools, meaning under-geared characters will struggle even in the opening chapters.

### Recommended Party Composition

When building your team, incorporating a mix of high-damage dealers and utility supports is essential. 

*   **Main DPS**: [Sandalphon](/en/characters/sandalphon-guide/) remains the premier choice due to his Supreme Primarch Mode, which allows him to spam lightstep sword waves for massive damage. Alternatively, the new DLC character [Beatrix](/en/characters/beatrix-guide/) excels at high-risk, high-reward DPS using her Delta Clock mechanic to manipulate her attack speed and damage output.
*   **Support/Utility**: Cagliostro or the new DLC character Maglielle provide invaluable healing, shields, and attack buffs. Maglielle's flying weapons offer consistent chip damage while keeping her safely out of melee range.
*   **Crowd Control/Stun**: Vaseraga or Siegfried can easily break enemy guards and build the stun gauge, which is vital for triggering Link Attacks during boss encounters.

Applying these **Endless Ragnarok story progression tips** early on will save you from hitting walls during the campaign's major boss fights.

## Endless Ragnarok Story Chapters Walkthrough

The **Endless Ragnarok story chapters** introduce a structured progression that alternates between narrative-focused instanced zones and open-ended combat trials. Following this **Endless Ragnarok story walkthrough** will help you navigate each chapter efficiently.

### Chapter 11: The Looming Shadow

The story begins in Seedhollow, where mysterious dimensional rifts have begun tearing through the skies. Speak to Rolan at the dock to initiate the quest "Distortions in the Sky." 

1.  **Investigate the Rifts**: Follow the quest markers through the streets of Seedhollow. You will face waves of Astral-aligned enemies. Use area-of-effect (AoE) skills to clear the lesser minions quickly.
2.  **The Vanguard of Chaos**: Near the castle gates, you will confront the Vanguard of Chaos. This boss uses wide, sweeping sword strikes and teleports across the arena. 
    *   *Strategy*: Wait for the boss to finish its three-hit combo before committing to heavy attacks. If you are using [Beatrix](/en/characters/beatrix-guide/), activate her Delta Clock during the boss's recovery animations to maximize your damage windows.
3.  **Unlock The Conflux**: Defeating the Vanguard unlocks access to the Conflux gateways in Seedhollow.

### Chapter 12: Echoes of the Arcarum

Travel to the Tredame Sanctum to investigate reports of an ancient machine activating. This chapter introduces you to the Arcarum creation, The World.

1.  **Navigate the Sanctum**: The path is linear but filled with traps and automated defense turrets. Destroy the power generators feeding the turrets before engaging the security droids.
2.  **Boss Fight: The World**: The World is a multi-phase boss that utilizes elemental shifting.
    *   *Phase 1*: The boss uses Fire and Water attacks. Avoid the circular ground indicators.
    *   *Phase 2 (50% HP)*: The World shifts to Earth and Wind. It will shield itself, requiring you to destroy four elemental pillars at the corners of the arena. Focus on one pillar at a time using high-stun skills.
3.  **Unlock the Summon System**: After defeating The World, Lyria gains the ability to summon primal beasts in combat, a crucial mechanic for the later stages of the campaign.

### Chapter 13: Heralds of Doom

This chapter takes you high into the Phantagrande Skydoms, where you face Ragnalia, one of the Heralds of Doom.

1.  **Airship Defense**: You must defend the Grandcypher from boarding parties. Keep enemies away from the main mast; if the ship's durability drops to zero, you will fail the mission.
2.  **Boss Fight: Ragnalia**: Ragnalia is a massive, flying dragon-like entity that attacks with Void energy.
    *   *Strategy*: Ragnalia spends a lot of time airborne. Use ranged characters like Eustace or Io to deal consistent damage. When Ragnalia channels "Calamity Breath," run to the sides of the ship to avoid the massive linear beam.
3.  **Unlock Master Traits**: Defeating Ragnalia unlocks the Master Traits system, allowing you to customize your characters' playstyles via the Insight, Essence, and Crux paths once they reach Level 100.

### Chapter 14: The Chaos Singularity

The final chapter of the main expansion campaign takes place within the Astral Rift, leading to a direct confrontation with Beelzebub.

1.  **Rift Descent**: Fight your way through waves of elite enemies. Do not rush; pull enemies in small groups to avoid getting overwhelmed.
2.  **Boss Fight: Beelzebub (Normal)**: Beelzebub is highly aggressive, utilizing fast teleportation, counter-attacks, and wide-area void spells.
    *   *Strategy*: Do not attack blindly. Beelzebub will frequently enter a parry stance; hitting him during this window triggers a devastating counter-attack. When he casts "Chaos Calamity," seek shelter behind the debris scattered around the arena.
3.  **Campaign Completion**: Defeating Beelzebub completes the main story arc of the expansion, unlocking the post-game progression systems.

To complete all **Endless Ragnarok story chapters**, you must defeat these bosses in order, as each encounter unlocks vital systems needed to survive the next.

## The Endless Ragnarok Epilogue Story and Chaos Difficulty

Once the credits roll, the real challenge begins. The **Endless Ragnarok epilogue story** content serves as the gateway to the endgame, introducing the Fatebreaker quests and the brutal Chaos difficulty tier. While the question of **Endless Ragnarok how long is the story** can be answered with a 10-hour estimate for the campaign, the epilogue adds dozens of hours of high-difficulty progression.

### Unlocking the Epilogue

To initiate the epilogue, return to the quest counter in Seedhollow and select the new questline marked with a red exclamation point. This will lead to a series of investigations into the remnants of Beelzebub's forces.

1.  **Fatebreaker Quests**: These are specialized, high-difficulty hunts that reward you with Weapon Transcendence materials. You must clear these quests to upgrade your gear past the base game's limits.
2.  **Seofon & Tweyen Boss Fight**: During the epilogue, you will face the duo of Seofon and Tweyen in a mock battle. This fight tests your ability to manage multiple high-threat targets simultaneously.
    *   *Strategy*: Focus on Tweyen first. Her ranged arrows can inflict paralyze and freeze, leaving you vulnerable to Seofon's fast sword rushes. Use crowd control or summons to keep Seofon distracted while your DPS characters burst Tweyen down.
3.  **Chaos Difficulty**: Completing the epilogue unlocks Chaos difficulty. In this tier, enemies gain new attack patterns, significantly higher speed, and the ability to one-hit kill characters who do not have optimized defensive sigils.

## Integrating The Conflux and the Summon System

To progress through the tougher stages of the campaign and epilogue, players must engage with two new core mechanics: The Conflux and the Summon System. 

### The Conflux Gateways

The Conflux is a roguelite solo mode accessed via gateways in Seedhollow or Tredame Sanctum. Runs take approximately 20 minutes and consist of randomly generated combat, treasure, and rare monster rooms. Before entering each area, you choose from a selection of temporary power-ups that remain active until you exit.

A final set of **Endless Ragnarok story progression tips** revolves around farming The Conflux early. The deeper you go, the better the rewards. It is the fastest source of the enhancement materials required for Weapon Transcendence. If you find yourself stuck on a story boss, spend an hour running The Conflux to boost your stats.

### The Summon System

Available only in Chaos-difficulty quests and select story encounters, the Summon System allows Lyria to call upon primal beasts to aid the party. Each player can bring up to four summons, though only one can be active on the field at any given time.

*   **Controllable Summons**: Lucilius and Rolan can be directly controlled once summoned, allowing you to execute powerful unique skills.
*   **Primal Burst**: Triggered when all four party members activate their Skybound Arts during Link Time while a summon is active. This deals massive elemental damage and temporarily stuns the boss.
*   **Passive Bonuses**: Even when not actively summoned, equipped primal beasts provide passive stat boosts to your party members.

## Optimal Builds for Ending the Ragnarok Threat

To successfully clear the epilogue and survive Chaos difficulty, you must optimize your gear and sigils. The expansion introduces Weapon Transcendence, allowing you to upgrade fully enhanced weapons beyond their standard limits.

### Weapon Transcendence

You can transcend six weapon types: Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, and Terminus. Transcending a weapon unlocks swappable trait slots (Slot II up to level 25, Slot IV up to level 10) and provides unique aesthetic effects. 

1.  **Prioritize Terminus Weapons**: If you have a Terminus weapon from the base game, transcend it first. The raw stat increases and the expansion of the damage cap are unmatched.
2.  **Collect Fatebreaker Materials**: Run the Fatebreaker quests regularly to gather the specific materials required for each transcendence level.

### Sigil Setup for Chaos Difficulty

While the early **Endless Ragnarok story chapters** focus on narrative progression, the endgame requires precise builds. You should aim for a mix of S-tier and A-tier sigils to maximize your survivability and damage output.

*   **S-Tier Sigils**: Look for *Warpath+*, *War Elemental+*, and character-specific sigils. These bypass standard damage caps and provide unique modifiers to your character's core mechanics.
*   **A-Tier Sigils**: *Critical Hit Rate V+*, *Alpha/Beta/Gamma* sigils from the base game remain highly effective for optimizing your damage rotations.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I unlock the Endless Ragnarok story chapters?
To unlock the expansion story chapters, you must complete the base game's main campaign and clear the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage." Once completed, a new red quest marker will appear in Seedhollow, allowing you to begin Chapter 11.

### What is the fastest way to farm Weapon Transcendence materials during the epilogue?
The fastest way to farm Weapon Transcendence materials is by running [The Conflux](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) gateways. Deeper runs yield high quantities of enhancement materials. Additionally, clearing specific Fatebreaker quests on Chaos difficulty rewards you with guaranteed Transcendence Jewels.

### Can I play the entire story walkthrough in co-op?
While the main campaign chapters can be played solo with AI companions, all post-game content, including Fatebreaker quests, Chaos-difficulty bosses, and epilogue hunts, fully support four-player online crossplay across PS5, PS4, Switch 2, and PC.

### Which characters are best suited for clearing Chaos difficulty story bosses?
Sandalphon is highly recommended due to his Supreme Primarch Mode's high damage and mobility. For support, Maglielle is excellent for keeping the team alive with shields. If you prefer high-risk DPS, Beatrix can melt boss health pools quickly if you manage her Delta Clock mechanic correctly.
