## Accessing the Expansion and Campaign Progression

Returning players must complete specific milestones before they can jump into the new DLC content. The expansion does not immediately unlock upon loading your old save file unless you have cleared the base game's final endgame hurdles. 

To start the Endless Ragnarok epilogue campaign, you must first complete the Proud-difficulty quest **"The Tale of Bahamut's Rage"**. Once cleared, a new questline will appear at the Quest Counter in either Seedhollow or Folca, labeled with a gold expansion icon. Completing this epilogue unlocks **Chaos difficulty**, the new highest difficulty tier in the game, where enemies can easily defeat under-geared characters in a single hit. 

For players wondering about **Endless Ragnarok how to access expansion** gateways for the new roguelite mode, you must travel to either Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. Look for the glowing green Conflux Gateways near the docks.

## Six New Playable Characters and Combat Archetypes

A core part of **Endless Ragnarok what is included** in the paid DLC is the addition of six new playable characters, bringing the total roster to 28. These characters introduce unique mechanics that shake up the established endgame meta. If you want to build these characters efficiently, check out our [character build guides](/en/builds/all-character-builds/) for optimal Sigil setups.

### Beatrix (DPS - Fire)
Beatrix utilizes the **Delta Clock** mechanic. By performing timed attacks at the precise moment her blade glows, she speeds up her attack animations and increases her critical hit damage. Maintaining her Delta Clock at maximum speed requires precise rhythm but yields some of the highest sustained DPS in the game.

### Eustace (Ranged - Earth)
Eustace acts as a high-speed ranged battery. His unique mechanic allows him to charge his rifle attacks by landing consecutive normal attacks. He can discharge this energy to paralyze enemies or fill the party’s Link Temp gauge rapidly, making him highly valuable in organized group play.

### Fraux (Melee - Fire)
As the Pactbearer to the Arcarum summon The Devil, Fraux alternates between close-range martial arts and summoning fiery fist strikes from her partner. Her unique mechanic, **Pact Link**, increases her attack speed and defense whenever she executes a perfect dodge or perfect guard.

### Fediel (Support/DPS - Dark)
One of the Six Dragons, Fediel controls the battlefield by creating dark fields that shield allies and inflict poison on enemies. Her shield scales directly with her maximum HP, making her a hybrid defensive support who can still deal respectable damage using her dark energy blasts.

### Gallanza (Melee - Earth)
Gallanza is a high-defense bruiser who gains attack power as his health decreases. His unique mechanic, **Spear Mastery**, allows him to shrug off light attacks while charging heavy, uninterruptible thrusts that deal massive stagger damage to bosses.

### Maglielle (Support/Ranged - Light)
Maglielle commands an array of flying weapons that attack automatically while she channels healing and barrier spells for the party. She can hover above the battlefield to avoid ground-based hazards, making her an excellent support character for chaotic boss fights.

## The Conflux: The Roguelite Endgame Loop

The Conflux is a solo-only roguelite game mode accessed via the gateways in Seedhollow or Tredame Sanctum. It serves as the fastest source of enhancement materials required for the new Weapon Transcendence system. 

When you enter The Conflux, your character's level is temporarily set to 1, and your equipped Sigils are disabled. You must choose temporary power-ups (called **Conflux Essences**) before entering each room. A standard run consists of clearing randomly generated rooms containing combat challenges, treasure chests, or rare monsters. Each run lasts approximately 20 minutes, culminating in a boss fight at the end of the depth.

### Conflux Room Types and Strategy
1. **Combat Rooms**: Standard rooms where you defeat waves of enemies. These award basic experience points and minor Conflux Essences.
2. **Treasure Rooms**: Safe rooms containing chests with rare Sigil drops and currency.
3. **Rare Monster Rooms**: High-risk rooms featuring mutated versions of standard bosses. Clearing these rooms guarantees high-tier rewards, such as Transcendence Ores and Master Star Grimoires.

The deeper you descend into The Conflux, the harder the enemies become, but the drop rate of S-tier Sigils, including the new **Warpath+** and **War Elemental+** Sigils, increases significantly. For a detailed breakdown of optimal builds for this mode, read our [Conflux roguelite guide](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/).

## Weapon Transcendence and Master Traits

For returning players looking to optimize their damage output, the progression cap has been significantly raised. This **Endless Ragnarok patch notes guide** highlights how weapon progression and character leveling have evolved beyond the base game limits.

### Weapon Transcendence
Once you have fully upgraded a weapon to Level 150, you can now unlock **Weapon Transcendence** using materials obtained from Fatebreaker quests and The Conflux. Transcendence allows you to upgrade all six weapon types (Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, and Terminus) past their previous limits. 

Transcendence unlocks two new customizable slots:
* **Slot II Traits**: Max level 25. Allows you to slot in utility traits like Cooldown Reduction or Cascade.
* **Slot IV Traits**: Max level 10. Allows you to slot in rare traits like War Elemental or character-specific Sigil effects.
* **Slot EX**: Unlocks unique aesthetic weapon glows and a flat stat increase to Attack and HP.

### Master Traits
Upon reaching character Level 100, players can access the **Master Traits** menu. Each of the 28 characters has a unique Master Grid consisting of three distinct paths: **Insight**, **Essence**, and **Crux**. Unlocking nodes on these paths requires Master Star Grimoires and MSP (Mana Points).

* **Insight Path**: Enhances character-specific skills and reduces cooldowns.
* **Essence Path**: Boosts base stats, including critical hit rate and damage cap limits.
* **Crux Path**: Unlocks Rank Perks and Rank Style upgrades that alter how a character's primary mechanic functions (e.g., widening the timing window for Beatrix's Delta Clock).

## The Summon System and Chaos Difficulty Bosses

The combat flow in high-level quests has been updated with the introduction of the **Summon System**. Available only in Chaos-difficulty quests and higher, this mechanic allows Lyria to summon powerful primal beasts directly into combat.

### Summon Mechanics
Each player can equip up to four summons in their loadout, but only one summon can be active on the field at any given time. While equipped, summons provide passive stat bonuses to your character. During combat, filling the Link Gauge to 100% and initiating Link Time allows players to trigger a **Primal Burst** if all four party members activate their Skybound Arts (SBA) in sequence. This summons a controllable primal beast, such as **Lucilius** or **Rolan**, to deal massive damage and apply temporary invincibility to the party.

### New Chaos Difficulty Bosses
The expansion introduces three major boss encounters that require mastery of the Summon System:
1. **Beelzebub**: The primary antagonist of the expansion. He utilizes screen-wide gravity wells and energy blades. Players must save their SBA chains to interrupt his "Chaos Legion" ultimate attack.
2. **The World**: An Arcarum creation that tests your party's positioning. The World constantly shifts elements, requiring players to adapt their defensive strategies mid-fight.
3. **Ragnalia**: The Herald of Doom. This boss features a massive stagger bar that can only be efficiently broken using Earth-element characters or summon attacks.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I unlock the new DLC characters?
You can unlock the six new DLC characters by purchasing the Endless Ragnarok expansion pack or buying their individual character tickets from the platform store. Once purchased, redeem the vouchers at Siero's Knickknack Shop in-game to add them to your roster.

### Can I play The Conflux with friends in co-op?
No, The Conflux is strictly a solo-only roguelite mode. It is designed to test your individual skill and character build optimization. However, the materials you earn in The Conflux can be used to upgrade your gear for co-op play in Chaos-difficulty quests.

### What are the best weapons to transcend first?
We recommend transcending your **Terminus** weapons first, as they provide the highest base stats and unlock both Slot II and Slot IV trait upgrades. If you have not unlocked Terminus weapons yet, transcending an **Ascension** weapon is a strong alternative for clearing early Chaos-difficulty content.

### What is the level cap in Endless Ragnarok?
While character levels remain capped at Level 100, your overall power is extended through the Master Traits grid and Weapon Transcendence, which allows weapons to reach Level 200. This significantly increases your damage cap beyond the limits of the base game.
