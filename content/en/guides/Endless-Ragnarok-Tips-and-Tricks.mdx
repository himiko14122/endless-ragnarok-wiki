## Character Selection and Progression Tips

Selecting your primary character determines how you approach the early hours of the DLC. With 28 total characters available, including 6 new DLC additions, understanding the meta hierarchy will save you valuable resources. 

### 1. Identify the Best Starter Characters
If you are looking for the **Endless Ragnarok best starter character**, Sandalphon stands out as the premier choice. Sandalphon's Supreme Primarch Mode allows him to spam lightstep sword waves, offering massive DPS while maintaining high mobility. For players who prefer traditional melee or ranged support, Cagliostro and Zeta offer excellent safety nets due to their invincibility frames and healing capabilities.

### 2. Learn the New DLC Roster Mechanics
The six new DLC characters bring unique combat systems that require specific playstyles:
*   **Beatrix:** Relies on the Delta Clock mechanic. You must time your attacks to the ticking clock interface to maximize her damage output.
*   **Eustace:** A premier ranged fighter who excels at keeping distance while dealing consistent thunder-elemental damage.
*   **Fraux:** Melee fighter bound to the primal beast The Devil, focusing on high-frequency fire combos.
*   **Fediel:** A defensive Dark-element powerhouse utilizing Six Dragons barriers to shield the party.
*   **Gallanza:** A heavy spear user who gains attack speed and power as his HP decreases.
*   **Maglielle:** A support character who summons flying weapons to attack while providing shields and healing zones.

### 3. Prioritize Level 100 Character Masteries
Do not spread your resources too thin. Focus on leveling one character to level 100 immediately to unlock their Master Traits. Master Traits are divided into three distinct paths: Insight, Essence, and Crux. Unlocking these paths grants access to unique Rank Perks and Rank Style upgrades that completely change how skills function.

### 4. Unlock Character-Specific Warpath+ Sigils First
Each character has a unique Warpath+ Sigil that drastically enhances their core mechanic. For instance, Sandalphon’s Warpath+ Sigil extends his Supreme Primarch Mode duration. These Sigils should be your top priority when trading materials at the Knickknack Shack.

### 5. Level Up Your Sub-Members Evenly
Even if you play solo, your AI teammates inherit your equipped Sigils and weapon stats. Ensure your active party of four has updated defensive Sigils like Aegis and Improved Dodge to prevent them from burning through your shared Life Gauge during high-level quests.

## Mastering the Conflux Roguelite Mode

The Conflux is a solo roguelite mode accessed via gateways located in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. It serves as the fastest source of enhancement materials in the expansion.

### 6. Keep Runs Under 20 Minutes
Efficiency is key in the Conflux. A standard run consists of clearing randomly generated combat, treasure, and rare monster rooms. Aim to complete runs within 20 minutes to maximize your hourly material yield.

### 7. Choose Pre-Area Power-Ups Wisely
Before entering each area, you will be presented with three random power-ups. These power-ups stay active until you exit the Conflux. Always prioritize raw Attack Power and Critical Hit Rate increases early in the run, shifting to Defense and HP Regen upgrades as you descend into deeper, more dangerous floors.

### 8. Hunt Down Rare Monster Rooms
Rare monster rooms feature golden slimes or unique silver beasts. While these fights are tougher and have strict time limits, they drop rare Sigil upgrade materials and Weapon Transcendence tokens that cannot be found anywhere else.

### 9. Optimize Your Gateway Selection
Gateways determine the difficulty and reward scaling of your run. If your character's Power Rating is below 15,000, stick to the Amber Gateways. Once you cross the 20,000 Power Rating threshold, farm the Obsidian Gateways for S-tier Sigil drops.

### 10. Do Not Hoard Conflux Currencies
The tokens earned during Conflux runs do not carry over between major game updates. Spend them immediately at the Tredame Sanctum vendor to purchase Weapon Transcendence materials and High-Grade Sigil Packs.

## Weapon Transcendence and Sigil Optimization

To survive the late-game content of *Endless Ragnarok*, you must push your weapons and Sigils past their base limitations. This is the core loop of **Endless Ragnarok how to get strong fast**.

### 11. Focus on One Weapon Type for Transcendence
Weapon Transcendence allows you to upgrade fully enhanced weapons beyond their level limits. There are 6 weapon types: Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, and Terminus. Focus on your Terminus weapon first, as it provides the highest base damage scaling in the game.

### 12. Maximize Your Swappable Trait Slots
Transcendence unlocks swappable trait slots on your weapons. Slot II can hold traits up to Level 25, while Slot IV can hold traits up to Level 10. Use Slot II for offensive traits like Damage Cap or Critical Damage, and reserve Slot IV for utility traits like Cascade or Quick Cooldown.

### 13. Hunt for S-Tier Sigils
The expansion introduces upgraded S-tier Sigils. Look for **Warpath+** and **War Elemental+** Sigils. War Elemental+ ensures all your attacks hit the enemy's elemental weakness, bypassing traditional damage calculations and providing a flat 20% damage boost.

### 14. Balance Alpha, Beta, and Gamma Sigils
A-tier Sigils like Alpha, Beta, and Gamma provide massive bonuses to your Skybound Arts (SBA) and Link Attacks. Mix and match these Sigils to activate set bonuses:
*   **Alpha:** Increases SBA damage and extends SBA duration.
*   **Beta:** Enhances skill damage and reduces skill cooldowns during Link Time.
*   **Gamma:** Generates a barrier based on damage dealt during SBA.

### 15. Farm Fatebreaker Quests for Transcendence Materials
Fatebreaker quests are high-difficulty boss fights designed specifically to drop Weapon Transcendence materials. You will need to farm these quests repeatedly to gather the rare drops required for Level 110+ weapon upgrades. Check out our detailed [Weapon Transcendence Guide](/en/weapon-transcendence/weapon-transcendence-guide/) for specific drop locations.

## Mastering the Summon System in Chaos Difficulty

Chaos difficulty is the hardest tier in *Endless Ragnarok*, where enemies can easily one-hit weaker characters. To survive, you must master the new Summon System.

### 16. Equip the Right Summons for the Job
Lyria can now summon primal beasts in combat. Each player can bring up to 4 summons into a quest, though only 1 can be active on the field at any given time. Equip passive summons that match your character's element to receive a permanent 10% elemental damage bonus.

### 17. Take Direct Control of Lucilius and Rolan
Unlike standard summons that perform a single attack and vanish, Lucilius and Rolan are fully controllable summons. When activated, your character temporarily swaps out for these powerful figures, allowing you to execute unique attack strings and deploy massive defensive barriers.

### 18. Trigger Primal Bursts During Link Time
A Primal Burst is the ultimate team attack in *Endless Ragnarok*. To trigger it, all 4 party members must activate their Skybound Arts (SBA) in sequence while Link Time is active. This deals catastrophic damage and inflicts a temporary defense down debuff on the boss.

### 19. Save Summons for Boss Overdrive States
When a boss enters their Overdrive state, their defense increases, and they perform high-damage area-of-effect (AoE) attacks. Summoning a primal beast like Bahamut during this phase can quickly deplete the boss's Overdrive gauge, forcing them back into a vulnerable Break state.

### 20. Learn to Perfect Dodge Chaos-Tier Attacks
In Chaos difficulty, blocking is often not enough, as many boss attacks will instantly break your guard. Practice the timing for Perfect Dodges, which grant temporary invincibility and fill your SBA gauge by 5% per successful dodge. Refer to our [Chaos Difficulty Survival Guide](/en/guides/difficulty-settings-explained/) for frame-data breakdowns.

## Advanced Boss Strategies and Endgame Progression

The endgame of *Endless Ragnarok* revolves around defeating powerful bosses like Beelzebub, The World, and the Heralds of Doom (Ragnalia). Use these advanced **Endless Ragnarok new player tips** to secure victory in these encounters.

### 21. Memorize Beelzebub's Phase Transitions
Beelzebub is the primary antagonist of the expansion. At 70% and 30% HP, he will teleport to the center of the arena and cast "Chaos Calamity," a screen-wide attack that requires precise jumping and dodging. Do not try to attack him during this phase; focus entirely on survival.

### 22. Coordinate Elements Against The World
The World is an Arcarum creation that shifts its elemental weakness throughout the fight. Coordinate with your team via the quick-chat menu to ensure you have a diverse spread of elements, or equip the War Elemental+ Sigil to automatically bypass this mechanic.

### 23. Break Ragnalia's Armor Plates
Ragnalia, the Herald of Doom, possesses heavy armor plates that reduce all incoming damage by 50%. Use characters with high stun values, like Vane or Gallanza, to target his legs. Breaking these plates will topple the boss and double your damage output during the DPS phase.

### 24. Divide and Conquer Seofon and Tweyen
In the dual boss fight against Seofon and Tweyen, do not fight them close together. Tweyen will continuously rain down status-inflicting arrows from afar while Seofon engages in close-quarters sword combat. Have your tank character draw Seofon's attention to one side of the arena while the rest of the party quickly bursts down Tweyen.

### 25. Complete Daily Fatebreaker Quests
Make it a habit to complete your daily allotment of Fatebreaker quests. These quests yield double rewards once per day, providing a steady stream of materials needed for Weapon Transcendence and S-tier Sigil refinement.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need to complete the base game to play Endless Ragnarok?
Yes. You must complete the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" in the base game of *Granblue Fantasy: Relink* to unlock the expansion content, new hub areas, and the Conflux gateways.

### Does Endless Ragnarok support crossplay?
Yes, the expansion supports full cross-platform play across all available platforms, including PS5, PS4, Switch 2, and PC (Steam). You can form parties with players on any platform for all multiplayer quests.

### What is the fastest way to get strong in the expansion?
The fastest way to increase your power is by farming the Conflux solo mode to obtain high-tier Sigils, then using the materials gained from Fatebreaker quests to unlock Weapon Transcendence past Level 100.

### Who is the best character for solo play?
Sandalphon is widely considered the best character for solo play due to his high self-sustain, mobility, and the massive damage output of his Supreme Primarch Mode. Cagliostro is also highly recommended for her healing capabilities.
