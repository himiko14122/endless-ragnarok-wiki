## The Conflux: Roguelite Progression and Gateways

The Conflux is a solo-only roguelite game mode accessed via gateways located in Seedhollow or the Tredame Sanctum. Each run lasts approximately 20 minutes and serves as the fastest source of enhancement materials in the expansion. 

When you enter The Conflux, you choose a series of gateways that lead to randomly generated rooms. These rooms fall into three primary categories: Combat, Treasure, and Rare Monster. Before entering each room, you are presented with three random power-ups. These power-ups stay active until you exit the run or fail. As you descend into deeper depths, the enemies scale in difficulty, but the quality of the rewards improves significantly.

### Room Types and Reward Structures

To optimize your runs, you must balance the risk of deeper depths against your current build's strength. Combat rooms test your clear speed, Treasure rooms offer safe recovery and currency, and Rare Monster rooms feature high-threat targets like Gold Slimes or unique Primal Beasts that drop rare Master Trait materials.

| Room Type | Common Enemies & Events | Primary Rewards | Strategy Tip |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Combat Room** | Waves of Horde enemies, Elite Heralds | Weapon Refines, Knickknack Vouchers | Focus on AoE skills to clear waves before the timer expires. |
| **Treasure Room** | Mimics, Chest puzzles, Recovery Shrines | Conflux Tokens, Curios, Sigil Dust | Always check corners for hidden chests before activating the exit gateway. |
| **Rare Monster Room** | Silver Slimes, Rainbow Slimes, Primal Shades | Master Trait Essences, Transcendence Shards | Save your Skybound Arts (SBA) to burst down fleeing targets quickly. |

When selecting power-ups, prioritize offensive multipliers such as Critical Hit Rate and Damage Cap increases in the early depths. In deeper depths (Depth 15 and below), transition to utility power-ups like Cooldown Reduction and Drain to sustain your health. For a deeper dive into optimizing your solo runs, see our [Conflux Farming Guide](/en/conflux/conflux-farming-guide/).

## Mastering Weapon Transcendence and Fatebreaker Quests

Weapon Transcendence allows players to upgrade fully enhanced Level 150 weapons beyond their standard limits. This system is available for all six weapon types: Defender, Ascension, Executioner, Stinger, Stunner, and Terminus. 

Upgrading a weapon through Transcendence unlocks swappable trait slots and unique aesthetic effects, such as weapon glows and custom particle trails. Slot II can be upgraded to a maximum level of 25, while Slot IV can reach a maximum level of 10. Additionally, players unlock Slot EX rewards, which provide passive stat boosts that do not count against standard Sigil caps.

### Transcendence Upgrade Requirements

To transcend your weapons, you must gather materials from Fatebreaker quests—ultra-high-difficulty boss fights featuring Beelzebub, The World, and Ragnalia—as well as deep runs in The Conflux. As outlined in this **Endless Ragnarok complete guide**, Weapon Transcendence requires targeted farming of specific boss essences.

| Weapon Type | Primary Upgrade Focus | Key Material Required | Max Slot Level (II / IV) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Defender** | HP, Defense, Damage Cut | Sentinel Essence (Fatebreaker) | Slot II: Lvl 25 / Slot IV: Lvl 10 |
| **Ascension** | Attack, HP, Healing Cap | Ascension Core (Conflux Depth 20+) | Slot II: Lvl 25 / Slot IV: Lvl 10 |
| **Executioner** | Weak Point DMG, Break Boost | Executioner's Blade (Fatebreaker) | Slot II: Lvl 25 / Slot IV: Lvl 10 |
| **Stinger** | Critical Hit Rate, Crit DMG | Stinger Needle (Fatebreaker) | Slot II: Lvl 25 / Slot IV: Lvl 10 |
| **Stunner** | Stun Power, Link Time Gain | Impact Shard (Conflux Depth 15+) | Slot II: Lvl 25 / Slot IV: Lvl 10 |
| **Terminus** | Catastrophe, Regen, Cap Break | Calamity Orb (Beelzebub Fatebreaker) | Slot II: Lvl 25 / Slot IV: Lvl 10 |

### Step-by-Step Transcendence Strategy

1. **Complete the Prerequisite**: Clear the Proud Quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage" to unlock the Fatebreaker difficulty tier.
2. **Farm Fatebreaker Quests**: Target Beelzebub for Calamity Orbs if upgrading Terminus weapons, or Ragnalia for Sentinel Essences.
3. **Run The Conflux**: Collect Transcendence Shards from Depth 15+ Rare Monster rooms to fund the lower-tier upgrade steps.
4. **Allocate Slot Traits**: Equip Slot II with Utility traits (e.g., Cooldown Reduction or Quick Cooldown) and Slot IV with offensive traits (e.g., Damage Cap) to optimize your build.

## The Summon System and Chaos Difficulty

The Summon System is a major combat overhaul where Lyria summons primal beasts to assist the party. This system is restricted to Chaos-difficulty quests and higher, where enemies can easily one-hit weaker characters.

Each player can equip up to 4 summons in their loadout, but only 1 summon can be fielded at any given time. While most summons provide passive stat bonuses or automatic support attacks, two summons are fully controllable: **Lucilius** and **Rolan**. When summoned, the player temporarily controls these characters, utilizing their unique skill sets and invincibility frames to bypass devastating boss mechanics.

### Primal Burst and Link Time

When all four party members activate their Skybound Arts (SBA) during Link Time while a summon is active, they trigger a **Primal Burst**. This ultimate attack deals massive elemental damage and applies a permanent, non-stackable buff to the party for the remainder of the encounter.

| Primal Beast | Summon Type | Active Ability / Burst Effect | Passive Loadout Bonus |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Lucilius** | Controllable | *Paradise Lost*: Deals massive AoE Light damage and inflicts Slow. | +15% Damage Cap, +10% SBA Gain |
| **Rolan** | Controllable | *Sacred Shield*: Creates a barrier blocking all damage for 10 seconds. | +20% Defense, +15% Auto-Revive Speed |
| **Proto Bahamut** | Auto-Cast | *Skyfall*: Fires a continuous beam dealing Fire/Dark damage. | +15% Critical Hit Rate |
| **Tiamat Omega** | Auto-Cast | *Cyclone Tempest*: Blows back enemies and heals party members. | +20% Skill Cooldown Reduction |

To survive Chaos difficulty, coordination is essential. Players must cycle their summons to ensure that defensive shields (like Rolan's Sacred Shield) are active during the bosses' enraged phases, particularly during the Seofon & Tweyen dual boss fight. ## Character Roster and Master Traits Progression

The expansion increases the total roster to 28 characters, adding 6 new playable DLC characters: Beatrix, Eustace, Fraux, Fediel, Gallanza, and Maglielle. 

Upon reaching Level 100, all 28 characters unlock the **Master Traits** system. This system features three distinct progression paths: **Insight**, **Essence**, and **Crux**. Each path contains 3 unique Rank Perks and 4 Rank Style upgrades, allowing players to specialize their characters into distinct playstyles.

### New DLC Characters Overview

*   **Beatrix**: A high-risk, high-reward DPS character utilizing the *Delta Clock* mechanic to freeze enemy animations and execute precise, timed combos.
*   **Eustace**: A mid-range specialist who uses customized rifle fire and grenades to inflict paralysis and defense down debuffs.
*   **Fraux**: A melee brawler who acts as the Pactbearer to The Devil, summoning fiery fists to execute close-range combos.
*   **Fediel**: A Dark-element specialist from the Six Dragons, capable of generating shields and decaying fields that damage enemies over time.
*   **Gallanza**: A heavy melee tank whose damage scales directly with his maximum HP and current defensive stats.
*   **Maglielle**: A support specialist who utilizes flying weapons to shield allies, heal chip damage, and shred enemy break gauges.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### How do I unlock the Endless Ragnarok DLC content?
To access the expansion content, you must complete the Proud-difficulty quest "The Tale of Bahamut's Rage." Once cleared, a new Epilogue questline will appear in Seedhollow, which unlocks the gateways to The Conflux, Weapon Transcendence, and Chaos-difficulty quests.

### Can I play crossplay with players on other platforms?
Yes. Endless Ragnarok supports full cross-platform play across all available platforms: PlayStation 5, PlayStation 4, Nintendo Switch 2, and PC (Steam). You can invite friends using their in-game Cygame ID regardless of their platform.

### What is the fastest way to farm Weapon Transcendence materials?
The fastest method is running The Conflux mode to gather general Transcendence Shards, and then targeting specific Fatebreaker quests (such as Beelzebub or Ragnalia) for weapon-specific essences. Ensure you run these quests in a full multiplayer lobby to optimize clear times.

### How do controllable summons like Lucilius and Rolan work?
In Chaos-difficulty quests, players can charge their Summon Gauge by performing combos and landing SBAs. Once fully charged, Lyria can summon Lucilius or Rolan. The summoning player temporarily takes direct control of the primal beast, gaining access to a unique, high-damage skillset and temporary invincibility.
