## PC Settings Optimization and Hardware Benchmarks

Achieving a locked 120 FPS on PC requires balancing visual fidelity with GPU load, especially during intensive link time attacks. The expansion's new summon system, which allows players to field controllable entities like Lucilius or Rolan, introduces heavy alpha transparency effects that can tank performance on mid-range graphics cards. 

Executing a proper **Endless Ragnarok PC settings optimization** routine involves adjusting specific high-impact parameters. The game's engine is heavily bound by CPU single-core speed during 4-player online matches, while particle effects and shadow maps scale directly with GPU memory bandwidth. Addressing this **Endless Ragnarok PC settings optimization** allows you to maintain clean visual presentation without sacrificing competitive stability.

| Setting Name | Performance Impact | Recommended (Mid-Range GPU) | Recommended (High-End GPU) | Visual Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Shadow Quality** | High | Medium | Ultra | High (Softens shadow edges) |
| **Particle Effects** | Very High | Standard | High | High (Affects spell & skill clutter) |
| **Volumetric Fog** | Medium | Low | High | Medium (Adds depth to environments) |
| **Ambient Occlusion** | Medium | HBAO | HBAO+ | Low (Improves corner shadowing) |
| **Anti-Aliasing** | Low | TAA / DLSS Quality | DLSS Quality / Native | High (Reduces jagged edges) |
| **Crowd Density** | Low | Medium | High | Low (Affects NPCs in Seedhollow) |

To optimize your PC performance, follow these step-by-step adjustments:
1. Set **Particle Effects** to "Standard". During chaotic fights where [Sandalphon](/en/characters/sandalphon-guide/) spams Supreme Primarch Mode lightstep sword waves, high particle settings can cause sudden frame drops of up to 40%.
2. Adjust **Shadow Quality** to "Medium". This frees up VRAM and stabilizes the frame rate during phase transitions in the fight against The World.
3. Enable **DLSS** or **FSR** in "Quality" mode if you are targeting resolutions above 1440p. This reduces the pixel shading load without introducing noticeable blur.
4. Turn off **Motion Blur** to improve visual clarity when dodging fast boss sweeps.

While PC is considered the **Endless Ragnarok best platform** for maximum fidelity, these adjustments ensure that even mid-range setups (such as an RTX 3060 or RX 6700 XT) can maintain a locked 60 FPS at 1440p during intense raids.

## PlayStation 5 and PlayStation 4 Performance Analysis

The PlayStation ecosystem hosts a significant portion of the player base. This **Endless Ragnarok PS5 performance guide** outlines how to configure your console to ensure maximum responsiveness. The PS5 version offers two distinct graphic profiles: Quality Mode and Performance Mode. 

Quality Mode targets a dynamic 4K resolution at 30 FPS, utilizing high-resolution textures and advanced lighting pathways. While visually striking—particularly in scenic zones like Tredame Sanctum—the 30 FPS cap introduces approximately 50ms of additional input latency. This latency makes reacting to Beelzebub's fast-moving shockwaves significantly more difficult. 

Performance Mode drops the internal rendering resolution to 1440p, upscaling it to your display output while targeting a locked 60 FPS. This mode is highly recommended for all players engaging in endgame content. The frame rate remains stable even when executing a full 4-player Primal Burst, ensuring smooth execution of complex DPS rotations for characters like Beatrix with her Delta Clock mechanic. Using this **Endless Ragnarok frame rate guide** to stabilize your performance on PS5 will directly translate to higher survival rates in Chaos-difficulty quests.

| **Average Frame Rate** | 59.8 FPS | 30.0 FPS | 29.5 FPS | 27.2 FPS |
| **Frame Drops (Primal Burst)** | None | 1-2 frames | 3-5 frames | 5-8 frames |

For players remaining on the legacy PS4 hardware, the expansion remains playable but introduces clear limitations. The base PS4 struggles to maintain 30 FPS during [The Conflux](/en/conflux/the-conflux-complete-guide/) runs, where large waves of elite monsters spawn simultaneously. If you are playing on PS4, disabling screen shake and reducing damage number clutter in the game options menu will help stabilize the frame rate.

## Crossplay and Network Performance Optimization

Endless Ragnarok supports full cross-platform play across PC, PS5, PS4, and Switch 2. This unified matchmaking system ensures a healthy player pool for challenging quests, but it also introduces network considerations. Peer-to-peer synchronization can sometimes cause rubberbanding if a player in your lobby has a high-latency connection.

To optimize your network performance and ensure smooth crossplay sessions:
* **Use a Wired Connection:** Always use a wired Ethernet connection instead of Wi-Fi, especially on PC, PS5, and Switch 2 (using a compatible dock).
* **Matchmaking Region:** Set your matchmaking region to your local area rather than "Any" to minimize ping times during critical boss phases.
* **Host Selection:** If you experience synchronization issues during the summon phase of Lucilius or Rolan, have the host player be the one with the strongest internet connection and hardware specs (ideally PC or PS5).

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Which platform offers the best overall performance for Endless Ragnarok?
PC (Steam) is the best platform for performance, offering uncapped frame rates, native 4K resolution, and support for DLSS 3.5 and FSR 3. This setup provides the lowest input latency and smoothest gameplay during intensive Chaos-difficulty fights.

### Can I transfer my save data between PS5 and PC or Switch 2?
While Endless Ragnarok features full crossplay for multiplayer matchmaking, cross-progression (save transfer) is only supported within the PlayStation ecosystem (PS4 to PS5). Save transfers between PC, PlayStation, and Switch 2 are not natively supported.

### How does the Switch 2 performance hold up during 4-player online raids?
In docked mode, the Switch 2 maintains a stable 55-60 FPS during normal combat. During highly intensive moments, such as 4-player Primal Bursts or during phase shifts against Beelzebub, the frame rate may briefly dip into the low 50s, but it remains highly playable.

### What PC graphics setting has the highest impact on frame rate?
Particle Effects and Shadow Quality have the highest impact. Lowering Particle Effects to "Standard" can significantly reduce frame drops during spell-heavy encounters, particularly when characters like Fediel or Cagliostro fill the screen with visual effects.
